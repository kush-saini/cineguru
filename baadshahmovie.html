Baadshah-1999


 7.2 / 10

Directed by Abbas-Mustan


Action Comedy


Average Box-Office


Shahrukh and Twinkle's 1st film together

Film ki कहानी


Raj apni maa ke saath rehta hai aur Badshah Detective agency chalaata hai aur maa ki aankhon ke operation ke liye paise jama krne ki koshish mai laga hai. K Jhunjunwala apni beti ki shaadi Nitin se karwana chahta aur madad keliye Raj ke paas aata hai. Raj Seema se pyaar ka naatak karke uske dil tod deta hai aur Jhunjunwala ka kaam poora kardeta hai. Raj ko Mahender Seth ki beti ko Goa jaakar gundo se bachaane ka naya kaam milta hai aur Raj apne dosto ke saath Goa ke flight pakadne jaa raha hai . Thapar ek businessman hai jo Goa ki Chief Minister Gayatri Bachchan ko marwana chahta hai aur ek baar iss kaam mai naa-kaamyab ho chuka hai, iss baar vo Rani aur Moti ko yeh kaam saunpta hai.Raj ka ticket Badshah ke naam se book karaya gaya hai. Rani aur Moti asli CBI officer Badshah urf Deepak Malhotra ko maardete hai jo Seema ka bhai bhi tha. CBI Raj ko Baadshah samajhke Gayatri Bachchan ko bachane ka mission dedeti hai. Thapar ke aadmi Raj ke dosto ko bandhi bana letey hai aur unhe aazad karne ke badle mai Raj se Gayatri Bachchan ko maarne ko kehte hai. Raj Mahender seth ki bachchi ko bacha leta hai aur Seema ki madad se Gayatri Bachchan ko saavdhan kardeta hai, Gayatri Bachchan bulletproof jacket ki vajah se bach jaati hai. Bachchi ke shareer se laga bomb Raj Thapar ki gaadi ke neeche fek deta hai aur Thapar bhaagne ki koshish mai apne lagaye hue bomb se hi maara-jaata hai. Ab Raj ki Detective Agency zoro-shoro se chal rahi hai.

Honest Overview



Shahrukh comedy role mai khoob jum rahe hai. Film mai Romantic gaaney , funny dialogues aur dhasoo action hai. Family ke saath popcorn khaate hue iss movie ka aanand uthaaey. Entertaining shuruaat se lekar ending tak !!
