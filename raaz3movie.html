Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Mashoor Abhinetri(Actress) Shanaya ki. Kaamyabi Shanaya ke kadam choomti hai par wo nayi actress Sanjana se jalti hai. Issi jalan ke chaltey Shanaya apne premi Director Aditya ki madad se Sanjana ko Bhootiya paani pila deti hai jissey Sanjana ko Bhoot dikhne lagta hai. Sanjana ek Party mai apni saari izzat aur Naam kho baithti hai aur ab Aspataal mai bharti hai. Aditya Sanjana se pyaar karne laga hai aur Hospital mai ussey Shanaya aur Bhoot se bacha leta hai. Anth mai Shanaya khud ko khatam karleti hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Aditya ) , Bipasha Basu( Shanaya ) , Esha Gupta( Sanjana ) , Manish Choudhary( Bhoot )
