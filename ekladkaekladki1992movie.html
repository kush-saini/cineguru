Directed by Vijay Sadanah


Comedy Romance


Below Average Box Office


Family movie

Film ki कहानी


Bhagwati Prasad ki ameer bhatiji Renu ussey milne aayi hai. Bhagwati Renu ke paisey churaake ussey boat mai dubaaney ki koshish krta hai par Renu ko machuarey bacha letey hai. Renu ki yaad-daasht jaa chuki hai aur vo police thaaney mai hai. Raja naam ka ladka ussey apni biwi bataake police se chchuda laata hai. Raja teen bachcho ke saath rehta hai aur gareebi ke baavjood khush hai. Renu ko Raja ki saadgi se pyaar hojaata hai. Bhagwati Renu ko maarne ke liye gundey bhejtaa hai par Police sahi waqt par aake unhe bacha-letey hai aur Bhagwati ko jail mai daal detey hai. Renu ki yaad-daasht wapas aa chuki hai aur wo phir se America jaane waali hai par Raja aur bachcho ka pyaar ussey rok leta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Raja) , Neelam Kothari( Rani ) , Asrani( Markutey ) , Tiku Talsania( Police Inspector ) , Anupam Kher( Bhagwati Prasad )

Honest Overview



Salman aur Neelam ki on-screen jodi khoob jum rahi hai. Salman ke fans iss movie ko dekh sakte hai. OVERALL DECENT MOVIE AND ONE TIME WATCH !!!
