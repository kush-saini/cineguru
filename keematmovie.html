Keemat – They Are Back-1998


 4.8 / 10

Directed by Sameer Malkan


Action Drama


Box Office Success


Very Humane Story

Film ki कहानी


Dev aur Ajay chori karne mai Phd hai aur ussi se apni rozi-roti kamaatey hai. Ajay ki ek chori ki wajah se Mohan naam ke ladke ki jaan chali jaati hai. Marne se pehle Mohan unhe apne gao aur parivar ke baare mai batata hai. Dev aur Ajay Mohan ke gao jaake uske pariwar mai sabse ghul-mil jaate hai par kisi ko Mohan ki maut sach nahi batatey. Mohan ki biwi pait se hai aur hospital na hone ki wajah se marjaati hai. Dev aur Ajay uss din Mohan k maut ka sach Mohan ke pitah Dinanath ko purey gao ke saamne bata-detey hai. Dinanath unhe ghar se nikaal-deta hai itna bada vishwas-ghaat karne keliye. Dev aur Ajay gundey Bhanu Pratap ko maut ke ghaath utaarke gao mai hospital banane mai kaamyab hojaatey hai aur Dinanath ka bharosa aur pyaar phirse jeet letey hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Film mai Financial EDucation ka ek bahut hi funny Lesson sikhaaya gaya hai.
