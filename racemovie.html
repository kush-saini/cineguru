Film ki कहानी


Ranvir ek kaamyab Business Man hai aur DHokebaazo se sakht nafrat karta hai. Ranvir apne Bhai Rajiv ki Sharaab ki lat chudhwana chaahta hai. Rajiv Ranvir ko maarke Insurance ke 100 Million dollars kamaane ka plan banaata hai aur Sonia ki madad se kaamyab hojaata hai. Kuch din baad Ranvir phir lauta hai , Rajiv ye dekh-kar hairaan hai. Ranvir Rajiv ko sab kuch samjhaata hai aur Insurance ke saare paise jeetne ka Mauka Rajiv ko deta hai Ek Aakhri Race ke zariye - Jo jeeta Saare $ uske. Uss Khatarnaak Race mai Rajiv maara jaata hai aur Ranvir baal baal bachta hai. Ranvir ab ek nayi Race ki talaash mai hai...
