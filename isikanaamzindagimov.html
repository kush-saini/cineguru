Film ki कहानी


Zamindar ne apni zameen gareeb Bansiram ko daaan mai di thi. Zamindar ki tabehat naazuk hai aur kuch hi din baad maut ussey apne saath lejaati hai parlok mai. Zamindar ka beta Devraj apne pitah ki zameen Bansiram se chudaaney ki hazaaro nakaam koshishey kartey kartey dum tod deta hai. Devraj ka betah bhi ussi zameen ke chakkar lagata lagata ussi zameen pe dum tod-deta hai. Devraj apne bete ke saath aatma ke roop mai zameen ke paas wale ped par baitha hua ussey dhaant raha hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Chotu ) , Farah Naaz( Chumki ) , Shakti Kapoor( Devraj ) , Rajesh Puri( Jyotish ) , Pran( Bansiram ) , Beena Banerjee( Kamla ) , Anjan Shrivastav( Doctor ) , Asrani( Accountant )

Honest Review



Film ka Sabse Intersting hissa hai Budhey Baba ka kirdaar jispe aapko taras aa jaega. FIlm ki Ending mai Shakti Kapoor ji ka Costume dekhne ke baad Hasee-Thahakey Guaranteed hai.
