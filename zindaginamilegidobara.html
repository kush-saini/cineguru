Film ki कहानी


Arjun Kabir aur Imran kahi saal baad ek dusre se kahi saal baad mil rahe hai ek Trip keliye. Kabir ki shaadi ho rahi hai Natasha se. Teeno Trip pe jaa chukey hai aur dher saare Mazedaar Romanchak anubhavo ka maza le rahey hai. Uss Trip pe teeno ko apni zindagi ke sabse Mushkil Sawalo ke jawaab mil jaatey hai jissey unki zindagi mai ab Ujaala hi Ujaala hai aur unki Dosti pehle se bhi zyada pakki ho chuki hai...

Honest Overview



Ye film Dosti aur Relationships pe bane kisi "UDEMY COurse ki tarah" hai. It is a Sacred Rule to play this Album on Every Road-Trip with your Friends, Yaad Rakhiye. Film mai Insaan ke Meta-Morphosis(Vikaas) ko khoobsurati se dikhaaya gaya hai aur "JUST DO IT" Mentality ko apnaane ki Subtle Advice bhi di gayi hai. This is not a film but a "VIBE" jo aapko Definitely Pasand aaegi. VERDICT -" A Mixed Bag of Emotions with Impeccable Story-Telling". 1000% RECOMMENDED to Every 1st year College Student !!!
