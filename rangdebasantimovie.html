Film ki कहानी


Sue Mckinley India aayi hai Swatantra Senaniyo pe ek film banane keliye. Sue ko apne actors mil jaatey hai 5 dost - DJ , Sonia, Aslam ,Sukhi ,Karan aur Ek Neta Laxman mai. FIlm ki Shooting chalu hojaati hai aur sab hasee-mazaak kartey huye apne Kirdaaro pe mehnat kar-rahey hai. Sonia ka mangetar aur Group ka member Ajay Singh Rathod ek Pilot hai aur faulty MIG-jet ki wajah se maara jaata hai. Ajay ki maut ne Paancho dosto ko Vidroh ki bhavna se bhardiya hai. Iss Vidhroh ka shikaar banta hai Ajay ki maut ka gunhegar Defense Minister jo unko haatho maara jaata hai. DJ Sukhi Karan Aslam aur Laxman Police ke haatho maare jaatey hai aur marne se pehle Apne-Apne Kirdaar ko hakeekat mai jee-jaatey hai...
