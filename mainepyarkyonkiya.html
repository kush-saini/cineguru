Maine Pyaar Kyun Kiya?-2005


 6.8 / 10

Directed by David Dhawan


Comedy Drama


Huge Box Office Sucess


You will love the characters

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Samir ki jo kaamyaab haddiyon ka doctor par ladkiyan uske clinic apni tanhaayi ka ilaaj karaane chali aati hai uspe looks pe lattu hokey. Samir ke clinic ki nurse hai Naina jo uske Clinic ko manage karti hai. Samir Sonia se pyar karta hai, Sonia hai toh bahut sweet par hai bahut badi dramebaaz. Sonia Samir se shaadi ki baat karti hai par Samir Biwi ka bahana bana deta hai ko ki hai hi nahi. Badi mushkil se Naina iss naatak ka hissa banne keliye maan jaati hai. Sonia se aur Samir ki maa se jhooth bolte bolte Naina thak chuki hai aur sheher chchod-kar jaa rahi hai. Samir ko ehsaas hota hai ki vo sach mei Naina se pyar karta hai. Samir Naina ko rokne airport pahuch jaata hai aur ussey apne dil ka haal bataata hai aur Naina haa kehedeti hai, Naina aur Samir ab asli mai shaadi karne wale hai. Dono behad khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Samir ) , Sushmita Sen( Naina ) , Katrina Kaif( Sonia ) , Sohail Khan( Pyare ) , Beena Kak( Maa ) , Arshad Warsi( Vicky ) , Isha Koppikar( Nishi ) , Rajpal Yadav( Thapa ) , Dolly Bindra( Dolly ) , Arbaz Khan( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Ye film hume sikhaati hai Dil ke maamlo mai jhuth nahi Sach hi kaam aata hai. Pyaare ka kirdaar amazingly cute aur funny hai. Iss film mai Hasee-THahaake guaranteed hai. Apne partner ke saath iss film ko dekhein. Salman ko comedy roles mai dekhna kisi treat se kam nahi. Extremely Entertaining film and a MUST WATCH!!!
