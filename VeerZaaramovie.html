Veer Zaara-2004


 8.5 / 10

Directed by Yash Chopra


Romance Drama


BlockBuster


Shot in India and Pakistan

Film ki कहानी


Saamiya ek pakistani vakeel hai jisey mashoor qaidi 786 ka case milta hai jo pichle 22 saal se kuch nahi bola hai. Qaidi 786 Saamiya ko apna naam Veer batata hai aur apni kahaani sunana chalu krta hai. Zaara apni daadi ki asthiya bahaane Bharat aati hai par ek bus accident mai phas jaati hai, Indian AirForce pilot Veer aake uski jaan bachata hai , dono mai dosti hoti hai aur Veer Zaara ko ek din mai apna gao dikhaata hai. Zaara Lahore ki train pakadne wali hai aur Veer apne dil ki baat Zaara ko bataana chaahta hai par uski mulaqat Zaara ke mangeytar Raza se hoti hai jo Zaara ko wapas le jaane aaya hai. Zaara ko pakistan pahuchke apne pyar ka ehsaas hota hai , Shabbo Veer ko phone pe Zaara ke dil ka haal bataate hue uski Zaara ko lejaane ko kehti hai. Sab chchod-chaad ke Veer Pakistan pahuchta hai. Zaara sabke saamne Veer ko galey laga leti hai. Zaara ke abbu iss rishtey se khush nahi aur Veer ko wapas lautne ko kehte hai. Veer wapas jaa raha hai lekin Raza apne apmaan ka badla lene keliye Veer ko jaasoos batakar jail ki salaakhon ke peeche pahucha deta hai. Saamiya Qaidi 786 ko insaaf dilaane mai kaamyab hojaati hai. Veer aur Zaara ek dusre ko saalo baad dekhte hai , Samay maano tham saa gaya hai . Veer aur Zaara Veer ke gao mai khushi-khushi zindagi bitaatey hai.
