Film ki कहानी


Ricky ek photographer hai aur apne Ladkibaaz mijaaz keliye mashoor aur Badnaam dono hai. Uski mulaakat hoti hai apni favourite Model Sheena se par Sheena ussey nafrat karti hai kyonki Ricky ne uski dost Nisha ka dil dukhaya tha. Kuch din baad Ricky ko ek ajnabee Rajveer 30 lakh rupay deta hai apni biwi Model Sheena ko pyaar mai fasaane keliye. Ricky Sheena ko fasaane ke chakkar mai khud fas jaata hai aur dono pyaar karne lagtey hai. Nashe mai Sheena Ricky ka khoon kardeti hai aur aagey jaan-ne keliye film dekhe...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Ricky ) , Udita Goswami( Sheena ) , Dino Morea( Rajveer ) , Tara Sharma( Nisha ) , Rajat Bedi( Steve ) , Himesh Reshamiya( Cameo ) , Suresh Menon( Benz )

Honest Overview



Chummiyo ke Devta Emraan ji aur har 90's kid ka Crush Udita Goswami ji ki "Bijli" Chemistry aapko pasand aaegi. Film aapko ek Sawaal(Question) poochne par majboor karti hai - "Arre kehna kya chaahte ho ?". FIlm ka Plot kisi makdi ke jaal ki tarah COMPLEX hai. VERDICT - "Watch this film for DAZZLING chehre and Groovy gaaney". A ONE time Watch and 1000% RECOMMENDED to EMRAAN-UDITA fans !!!
