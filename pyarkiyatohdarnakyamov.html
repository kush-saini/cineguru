Pyaar Kiya Toh Darna Kya-1998


 6.6 / 10

Directed by Sohail Khan


Comedy Romance


Super Hit


Salman & Kajol's 1st movie together

Film ki कहानी


Muskaan gaon mai apne bhai Vishal aur Chacha ke saath rehti hai. Muskaan dur shehar mai College padhne keliye jaati hai par Vishal apni behen ki kuch zyada hi chinta karta hai. Muskaan ke dil mai Suraj naam ke ladke keliye ek khaas jagah ban jaati hai. Ek din Vishal college aake Suraj aur uske dosto ko kisi galatfaimi ke chalte khoob peet deta hai aur Muskan ko wapas gao lejakar uski shaadi Thakur Vijay ke karwana chahta hai par Vijay ke iraado ki kisi ko bhanak nahi hai. Suraj Muskan ke pariwar ka dil jeetne uske gao chala jaata hai. Suraj ki sauteli maa aur baap waha aa pahuchte hai. Suraj ki maa Muskaan ko bhura-bhalaa kehdeti hai, yeh sunke Muskaan roti hui chali jaati hai aur raste se usey Vijay Singh agwa karleta hai. Muskaan ko bachane ke liye Vishal Suraj aur Chachu ek hojaate hai Muskaan ko chudaake Vijay aur uske gundo ki khoob dhulaayi karte hai. Suraj ke maa-baap Muskaan ko apna-letey hai. Sab khushi-khushi apni zindagi jeetey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Suraj ) , Dharmendra( Ajay Singh ) , Kajol( Muskan ) , Arbaaz Khan( Vishal ) , Kiran Kumar( Suraj's papa ) , Aasif Sheikh( Chote Thakur ) , Tiku Talsania( Principal ) , Nirmal Pandey( Thakur Vijay )

Honest Overview



Oh Oh Jaane Jaana aur Oodhli Chunariya , ye dono gaane iss film ki jaan hai. Dharam paaji ke dialogues film mai chaar chand lagate hai. Arbaaz Khan ki acting tareef ke kaabil hai. Sohail Khan comedy bahut achchi likhte hai. Overall Kaafi Entertaining movie. Kisi Sunday ko haath mai popcorn liye aaram se dekhiye film. Great Entertainer . Highly Recommended !!!
