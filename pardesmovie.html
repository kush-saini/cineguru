Pardes-1997


 8.3 / 10

Directed by Subhash Ghai


Romance Drama


Super Hit


Shot in USA

Film ki कहानी


Kishorilal ek Nri businessman hai aur apne bete Rajiv ki shaadi ek bhartiya ladki se karwana chahta hai. Kishorilal India pahuchkar apne dost ki beti Ganga se Rajiv ka rishta jodna chahta hai. Rajiv India pahuchkar Ganga ko pasand karleta hai aur dono sagai karke vapas America chale jaate hai. Ganga ko America raas nahi aa raha hai aur Kishorilal ke gharwaale uski izzat nahi kar rahe hai sivaaey Arjun ke. Arjun Kishorilal ka sautela beta hai. Ganga aur Arjun ki badhti nazdeekiya dekh-kar Kishorilal Arjunko dur bhejdeta hai aur Ganga ko Rajiv ke saath Las Vegas ki trip par bhejdeta hai. Rajiv Ganga ka faeida uthaane ki koshish karta hai par Ganga bachke bhag jaati hai aur Arjun ki madad se India aa jaati hai. Arjun Ganga ko mann hi mann chaahne lagta hai. Sabko ye lag raha hai ki Arjun Ganga ko bhaga kar le gya hai. Kishorilal India pahuchta hai, Ganga usey Rajiv ki badtameezi ke baare mai bataati hai. Kishorilal Rajiv ko kas ke tamaacha lagata hai aur Ganga ka haath Rajiv ke haath mai dekar unki shaadi krwadeta hai. Ganga aur Arjun America mai khushi-khushi apni zindagi bitaate hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Arjun ) , Mahima Chaudhary( Ganga ) , Amrish Puri( Kishorilal ) , Apurva Agnihotri( Rajiv ) , Alok Nath( Ganga's father ) , Dina Pathak( Ganga's mother ) , Himani Shivpuri( Kulwanti )

Honest Review



Ye film hume batati hai ki " Pyaar raasta dhoondh hi leta hai ". Mahima Chaudhary bahut sundar lag rahi hai. Shahrukh Khan aur Amrish Puri ne laajawab acting ki hai. Film ke gaano ki lat lag sakti hai, itne achche hai gaaney matlab . Romantic Masala entertainer . Must Watch !!
