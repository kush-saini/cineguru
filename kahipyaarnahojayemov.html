Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Prem ki jo ek singer hai aur shaadiyon mai gaata hai. Prem ke saath bahut bura hua hai ,uski mangetar Nisha shaadi ke din bhag gayi. Iss baat se Prem depressed hai aur sharaab ke nashe mai dooba rehna chaahta hai jissey uske pariwar wale pareshan hai. Prem ki ek dost hai Priya jo uske saath kaam karti hai, dono achche dost hai. Priya apne ghar ki pareshaniya mitaane ke liye ek NRI ladke Rahul se shaadi karne ka mann bana leti hai. Rahul ye samajh jaata hai aur Priya se shaadi ko inkaar kar-deta hai. Kismat ek flight mai Nisha Rahul Prem aur Priya ko saath le aati hai. Prem-Priya ko ek dusre ke sachche pyaar ka ehsaas hota hai aur Rahul-Nisha saath-saath hojaate hai...
