Film ki कहानी


Karan College mai aur Doston mai apna Samay barbaad karta rehta hai jissey uske Maata-Pitaah Chintit rehtey hai. Karan josh mai aakar Indian Miltary Academy mai daakhila leleta hai par tangh hokar kuch hafto baad hi waapis aajaata hai. Karan ko apni zimmedaari ka ehsaas hota hai aur woh phir se Army laut jaata hai. Karan ab Captain Karan Shergill ban chuka hai. Karan apni kaabil team ke saath milke Pakistani sipahiyo ka khaatma krke apne Mission ko poora kardeta hai. Karan apne pitah ki aankhon mai apne liye garv dekh paa raha hai aur behad khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Hrithik Roshan( Karan ) , Preity Zinta( Romila ) , Boman Irani( Sanjeev ) , Amitabh Bachchan( Sunil ) , Sushant Singh( Jalal ) , Parmeet Sethi( Shahbaaz ) , Amrish Puri( Gautam )

Honest Overview



Woh chandh Shabdh jo har Beta apne Pitah se sunna chaahta hai - "I am Proud of You" - film issi Sentiment ke irdh-girdh ghumtey huey ek Laaprarwah Naalayak ladke ka Chaukanne Sipaahi mai Evolution dikhaati hai. Jab kabhi apne Pitah se daant khaa kar aaye toh iss film ko zaroor dekhein - "MOTIVATION ki Entry hogi aur Gussey/Naraazgi ka Exit". AMitabh ji apni ICONIC awaaz se aapke andar PATRIOTISM ki Tsunami le aaengey. TEARS & GooseBumps Guaranteed. VEIN-Popping Thrill with Chutki bhar Romance. A MUST Watch for Every PROUD INDIAN !!!
