Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna-2006


 7.3 / 10

Directed by Karan Johar


Drama Relations Love


Box-Office Success


Fresh Perspective on Love

Film ki कहानी


Maya Rishi se shaadi kar rahi hai. Shaadi se pehle Maya ki mulakaat ek ajnabi Dev se hoti jo usey pyar aur rishto ke baare mai sujhav de kar chala jaata hai. Usi din Dev ka accident hojaata hai jiski wajah se Dev ka football career khatam hojaata hai. Dev ab ek football coach hai aur uski biwi Rhea usey naakamyabi ka taana maarti hai. Rhea Rishi se kabhi pyaar nahi kar paati . Kismat Rhea aur Dev ko phir se milwadeti hai. Dono ki zindagi mai ek hi dukh hai, Ye dukh unhe kareeb leaata hai aur pyaar unki zindagi mai aasha ki ek kiran banke aaya hai. Rishi ke pitah Sam aur Maya ki maa Kamal unke iss naajaiz rishtey ko jaan jaatey hai. Sam duniya chchodne se pehle Maya se Rishi ko chchodne ko kehta hai. Kahi saal beet chuke hai Maya aur Dev apne puraaney rishto ko chchod chuke hai aur akele zindagi bitah rahe hai. Rhea Dev ko rokne keliye railway station pahuchti hai aur dono premi ek dusre ko gale laga lete hai. Dev aur Maya ek nayi pyaar bhari zindagi ki shuruat karte hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Amitabh Bachchan( Sam ) , Shahrukh Khan( Dev ) , Abhishek Bachchan( Rishi ) , Rani Mukherji( Maya ) , Preity Zinta( Rhea ) , Kirron Kher( Kamal )

Honest Overview



" Pyaar mai kuch bhi sahi-galat nahi hota ". Iss film mai naajaiz rishto ke baarey mai baat ki gayi hai. Sabhi actors ne behetreen kaam kiya hai. Film ki locations behad khoobsurat hai. Agar aapko foreign locations pasand hai toh ye movie zaroor dekhiye. " Film Superhit emotional gaano ke saath packaged ek Visual Treat hai ". Totally Recommended for Shahrukh fans !!!
