Shaadi Karke Phas Gaya Yaar-2006


 4.9 / 10

Directed by K.S. Adhiyaman


Romance Marriage


Flop at Box Office


Impactful Story

Film ki कहानी


Ayaan ek Businessman hai aur Ahana ek fashion model. Pehli mulaakat ke baad Ayaan ke sapno mai Ahaana ke hi khayal hai. Ahaana ko Ayaan ka mazakiya andaaz aur bhalai pasand aati hai. Ayaan aur Ahaana iss pyaar ko shaadi ka naam dedetey hai. Ahaana ko miscaariage hojaata hai jissey Ayaan bohot dukhi hai aur Ahaana se uski shaadi tootne ki kagaar par hai. Ahaana fir se maa ban chuki hai, Ayaan ka apne bachche ke saath pyaar dekh-kar wo fir se apne pati se pyaar karne lagti hai aur ek Pariwar bhikarne se bachch jaata hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Ayaan ) , Shilpa Shetty( Ahana ) , Reema Lagoo( Ayaan's father ) , Mohnish Behl( Karan ) , Shakti Kapoor( Mr Kapoor ) , Aasif Sheikh( Bunty )

Honest Review



Movie bikul aisi hai jaise koi Vada Pav ho bina hari chatni ke. Aasif Sheikh aur Shakti Kapoor ki acting film ko BORING se MILDLY AMUSING banaati hai. Time Pass karne keliye film achchi hai. DEFINITELY A ONE TIME WATCH BECAUSE OF THE STORY !!!
