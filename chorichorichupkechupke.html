Chori Chori Chupke Chupke-2001


 6.9 / 10

Directed by Abbas Mustan


Love Drama


Decent Hit


Story was ahead of its time

Film ki कहानी


Raj aur Priya ki zindagi mai sab kuch hai rutba, paisa aur izzat sirf ek cheez ko hi dono tarastey hai - Ek bachche ki kilkaari sunney ko. Raj apne bachche keliye ek maa dhoondhne Switzerland se India aaya hai. Ek Dance-Bar mai pohoch kar uski nazar ek aisi ladki(Madhubala) par padti hai jo tan ka dhandha karti hai par dil ki achchai chupaye nahi chchupti . Raj Madhu ko apne bachche ko janam dene ke badle paise dene ka sauda karta hai. Madhu Raj ke saath wapas Switzerland chali jaati hai aur Priya se milti hai. Priya ko Madhu bohot pasand aati hai. Kuch mahine beet chuke hai, Madhu ab pait se hai. Madhu Raj ke pyaar mai thodi behek jaati hai aur apna bachcha dene se inkaar kardeti hai par Madhu apni zindagi ka sabse bada tyaag karke bachcha Priya ko saunp deti hai. Maa banne ka ehsaas ussey badal-deta hai. Wapas jaate huey wo Raj se milti hai aur wapas uss andhere jagat mai wapas kabhi na lautne ka wada karti hai. Kisi ka pariwar poora hogya aur Ek Pyaar adhoora reh gaya par jo bhi hota kehte hai na ke achche keliye hi hota hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Raj ) , Rani Mukherji( Priya ) , Preity Zinta( Madhubala ) , Farida Jalal( Asha ) , Dalip Tahil( Ranjit ) , Johnny Lever( Pappu ) , Amrish Puri( Sr. Inspector ) , Prem Chopra( Sr. Inspector )

Honest Overview



Ek Aurat ka tyaag kaise ussey nark se nikaalkar swarg mai pahucha deta hai iss film mai dikhaya gaya, Film dekhne par samaj aa jaega iss sab ka matlab. Dialogues , ScreenPlay aur gaano ke lyrics incredible hai. Preity Zinta iss film ki jaan hai. Salman ka "Nice-guy" avatar aapko kaafi pasand aaega. 1000% Strongly recommended for Sabke liye !!!
