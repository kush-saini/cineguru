Don-2006


 8.8 / 10

Directed by Farhan Akhtar


Crime Action Thriller


Box-Office Hit


Shahrukh's most charismatic avatar

Film ki कहानी


DON Asia Drug market ka king hai. Don Ramesh ko jaan se maardeta hai. Ramesh ki biwi Kamini Don se badla lena chaahti hai aur Don ko pakadne mai police ki madad krne ko taiyaar ho jaati hai. Don Kamini ko jaan se maarke police ke saamne se bachchke nikal jaata hai. Roma apne bhai Ramesh aur bhabhi Kamini ki maut ka badla lene keliye chalaki se Don ke gang ka hissa banjaati hai. Don ek din police se bhaagta hua buri tarah se zakhmi hojaata hai. ACP De Silva Don ke behroopiye Vijay ko Don jaisa banakar ek zaroori disc lene wapas bhejta hai. Roma Vijay ko Don samajhkar maarne hi wali hoti hai ki De Silva usey sab sach batadeta hai. Ek accident mai De Silva maara jaata hai. Vijay apni begoonahi saabit krne keliye vo disc lene mai kaamyab hojaata hai aur disc police ko saunp deta hai. Vijay zakhmi hai aur hospital van mai Roma se dur jaatey huye kuch aisa bolta hai jo DON bola karta tha. Roma samajh jaati hai ki DON abhi bhi zinda hai par ab bahut der ho chuki hai aur DON faraar hai. DON apni girlfriend Anita ko batata hai kis tarah usne Vijay ko maarke uski jagah li aur Police ko khaali disc dekar bevkoof banaya. DON fir se aazad hai aur has raha hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Don/Vijay ) , Priyanka Chopra Jonas( Roma ) , Kareena Kapoor Khan( Kamini ) , Isha Koppikar( Anita ) , Arjun Rampal( Rhea ) , Boman Irani( Vardhan/ DCP De Silva ) , Om Puri( Malik ) , Pavan Malhotra( Narang ) , Rajesh Khattar( Singhania )

Honest Overview



Khoonkhaar aur shaatir Don ka kirdaar Shahrukh ne bakhoobi nibhaya hai . " Entertaining gaaney , Suspense aur Dhamakedaar action ". Shahrukh Priyanka aur Boman ne laajawab abhinay kia hai. One of the best action thriller movies made in Bollywood . Negative character mai Shahrukh kaafi natural flawless aur charming lag rahe hai. Agar action movies pasand hai toh ye movie definitely dekho !!!
