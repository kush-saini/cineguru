Film ki कहानी


Sanju Pandit aur Idris ke saath milke ek Bank ko loot leta hai, Teen mahiney baad Sanju ki yaad-daasht chali gayi jiski wajah se Pandit aur Idris ko apna Hissa nahi mil paa raha. Sanju Lootey huey paise usney kaha chupaaey thay ye dhoondhney ki koshish mai dar-dar bhatak raha hai. Iss sab ke beech ussey apni patni ke baare mai kuch anokha pata chalta hai. Sanju ko apni biwi pe shak hai. Sab train mai hai jaha Pandit aur Idris ko uss aadmi ne maardiya jo Sanju ko dhamka raha hai. Sanju aur uski biwi bhi adhmari haalat mai. Ab aagey jaaney keliye film dekhein...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Sanju ) , Vidya Balan( Neetu ) , Rajesh Sharma( Pandit ) , Namit Das( Idris )

Honest Overview



Film mai dikhaya gaya MAA-Betey-Bahu ka ye ADHBHUT Love Triangle ghar Ghar ki Kahaani hai jo har Couple ki Dukhti Rag pe Haath Rakhta hai. WORLD ki har Wife iss film ko dekh-kar Chaahegi unke Husband ko bhi Sanju jaisi Bimaari ho. VERDICT - " Dekhne mai Entertaining aur UnPredictable bilkul Ranveer SIngh ji ke Fashion Sense ki tarah". After watching the film aap kahengey-" Pata nahi kya hua par Mazaa bahut aaya" . 1000% STRONGLY RECOMMENDED to those jo Shaadi ka ladoo kha chukey hai !!!
