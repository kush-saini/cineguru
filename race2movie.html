Race 2-2013


 5.9 / 10

Directed by Abbas Mustan


Action Thriller


SuperHit


Story of Revenge with Twists

Film ki कहानी


Ranveer ne abhi apni biwi Sonia ko khoya hai. Ranveer Sonia ke khooni Vikram ka pata laga leta hai. Ranveer Nakli Note ka sauda karke Vikram ke Saare Dhandhe bandh karwa deta hai. Ranveer Italy ke sheher Turin ka mashoor Shroud churaake UnderWorld Don Armaan Malik ko bechne ka plan banata hai. Shroud ki deal poori ho chuki hai aur Armaan chalaaki se Ranveer ko maarkar apne Private Plane mai udd raha hai ki tabhi Ranveer uske saamne aa jaata hai. Ranveer kabhi maraa tha hi nahi kyonki woh pehle hi Armaan ki saazish ko jaan chuka tha. Dono mai Bhayanak ladai hoti hai. Dono plane se girke bachch jaatey hai. Armaan ko Godfather Anza ko Shroud bechtey waqt pata chalta hai ki Shroud nakli hai. Armaan apna sab kuch Anza ke haathon gawa chuka hai aur Ranveer ki talaash mai hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Ranveer ) , John Abraham( Armaan ) , Anil Kapoor( Robert ) , Rajesh Khattar( Vikram ) , Deepika Padukone( Aleena ) , Jacqueline Fernandez( Omisha ) , Aditya Pancholi( Anza ) , Bipasha Basu( Sonia )

Honest Overview



Ye film uss Recipe ke jaisi jisme Garnishing kamaal ki hai par Swaad OKAY-OKAY hai. Vazandaar Dialogues and Dil mai Dhak-Dhak karde aisa MIND-BLOWING Action. Film mai Vada-Pav jaisa REFRESHING Swaad hai aur ITALIAN Cuisine ke GOOD LOOKS hai. ACTING takes a Backseat in this "Race". KATAhi zeher POPCORN Worthy film. 1000% RECOMMENDED to SAIF & JOHN fans !!!
