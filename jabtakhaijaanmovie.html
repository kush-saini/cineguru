Film ki कहानी


Samar Indian Army mai Major hai . Samar ne abhi Akira ko doobne se bachaya hai, Akira Samar ki diary mai uski kahaani padhti hai. Kahaani - Samar London mai choti-moti naukriya karta hai aur gaana gaake paise kamaata hai , Meera Samar se gaana seekhti hai . Dheere-dheere dono mai pyaar hojaata hai. Ek din Samar ka accident hojaata hai. Meera apne bhagwan se Samar ko bachane ke badle Samar se kabhi na milne ki kasam khaati hai. Samar bach jaata hai aur India wapas aake Army join karleta hai. Akira Samar aur Meera ko fir se milwa deti hai , Meera apni kasam tod-deti hai aur ab Samar ke saath hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Samar ) , Anushka Sharma( Akira ) , Katrina Kaif( Meera ) , Sharib Hashmi( Zain ) , Anupam Kher( Meera's father ) , Rishi Kapoor( Cameo ) , Neetu Singh( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Ladakh aur London ki locations behad khoobsurat lag rahi hai. Samar ka zinda-dil kirdaar aapka dil jeet lega. Film ke gaaney film mai chaar-chaand laga dete hai. MUST WATCH FOR SHAHRUKH FANS !!!
