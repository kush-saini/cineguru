Film ki कहानी


Ishar Singh 1897 ki British Indian Army mai ek havaldaar hai. Ek Gustaakhi ki sazah mai Ishar Singh ko Saragarhi ke kiley mai bhejdiya gaya hai. Ishar Saragarhi pahuchke 21 Sikh regiment ke siphaiyo ko Anushaasan ka paath padhaata hai. Dheere-Dheere Ishar ki dosti sabhi sipahiyo se hojaati hai. Afghan Saragarhi ke kiley pe hamla karne wale hai. Ishar ek aakhri ladaai ke liye tayyar hai apne 20 saathiyo ke saath. 21 Sikho mai aur Dus Hazaar Afhano mai ladaai chchidh chuki hai. Afghan jung jeet-jaatey hai par Ishar apni aakhri saans tak Apni kaum aur Har shaheed sipahi keliye ladhta hai. Ye kahaani Bhartiya itihaas ki kitaab mai ek Mehetvapoorna panna ban chuki hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Ishar ) , Parineeti Chopra( Jeevani ) , Mir Sarwar( Khan Masud ) , Ashwath Bhatt( Gul Badshah ) , Rakesh Chaturvedi( Saidullah )
