Film ki कहानी


Daniyal Khan kabhi Indian Army mai Captain hua karta tha par abhi ek shaant aur gumnaam zindagi jee raha hai Pahadiyo mai. Bhartiya Khufiya Agency RAW ke Chief Roy Daniyal ko dhoondh kar 26/11 Mumbai Attacks keliye zimmedaar logo ka khaatma karne ka mission niyukt(assign) kartey hai. Daniyal Retired RAW Agent Nawaz Mistry ke saath milke London America aur Pakistan jaake sabhi Aatankiyo ko Allah se paas pahuchaa deta hai. Daniyal Pakistani Sena ke haatho maara jaata hai. Desh ka badla poora hua par Daniyal-Nawaz ki kahaani adhoori reh gayi...
