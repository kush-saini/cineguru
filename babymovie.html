Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ek Special Task Force "Baby" ki jo Bhartiya Sarkar ne Aatankwaadi gatividhiyo ko rokne keliye banayi thi. Baby ko lead karta hai Feroz Ali Khan. Baby ka sabse kaabil officer hai Ajay Singh Rajput. Ajay ne kuch din pehle hi gaddar Jamal se khufiya jaankari nikalwa kar Delhi mai hone wale blast ko roka hai. Ajay ka agla mission hai bhaage huey aatankwadi Bilal ko terminate(khallaas) karna. Bilal ko terminate kartey waqt Ajay ke haath Maulana Mohammad Rehman bhi lag jaata hai jo Pakistaniyo ke dilo mai Hindustaniyo ke khilaaf zeher gholta hai. Apni team ki madad se Ajay Maulana ko India pahuchaaney mai kaamyab hojaata hai. Maulana ab jail mai hai...

Honest Overview



Ye film Jasoosi ki AUTHENTIC(Asal) side ko darshaati hai. Akshay behad "SHARP" aur "INTENSE" dikh rahey hai aur 100(Sau) Pratishat aapko apni adaakari(Acting) se Hakka-Bakka kardengey. Ye Movie aapki Instant Favourite ban jaegi. This movie is India's Very Own Mission Impossible. Kam shabdo mai bole toh - " Marvelous Addition to INDIAN Cinema ". 1000% STRONGLY Recommended !!!
