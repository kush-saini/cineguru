Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Desh ki shaan Mashoor Dhaavak(Runner) Paan Singh TOmar ki jo National Champion reh chukey thay. Paan Singh Army se nikalne ke baad ek sedhi saadhi zindagi bitaana chaahtey thay par Zameen kay ek vaadh-vivaadh ko lekar Bandook uthaane ko majboor hokar ek Daaku ban gaye. Paan Singh apna sabse bada badla le chuka hai aur Police se bachta hua bhaag raha hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Paan Singh ) , Rajendra Gupta( Randhawa ) , Brijendra Kala( Reporter ) , Vipin Sharma( Masand ) , Mahi Gill( Indira ) , Nawazuddin Siddiqui( Gopi ) , Zakir Hussain( Inspector Rathore )
