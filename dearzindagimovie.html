Dear Zindagi-2016


 8.3 / 10

Directed by Gauri Shinde


Philosophy Drama Life


Box Office Success


Shahrukh and Alia Bhatt's 1st movie appearance together

Film ki कहानी


Kaira filmein banana chahti hai. Kaira ka boyfriend uska dil todke kisi aur se shaadi karleta hai. Iss se dukhi hokar Kaira apne maa-baap aur bhai ke paas Goa rehne chali jaati hai. Kaira gharwalo se apne rishtey ko lekar bhi khush nahi hai aur raat ko so bhi nahi paati. Iss sab ka ilaaj karane voh Psychologist Dr. Jehangir Khan ke paas jaati hai. Dr Jehangir Kaira ko zindagi jeena sikhadeta hai. Ab Kaira ke saare rishto mai bas khushiya hi hai. Maano ki ab Kaira phir se saans lena seekh chuki hai. Kaira ki mulakat ek ajnabi se hoti hai aur pyaar ki ek nahi kahaani shuru ho chuki hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Alia Bhatt( Kaira ) , Shahrukh Khan( Dr. Jehangir ) , Ali Zafar( Rumi ) , Ira Dubey( Fatty ) , Yashaswini Dayama( Jackie ) , Kunal Kapoor( Raghu )

Honest Overview



Alia Bhatt ne effortless acting ki hai. Ye movie aapko zindagi jeena sikhaati hai . " Film dekhne ke baad aapko bohot achcha mehsoos hoga " Angrezi mai bole toh IT's a FEEL GOOD Movie . Ye film zindagi ki choti-choti khushiyo ko enjoy karna sikhati hai. MUST Watch !!!
