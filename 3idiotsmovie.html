3 Idiots-2009


 8.7 / 10

Directed by Rajkumar Hirani


Comedy Drama Feel-Good


Mega BlockBuster


India's Most Loved Film

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai teen dosto ki Rancho Farhan aur Raju. Teeno ek hi college mai padhtey hai. Rancho padhai ko padhai nahi khel samajhta hai. Farhan photography karna chaahta hai par abba ke darr se engineer ban raha hai aur Raju khud se zyada bharosa bhagwan pe karta hai. In-teeno ko College Director ka bulawa aata rehta hai.Rancho humesha apni class mai first aata hai aur Raju aur Farhan niche se first aate hai.Teen saal guzar chukey hai aise hi aur 4th year aa chuka hai. Ek din College Director Viru Raju aur Farhan ka mazak udaa-deta hai sabke saamne. Woh raat Teeno Viru se badla lene keliye uske ghar ke bahar susu kardetey hai. Raju ko Viru apne office mai suspend karne keliye bulaata hai par agar wo Rancho pe ilzaam lagaye toh khud bach jaaega. Raju apne dost ko dokha dene se achcha khidki se koodna samajhta hai aur buri tarah ghayal hojaata hai. Hospital mai Rancho aur Farhan uska manoranajan karte rehte hai. Raju theek hojaata hai. Rancho se kiye wado ke anusar Raju khudpe bharosa karke interview dene jaata hai. Farhan ki intership Wildlife Photography mai teh ho chuki hai par wo darr raha hai lekin Rancho ki hausla-afzaai ke baad Farhan apne abba ko mana leta hai. Farhan aur Raju behad khush hai. Kahi Saal beet chukey hai aur Raju aur Farhan dhoondhne nikle hai Rancho ko , dhoondhne pe pata chalta hai ki wo Ladakh mai hai aur uska naam Phunsukh Wangdoo hai. Shuruat mai thodey naraaz hotey par thodi der baad dono Rancho se galey lag jaate hai...

Honest Overview



Entrepreneurs ke liye ye film ek Motivation ka definite dose hai. Ye film har baar nayi si lagti hai chaahe kitni bhi baar dekhi jaaye. Content Creators ke liye ye film kisi Film-School ke jaisi hai. Ye film almost kisi Therapy Session ke jaisi hai. Jab bhi aap low feel karein iss film ko dekh-ke aapka mood definitely better ho jaega aur ye film College se judaa har emotion apne andar liye huey hai. Aamir-Madhavan-Sharman teeno ne hi apne career ki Most Magical Performance di hai. Director Rajkumar Hirani has hit the ball out of the park with this Master-Stroke. Apne Chaddi-Baddi yaaron ke saath iss film ko dekhna is almost A RULE. 1000% VERY STRONGLY RECOMMENDED WATCH, HAVE FUN...
