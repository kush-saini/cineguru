Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai paanch doston ki Deva, Chip, Rocker, Simran aur PP ki. Police PP aur Simran ko ek Robbery ke sandeh mai pakadletey hai. London ka Mashoor Lawyer Krishan Pandit Simran aur PP ki taraf se case lad raha hai. PP aur Simran ko puraa sach bata detey hai kis tarah Majboori mai unhe Robbery karni padi aur kaise Chip Deva aur Rocker maare gaye. Pandit PP aur SImran ka case jeet jaata hai. Pandit ko par iss case ki Kahaani kuch khatak rahi hai lekin kyu ? - jaaniye film dekh-kar...
