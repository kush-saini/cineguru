Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Reshma ki jiski zindagi Black and White hai par wo sapne dekhti hai Rango bhari Cinema ki Duniya ke. Reshma ghar se bhaag jaati hai aur Director Selva ki nazrein uske andar ke X-factor ko pehchaan leti hai. Reshma ko uske fans Silk ke naam se jaantey hai. SIlk actor Suryakanth se apne Rishtey khatam karke maano bilkul toot si gayi hai. Barso baad Reshma apne gao lauti hai par Maa bhi ussey thukraa deti hai. Kuch dino baad Reshma mrit paayi jaati hai. Ab uski tanhaai khatam ho chuki hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Abraham ) , Vidya Balan( Reshma ) , Naseeruddin Shah( Suryakanth ) , Tusshar Kapoor( Ramakanth ) , Rajesh Sharma( Selva )

Ye film Dekhne ke Baad sabhi (Boys)Laundey LOVE(pyaar) mai hongey aur sabhi Ladkiya thoda INSECURE feel kar sakti hai.
