Film ki कहानी


Marigold choti-moti American filmo me kaam karne wali ek actress hai aur India aayi ek movie shoot karne. Woh movie cancel ho gayi hai par luckily ussey ek doosri film mai role mil gaya hai. Marigold ki dosti hojaati hai film ke choreographer Prem se. Prem Marigold ko apne ghar invite karta hai aur ussey Rajasthan bhi ghumaata hai. Marigold Prem se pyaar karti hai par Prem aur Jaanvi ki arranged marriage se dukhi hai. Barry Marigold ka boyfriend hai ussey milne waha aaya hai. Marigold ab Prem ko chaahti hai aur Barry Jahnvi ko aur dono jodo ki aakhir mai shaadi hojaati hai but ek twist ke saath...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Ali Larter( Marigold ) , Simone Singh( Sharia ) , Nandana Sen( Jaanvi ) , Gulshan Grover( Vikram )

Honest Overview



Salman Khan aur Ali Larter ki Chemistry aapko behad pasand aaegi. Salman ek bikul hi alag avatar mai dikhengey aapko iss film mai. Rajasthan ki khoobsurati bhi aapko dekhne ko milegi iss film mai. TO BE BLUNT HEROINE KI KHOOBSURATI KE ALAWA AUR KUCH BHI INTERESTING NAHI HAI FILM MAI !!!
