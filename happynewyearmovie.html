Happy New Year-2014


 5.2 / 10

Directed by Farah Khan


Action Comedy


Box Office Success


Shot in Dubai

Film ki कहानी


Charlie apne pitah Manohar ka badla Charan Grover se lena chahta hai jiski wajah se aaj Manohar jail mai hai. Charlie Dubai jaake 300 crore ke heere churane ka plan banata hai. Charlie ki team mai hai taakatwar Jagmohan, Security expert Tammy, Computer Hacker Rohan aur Nandu jo ki Charan ke bete ka humshakal hai. Dubai pahuchke team heere chori karne mai kaamyab hojaati hai aur Charan ab salakho ke peeche hai. Charlie ka badla poora ho chuka hai aur team ke log khushi se apni zindagi bitah rahe hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Charlie ) , Deepika Padukone( Mohini ) , Abhishek Bachchan( Nandu ) , Jackie Shroff( Charan ) , Sonu Sood( Jagmohan ) , Boman Irani( Tammy ) , Vivaan Shah( Rohan )

Honest Overview



Film kisi bhadh-dhe mazaak jaisi hai aur actors ki wajah se hi chali hai. Film ke gaaney badiya hai. Faran Khan ji ne dissapoint kiya hai. Only a One Time Watch par koi zabardasti nahi hai , agar nahi bhi dekhoge toh bhi chal jaega !!!
