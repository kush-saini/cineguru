Film ki कहानी


Aasha apne college ke Rajesh se pyaar karti hai par apne pitah ke saamne usko naa jaan-ney ka naatak karti hai. Ek din College ke ek function mai Aasha ke papa ussey Rajesh ke saath nain-matakka kartey huey pakad letey hai aur khari-khoti sunaatey hai. Aasha ke pitah aur Rajesh ke pitah Business ki duniya mai ek doosre ke sabase bade Prati-dhvandhi(Competetor) hai aur ek-duje se nafrat kartey hai. Rajesh aur Aasha apne ghar chchodkar bhaagjaatey hai par ek gunda Rana unhe rok leta hai. Rana khazaane ki talaash mai hai jiska naksha Rajesh ko yaad hai. Rajesh Rana ko khazane tak pahucha deta hai jaha khoofiya Inspector Sher Khan unhe kanoon ka paath padhata hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Rajesh ) , Juhi Chawla( Aasha ) , Tiku Talsania( Aasha's papa ) , Shafi Inamdar( Bhushan ) , Paresh Rawal( Hari ) , Kader Khan( KK ) , Kiran Kumar( Rana ) , Dalip Tahil( Mike ) , Viju Khote( Godbhole )

Honest Overview



FIlm kuch jagaho par Bevajah ka Stupid Melodrama dikha ke Irritate karti hai but baaki film DECENT hai. kader Khan ji ne apne anokhe kirdaar ki madad se iss doobti hui film ko kinaare tak pahuchaya hai. Film shuruat mai thoda Thrill aur Excitement deti hai lekin aakhir mai aapki ummeedo pe hugg deti hai. MERELY & BARELY A ONE TIME-WATCH ONLY & ONLY FOR AAMIR-JUHI fans !!!
