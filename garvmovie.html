Garv-2004


 5.8 / 10

Directed by Puneet Issar


Action Drama


Box Office Success


Punnet Issar's Directorial debut

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ACP Arjun Ranawat ki jo desh ke bhakshako ka vidhwans karne ka sankalp le chuka hai. Arjun ka saathi ACP Haider Ali bhi Arjun ke encounter karne se sehmat hai par brasht netao aur police wale unhe chaiyn ki saans nahi lene detey. Haider ke mazhab ko dekh-kar ussey police force se nikaal diya jaata hai. Arjun ki Behen Rakhi ka Brasht Neta, Policewale aur Unerworld Don Zafar milke rape kardetey hai, jiska badle letey huey wo unn-sab ko maut ke ghaat utaar deta hai. Inhi hatyao keliye Arjun ko maut ki sazaa sunaai jaati hai par Arjun khaandaan ki izzat ko bachaney ke liye Court mai kuch nahi bolta lekin aakhri pal mai Rakhi apne saath huey kukaram ke baare mai Judge sahab ko bataati hai. Judge sahab Arjun ko baaizzat bari karte hai aur sabhi brasht netaao ko sazaa sunaate hai. Arjun ko uske man ka sukoon wapas mil chuka hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Arjun ) , Arbaaz Khan( Haider ) , Shilpa Shetty( Jannat ) , Akansha Malhotra( Rakhi ) , Kulbushan Kharbanda( Acharya ) , Anupam Kher( Lawyer ) , Govind Namdev( Trivedi ) , Shivaji Satam( Deshpandey ) , Mukesh Rishi( Zafar Supari ) , Mohan Joshi( Kulkarni )

Honest Overview



"Kanoon ki rakhwali Police ke hotey huey koi bhi iss desh ka Baal ki baaka nahi kar sakta" iss film mai policewaalo ke zindagi ke asli pressure aur mental trauma ko dikhaya gya hai. Film se brilliant storytelling , screenplay expect mat kariega par Film mai ek bhi pal Boring nahi hai. Film ke kuch scenes aapko jhanjhor dengey. RECOMMENDED ONLY FOR BHAIJAAN ke BHAKTS!!!
