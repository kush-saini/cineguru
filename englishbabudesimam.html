Film ki कहानी


Hari aur Vikram London mai apne pitah Gopal mayur ki Mayur Industries sambhaalte hai. Hari apne pitah ke dost ki beti se shaadi karna nahi chahta aur flight pakad kar bhaag raha hota hai, voh flight crash ho jaati hai. Hari Mumbai ke kinare behta hua pahuchta hai, Katariya naam ki ek ladki uski jaan bachaleti hai. Pyar mai padkar dono shaadi krlete hai, Katariya ab pait se hai .Ek haadhse mai Hari aur Katariya apni jaan gawa dete hai par doctors bachche( Nandu ) ko bachane mai kamyaab ho jaate hai. Katariya ki behen Bijuriya Nandu ko palti hai. Vikram India aake apne khandaan ke waaris Nandu ko wapis lejaana chahta hai par Bijuriya Nandu ko apna beta maanti hai aur khudse dur nahi dekh sakti par Vikram zidd pe ada hai. Ek din Nandu ko bachate hue Bijuriya Bheema ke jaal mai fas jaati hai, Bheema uski izzat lootne hi waala hota hai ki tabhi Vikram waha pahuchkar Bheema ko maarke Bijuriya ko bacha leta hai. Vikram Bijuriya ko apna kar ussey shaadi karleta hai aur apne naye pariwar ke saath London chala jaata hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Vikram/Hari/Gopal ) , Sonali Bendre( Bijuriya ) , Rajeshwari Sachdev( Katariya ) , Kiran Kumar( Bheema ) , Saeed Jaffrey( Vakeel Madadgar )
