Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Radio Jockey Ambar aur Chef Nikhil Arora urf Nick ki. Nikhil Ambar ke saath apne radio interview keliye late hojaata hai. Iss baat se naraaz hokar Ambar Radio pe Nick ki khilli udaadeti hai. Nick aur Ambar ek dusre se bina miley nafrat karney lagey hai. Nick aur Ambar ek dusre se haqeeqat mai milkar Pyaar ke bandhan mai bandh jaatey hai. Dono saath rehene lagtey hai, Ambar pait se hai aur Nick bachcha nahi chaahta par dheere dheere apne hone wale nanne-munne keliye dono kareeb aajaatey hai. Dono ab Kunware Maa-Baap ban chukey hai aur behad khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Nick ) , Preity Zinta( Ambar ) , Arshad Warsi( Ron ) , Jave Jaffrey( Jaggu ) , Jugal Hansraj( Jignesh ) , Abhishek Bachchan( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Ye film har Male ke liye Relationships , Zindagi aur Character Development ka ek Crash Course hai with Pyaar filled NOK-JHOK and lots of Interesting Characters. Javed Jaffrey apne Eccentric(Vichitra) kirdaar se aapko zaroor hasaaengey. Ye film sab ladko ko COMMITMENT karne ki seekh deti hai aur ye bhi bataati hai that GIRLS are Superior than us in EVERY Possible Way without hurting our LITTLE EGO's. MUST WATCH for Boys kisi Relationship mai aane se pehle. 1000% STRONGLY RECOMMENDED to Nibba-Nibbi's of Current Generation !!!
