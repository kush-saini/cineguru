Gabbar Is Back-2015


 4.9 / 10

Directed by Krish


Justice Action


Box Office Success


Story of Modern Bhagat Singh

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ek aam College Professor Aditya Singh Rajput ki. Aditya ka alter-ego hai Gabbar jisne apni ek alag duniya banayi hai. Uss duniya mai har rishvatkhor beimaan insaan ko Gabbar ki adalat ke kathgare mai khada hona padta hai aur sabko Saza-e-Maut hi milti hai. Police ke officers Gabbar ke khilaaf koi sabut nahi juta paa rahey hai. Police mai Driver Sadhu Gabbar ko pakadne keliye chup-chaap information ikkhati kar raha hai kyonki sabhi seniors uska mazaak udaatey hai. Sadhu CBI officer Kuldeep ke saamne apni mehnat pesh karta hai jissey Officer behad khush hai. Gabbar khudko police ke havaaley kar chuka hai aur saari hathyao ki zimmedaari leta hai. Gabbar ko faansi hojaati hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Gabbar ) , Sunil Grover( Sadhu ) , Shruti Hassan( Shruti ) , Jaideep Ahlawat( Kuldeep ) , Suman Talwar( Digvijay ) , Kareena Kapoor( Cameo ) , Chitrangada Singh( Cameo )

Honest Review



Tayyar ho jaaiye SUNIL Grover ji ko ek alag Avatar mai dekhne aur chaukne keliye. Ye film Kanoon ke daayre mai rehkar Fearlessly(Bekhauf) ladhne ki seekh deti hai. Ye film Sukhe Gol-Gappe ke jaisi jiska Swad alag hai par ussey BeySwaad nahi kaha jaa sakta. 1000% Advised to YOUTH !!!
