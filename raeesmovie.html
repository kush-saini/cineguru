Film ki कहानी


Raees bachpan se hi gareebi ke kaaran Jairaj ke liye daaru ki smuggling karta hai. Raees ab bada ho chuka hai aur apna dhandha kholna chahta hai. Mumbai ke Musabhai ki madad se Raees apne dhandha khol-leta hai. Sabhi police wale Raees ki jeb mai hai. Inspector Majmudar Raees ke pichey pada hai par Raees netao ke support se Majmudar ka transfer karwa deta hai. Raees ka badhta dhandha dekh-kar Jairaj ussey marwaney ka intezaam karta hai. Raees badla lene keliye Jairaj ke aadmiyo ko maarta hua aakhir kaar Jairaj tak pahuchta hai aur bhaari mann ke saath Jairaj ke shareer ko goliyon se bhoon deta hai. Majmudar wapas aa chuka hai. Musa ne Raees ko dhoka dekar ussey blast karwadiya hai jisme bekasoor maare gaye hai.Raees Majmudar ko surrender kar chuka hai. Majmudar Raees ko sunsaan jagah pe lejakar encounter kardeta hai. Raees aakhri saans leta hue apno ko yaad karta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Raees ) , Nawazuddin Siddiqui( Inspector Jaideep ) , Mahira Khan( Aasiya ) , Mohammad Zeeshan Ayub( Raees's friend ) , Sheeba Chaddha( Raees's mother ) , Atul Kulkarni( Jairaj )

Honest Overview



Ye film Tom and Jerry ke kisi episode jaisi jaha dono ek-dusre se ek kadam aage rehne ki koshish kar rahe hai. Shahrukh aur Nawazuddin ke har dialogue pe seeti bajaane ka mann karta hai. " Action-Packed , Masaledaar movie ". MUST WATCH FOR SHAHRUKH AND NAWAZ FANS !!!
