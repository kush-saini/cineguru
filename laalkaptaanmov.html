Laal Kaptaan-2019


 5.6 / 10

Directed by Navdeep Singh


Drama Action


Box Office Failure


Completely Wicked

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ek Gossain ki jo inaam keliye logo ka khoon karta hai par uski Zindagi ka asli maqsadh hai apne jaani dushman Rehmat Khan ko Khuda se paas bhejhna. Rehmat pe hamla karne ki ek naakam koshish mai Gossain pakda jaata hai. Gossain ki kismat ussey Rehmat ke changul se nikalne mai madad karti hai. Kahi mahino ke baad Gossain Rehmat ke aadmiyo ko maarke ussey bandhi banaa leta hai aur Ussey faansi pe chadha kar apne pariwar ki maut ka badla leta hai. Anth mai ek kamaal ki cheez hoti hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Gossain ) , Deepak Dobriyal( Gumnaam ) , Sonakshi Sinha( Cameo ) , Simone Singh( Begum ) , Manav Vij( Rehmat )

Honest Overview



Saif Ali Khan ji ka Devilish Charm aur Volcanic Intensity iss film ka sabse mazboot aur EXCITING pehlu hai. Ye film Adhpaki Biryani ki tarah kabhi Zaaykedaar hai toh kabhi Beswaad. FILM technically behad Strong hai aur apne VIOLENT aur BARBARIC action Scenes se aapki Attention Hold kar sakti hai. Agar Film ki lambaai "The Great Khali" level ki naa hokey "Rajpal Yadav" level ki hoti toh Better hota. VERDICT - DELICIOUS but not Enough !!!
