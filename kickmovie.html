Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Devilal Singh ki jo apne dil ki sunta hai aur wo hi karta hai jisse ussey "kick" milti hai. Issi pagalpan pe Dr.Shaina fida hojaati hai jo ki ek dimaag ki doctor hai par Devi ki gair-zimmedaari dekh kar ussey rishta tod deti hai. Ab Devi ka nayaa junoon hai paisa kamaana. Devi Devil banke ameer logo ko loot-ta hai aur kaabil Inspector Tyagi ko Devil ka case mila hai. Devi Tyagi ko agli chori ke baare mai clue dekar phone kaat-deta hai. Devi Poland pahuchkar Memory loss ka patient bankar aata hai ussi ke ghar mai rehta hai. Ittefaq se Tyagi aur Shaina ke maa-baap ne unki meeting teh kari hai aur Tyagi bhi Devi ke saath ussi ghar mai reh raha hai. Devi bimar bachcho keliye Shiv Gajra ko lootne ka plan banata hai jisne past mai ussey beizzati karke apne office se nikaal pheka tha. Devi iss chori mai kaamyaab hojaata hai aur Gajra ko bhagwan ke paas pohochaa-deta hai. Bachcho ki bheed Devi ko police se bhaagne mai madad karti hai. Inspector Tyagi ko Devi ke nek iraado ka pata chal jaata hai. Kuch dino baad Devi Inspector banke Tyagi ke saamne muskura rahaa hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Review



"Zyada na sochtey hue bas Moments keliye jeena chaiye" film ye message dekar jaati hai. Nawazuddin Siddique ne Memorable performance di hai. Aisa interesting Villain aap pehli baar dekhengey. UNBELIEVABLE ACTION SEQUENCES aur ACTING ka zordaar dhamaaka hai ye film. EXCITING from THE GET GO with a satisying end . A 1000% RECOMMENDED WATCH !!!
