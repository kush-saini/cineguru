Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai teen dosto ki Sameer Aakash aur Sid. Teeno ki dosti Fevi-Quick ke jod jitni mazboot hai. Aakash bilkul pagal hai aur dosto keliye kuch bhi kar sakta hai, Sameer ko aaye din kisi se pyaar hojaata hai aur Sid apni rango ki duniya hai khoya rehta hai. Ek raat Aakash Sid ki pasand ki ek ladki Tara ko lekar mazak kardeta hai, Wo raat unki zindagi badal-deti hai. Teeno dost ab apni zindagiyo mai busy ho chuke hai. Aakash Sydney chala gaya hai dad ka business sambhaalne aur ussey apni kind-of-a-dost ladki Shalini se pyaar hojaata hai jiski shaadi Rohit se hone waali hai jo thoda badtameez hai. Shalini bhi Aakash ko chaane lagi hai par Rohit ke maa-baap ka ehsaan chukaane keliye Rohit se shaadi kar rahi hai. Aakash Shalini ki shaadi mai ghuske ussey sabke saamne apne pyaar ka izhaar karta hai aur Rohit ke pitaah bhi iss rishtey se sehmat hai, Dono premi ek ho chuke hai. Aakash uss raat keliye Sid se maafi maangta hai aur aisa lagta hai ki unki dosti ka RESUME button dab chuka hai...
