Dangal-2016


 8.3 / 10

Directed by Nitesh Tiwari


Sports Drama


Gigantic Box Office


Hearty Reception Around the Globe

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Pehelwani mai National Champion Mahavir Singh Phogat ji ki jinhe paise kamaane keliye Pehelwani ko chchodna pada. Mahavir apne bachcho ke zariye Desh ke liye Medal laane ka apna sapna pura karna chaahta hai par unhe har baar ek beti hi hoti hai. Mahavir niraasha se ghir chukey par ek din unki sabse badi do betiyan Geeta aur Babita ke kisi ko peetne ki khabar saamne aati hai jissey Mahavir samajh jaata hai ki uska sapna betiyan bhi poora kar sakti hai. Agle din se Geeta aur Babita ki kadhi training chaalu hojaati hai. Geeta alag-alag shehero ke Dangal jeetne lagti hai aur dekhte-dekhte National level Champion ban jaati hai, Purey gao ko uspe naaz hai. Geeta National Sports Academy chali jaati hai CommonWealth Games ki tayaari keliye aur naye Coach ki training apnaati hai apne pitah ki training ko bhool ke. Geeta kahi matches haar rahi hai Vishwa-stariya level pe aur CommonWealth Games se pehle wo apne papa se chupke se training le rahi hai aur ussi Training ki badaulat wo CommonWealth Games mai Desh keliye Gold Medal leaati hai. Mahavir ki khushi ka thikaana nahi hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Mahavir Singh Phogat ) , Fatima Sana Shaikh( Geeta ) , Zaira Wasim( Geeta ) , Sanya Malhotra( Babita ) , Aparshakti Khurana( Omkar ) , Vivan Bhatena( Mahavir's colleague ) , Girish Kulkarni( Pramod ) , Ritvik Sahore( Young Omkar )

Honest Overview



Ye film sarkar ko athletes ki durgati ka ehsaas dilaati hai aur har Indian athlete ki taraf ke madad ki pukaar bhi lagaati hai. Haryanvi boli ki mithaas film ko aur bhi mazedaar banadeti hai. Ye film Vision ka peecha karna sikhaati hai phir chaahey wo Sports ho yaa kuch bhi. Aamir Khan ki iss role keliye dedication Exemplary hai aur film mai wo saaf dikhta hai. Masterful Direction by Nitesh Tiwari. Suspense and Thrill koot-koot ke film mai daala gaya hai. Popcorn lekar baith jaaiye iss film ko dekhne. 1000% RECOMMENDED TO Every AMBITiOUS INDIVIDUAL OUT THERE !!!
