Drama Romance


SuperHit


Fantastic Music

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Raj ki jo desh ke har naujavaan ki tarah Mumbai aaya hai ek singer banne ka sapna lekar aur apne jigri dost Abdul ke yaha thehra hua hai. Raj ki awaaz mai jaadoo hai par jeb mai footi kaudi bhi nahi hai. Raj ek ladki ki jaan bachane usey hospital lejaata hai, waha pe sab ussey Pooja ka aashiq Romi samajletey hai aur Raj ko ek nayi zindagi mil gayi hai jisme paisa aur aaram hai. Pooja ki dost Jahnvi ko dekhte hi Raj uspe fida hojaata hai aur aag dono taraf barabar lagi hai. Pooja ko hosh aa chuka hai par pariwar ke saamne wo Raj ka parda-faash nahi karti kyonki Raj ki nek-dili se Pooja ko pyar ho gya hai. Raj ki asliyat sabko pata chal jaati hai par Pooja ke pariwar wale Raj ko maaf kardetey hai . Pooja apne pyaar ko tyaag ke Raj aur Jahnvi ko milwa deti hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Overview



Ye film hume sikhati hai ki "Kismat achche logo ka thoda zyada saath deti hai". Shakti Kapoor ji ki badaulat film behad funny hai. "Shahrukh" ka cameo dekhna please mat bhuliyega.Har 90's kid iss film ke gaano se pyaar karta hai.First frame se lekar Last frame tak bas ek shabd "Entertaining". 1000% recommended for Bhai ke fans!!!
