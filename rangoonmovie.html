Film ki कहानी


World War - 2 ke dauraan Apni chalaaki se Subedaar Malik Dushman Desh ki kaid se bachke nikalne mai kaamyab hojaata hai. India pauchkar Malik ko lokrpiya abhinetri Julia ki hifaazat ka zimma saunpa gaya hai. Japaani Hamle mai Malik aur Julia apne dal se alag ho chukey hai. Kahi baar maut ka Saamna kar chuka Malik aur adakaara Julia mai bilkul nahi banti par halaat unko ek dusre ke kareeb leaatey hai. Malik Julia ko uske premi Rustom Billimoria ke paas mehfooz pahucha deta hai. British kaam mai Dakhal-andaazi karne keliye Malik ko kadhi sazaa milne wali hai par Malik unki besh keemti talwar lekar bhaag jaata hai. Maara Maari mai Julia aur Malik humesha keliye ek saath ho gaye hai Maut ko gale laga kar...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Rustom ) , Shahid Kapoor( Nawab ) , Kangana Ranaut( Julia ) , Richard McCabe( David ) , Gajraj Rao( Ahuja ) , Surendra Pal( Maharaja )

Honest Overview



Film mai CALL of DUTY - WORLD at WAR game jaisi vibes hai jissey har HardCore Gamer relate kar sakta hai. World War + Ghane Jungle ka BACKDROP(Prashthabhoomi) film ko Visually Stunning banaata hai. INSAAN ke Primal Insticts - Survival aur Pyaar ko khoobsurati se pesh kiya gaya hai. IMMACULATE Acting by Kangana Ranaut. Ye film Bread Pakode ke jaisi hai Jisme Shahid Paneer banke uska Swaad dus gunaa badha rahey hai. An Interesting Piece of Cinema jo POPCORN Worthy hai. 1000% RECOMMENDED to those jinhe History aur World-War fascinate karta hai !!!
