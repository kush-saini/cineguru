Had a Bad Luck at the Box-Office


Outlandish Story

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Mashoor jaadugar Ethan Mascarenhas ki jo apne ek show ke dauraan ghayal ho gaye thay aur abhi apne chehrey ke alawa Shareer ke kisi bhi hissey mai koi Gathi-Vidhi nahi karpaatey. Ethan ka dhyan rakhti hai unki Khoobsurat Housekeeper Sofia. Ethan apni zindagi ki bebasi se pareshan aakar apni zindagi ko Legally khatam karna chaahta hai. Ethan ki dost Lawyer Devyani ki madad se Ethan ki ye arzi Court mai accept karli jaati hai. Ethan apne marne se pehle Sofia se shaadi karleta hai. Ethan ab Bhagwan ke paas hai aur ek nayi zindagi keliye line mai khada hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Hrithik Roshan( Ethan ) , Aishwarya Rai( Sofia ) , Shernaz Patel( Devyani ) , Aditya Roy( Omar ) , Nafisa Ali( Ethan's maa ) , Suhel Seth( Doc ) , Rajit Kapur( Vipin )

Honest Overview



First of all, Ye film keval Emotionally Evolved HomoSapiens(Insaan) ko hi dekhni chaahiye. Film Melancholy(Google agar nahi pata) aur Khushiyo ko ghol kar banayi gayi Shikanji ki tarah hai - "Thodi Khataas aur Thodi Mithaas apne andar liye huey". POWER-Full Istemaal of MUSIC jo Heart-Touching hai. "Bahubali" level of Acting by Hrithik Roshan. Film forces you to think Hard on The Definition of Life. 1000% RECOMMENDED to those jinhe Kitaabey padhne ka shauk hai !!!
