Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Kiran ki jo chhuttiyon mai apne bhai Vijay aur bhabhi Poonam ke ghar aayi hai. Kiran ek Navy Officer Sunil se pyar krti hai jo abhi ek khatarnak mission pura krke usse milne aaya hai. Sunil Kiran ko ek ghar tohfe mai deta hai. Ek sarfira aashiq Kiran ko ek dhun bajake aur phone krke pareshaan kr rha hai. Ek din Sunil ki mulaqat Captain Mehra ke bete Rahul se hoti hai aur dono mai dosti hojati hai. Rahul dimaagi taur se bimaar hai aur apni mari hui maa se phone pe baatein krta hai aur Kiran se pyaar krta hai jo usi ke saath pdti thi. Sunil aur Kiran uss sarfire aashiq ko milne ke liye bulakar ussey pakadne ka plan banate hai par Rahul Sunil ko dekh kar waha se nikal jaata hai. Sunil aur Kiran shaadi krlete hai aur honeymoon ke liye Switzerland chale jaate hai. Rahul Switzerland pahuch jaata hai aur wahi Sunil usey Kiran se milwata hai , Kiran Rahul ko pehchaan leti hai aur uska majak udati hai. Sunil ko Vijay ka phone aata hai aur Sunil ko Rahul pe shaq hota hai .Sunil Kiran ko boat pe jaane ko kehta hai. Sunil usi dhun ka peecha krte hue Rahul tak pahuchta hai aur uska shaq yakeen mai badal jaata hai. Sunil Rahul ko berehmi se maarta hai . Rahul Sunil ke sharir mai chaku ghop kar Kiran ki boat pe pahuch jaata hai aur kehta hai K..K..K..Kiran .Kiran darr jaati hai , Rahul apni maa se baat krne lgta aur shaadi ki taiyaari krne lgta hai. Tabhi Sunil waha aajata hai aur Rahul ko uski maa ke paas pahucha deta hai. Sunil aur Kiran apne honeymoon se wapas aajate hai.
