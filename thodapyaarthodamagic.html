Film ki कहानी


Ranbir ek kaamyab business man hai aur har din ki tarah aaj bhi wo office jaa raha hai. Uska dhyaan bhatak jaata hai aur Accident mai EK Couple maara jaata hai jinke 4 bachche hai. Court unn Chaar bachcho ko Ranbir ke saath rehne ka Order deta hai. Bachche Ranbir ko apne Maa-Baap ka gunhegaar maantey hai aur Kadvaahat se baat kartey hai. Bachcho ki dhek-bhaal keliye Housekeeper Geeta ko rakha jaata hai jo Bachcho aur Ranbir ko kareeb leaati hai. Ranbir ab Geeta ko chaahne laga hai. Asal mai Geeta Jannat se aayi Pari hai aur uske wapas jaane ka waqt aa chuka hai par Ranbir aur Bachcho ka Pyaar ussey Prithvi pe rok leta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Ranbir ) , Rani Mukherji( Geeta ) , Rishi Kapoor( God ) , Ameesha Patel( Malaika ) , Sharat Saxena( Jaswant ) , Razak Khan( Pappu )

Honest Overview



FIlm kisi DISNEY Fairy Tale ki jitni Charming hai aur Aapko apne Bachpan mai ek Deep Dive maarne ki Oppurtunity deti hai. "Pyaar woh Lava hai jo Hardest Paththar jaise DIl ko bhi pighla ke narm bana sakta hai" film mai khoosburati ke saath dikhaaya gaya hai. Bachcho ki Daada-Giri aur Jadoo with a CUTE Twist film ko UNBEATABLE banaata hai. FILm SHIKANJI ke glass ke jaisa REFRESHMENT deti hai. 1000% HIGHLY RECOMMENDED to those jinke andar ka bachcha Unhe aaj bhi pukaar lagataa hai !!!
