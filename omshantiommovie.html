Om Shanti Om-2007


 8.2 / 10

Directed by Farah Khan


Romance Drama


BlockBuster


Deepika Padukone's 1st film

Film ki कहानी


Om Makhija filmo mai junior artist ka kaam karta hai aur Shanti Priya naam ki actress se pyaar karta hai. Ek film ki shooting ke dauraan Om Shanti ko jalne se bachata hai aur dono mai dosti hojaati hai. Om ko Shanti aur Producer Mukesh Mehra ki shaadi ka pata chalta hai aur uska dil toot-jaata hai. Mukesh Shanti ko apni film ke set pe bulaata hai aur Shanti ko zinda jala deta hai, Om waha se guzar raha hota hai aur Shanti ko bachane pahuchta hai par Mukesh ke guards ussey buri tarah dhotey hai. Adhmaraa saa Om mashoor actor Rajesh Kapoor ki gaadi se takraata hai , Rajesh Om ko hospital mai bharti karata hai , ussi hospital mai Rajesh ki biwi bachche ko janam dene wali hai . Om dum tod deta hai aur Rajesh ke ghar ek beta paidah hota jiska naa vo OM rakhte hai. OM Makhija ne OM Kapoor ke roop mai naya janam liya hai. OM Kapoor bada ho chuka hai aur ek movie star hai, OM Kapoor ki mulakaat Mukesh Mehra se hoti hai aur ussey apni purani zindagi yaad aajati hai. OM ShantiPriya ke hoobahoo dikhne wali Sandy ki madad se Mukesh ko kaanoon ke hawale karne ka plan banaata hai. OM Mukesh ko ussi set pe lejaata hai aur ShantiPriya ki maut ka zimmedaar thehraata hai. Mukesh aur Om mai ladai chchid jaati hai tabhi ShantiPriya ka bhoot waha aajata hai aur Mukesh ko jaan se maardeta hai. Om apni pyaari Shanti ko ek aakhri baar dekhta hai .

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Om / Om Kapoor ) , Deepika Padukone( Shantipriya / Sandy ) , Arjun Rampal( Mukesh ) , Shreyas Talpade( Pappu ) , Kirron Kher( Om's mother ) , Javed Sheikh( Om Kapoor's father )

Honest Overview



Sapno ki duniya ko parde pe utaarna Farah Khan ko bahut achche se aata hai. " Halki-fulki comedy , Hit songs aur adhoore pyaar ki kahaani ". Film mai 70s ke daur ko dikhaya gaya hai. Family ke saath baithkar iss film ko enjoy kariye. Frame by Frame entertainment guaranteed !!!
