Sarfarosh-1999


 7.7 / 10

Directed by John Matthew Matthan


Action Drama


Box-Office Success


Based on Terrorism

Film ki कहानी


Ajay Singh Rathore ab ek ACP hai aur Gulfam Hassan ki ghazale sunna pasand karta hai. Gulfam ke ek show pe uski mulakaat apne college ki dost Seema se hoti hai aur Ajay ki Gulfam sahab se dosti bhi hojati hai. Seema Ajay ke ACP banne se bahut impress hai aur college se hi usko chaahti hai, Ajay ke dil ka haal bhi kuch aisa hi hai. Ajay Arms Trafficking ke case pe kaam kar raha hai jiske saare gawah maare jaa rahe hai aur Main Suspect Sultan unke haatho se bachke Pakistan bhaag gaya hai. Khabar saamne aati hai ki agli arms deal Gulfam ki puaraani haveli mai hone wali hai. Ajay Inspector Salim aur kuch policewalo ke saath waha pahuchta hai aur ussey Gulfam ki asliyat jaanke dukh hota hai. Gulfam Ajay ko khudke pe huye julm ke baare mai bata raha hai ki tabhi police force aake ussey gher leti hai. Gulfam aatm-hatya karke allah miyan se mulakaat pakki kardeta hai. Ajay apne next case keliye tayyar hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Ajay ) , Sonali Bendre( Seema ) , Naseeruddin Shah( Gulfam Hassan ) , Mukesh Rishi( Saleem ) , Pradeep Rawat( Sultan ) , Manoj Joshi( Bajju ) , Makrand Deshpande( Shiva ) , Akhilendra Mishra( Mirchi Seth ) , Govind Namdev( Veeran )

Honest Overview



Mazhab ka sahara lekar nafrat failaaney walo ka kya hashr hota hai iss film mai dikhaya gaya hai. Ye film ek BiG Salute hai Desh ki Police ko Bollywood ki taraf se. Naseeruddin ji ne hothon pe gazal aur zehen mai jihaad rakhne wale Gulfam ke kirdaar ko yaadgaar bana diya hai. Nawazuddin Siddique ji ka Cameo miss mat kariega. Aamir apne iss kadak avatar se aapko chauka dengey. 1000% STRONGLY RECOMMENDED !!!
