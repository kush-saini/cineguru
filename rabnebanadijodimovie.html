Rab ne Bana di Jodi-2008


 7.8 / 10

Directed by Aditya Chopra


Romance Comedy


Super Hit


Anushka Sharma's 1st film

Film ki कहानी


Surinder Suri Punjab Power mai kaam karta hai aur bahut seedha sa sharmila sa ladka hai. Surinder apne guru ki beti Taani ki shaadi mai aaya hai, Ek accident mai dulhe ki maut hojaati hai. Surinder ke guru ji ko ek ghatak sadma pahuchta hai aur unki aakhri ichcha ke chalte Surinder ki shaadi Taani se hojaati hai. Taani patni ka har dharm nibhaa rahi hai par Suri se pyaar nahi karti. Suri ka yaar Bobby ussey Taani ji ko hero-giri se impress karne ki salah deta hai, Suri Bobby ki madad se apne dusre roop Raj ko janam deta hai. Raj Taani ke saath dance competition mai hissa leta hai. Raj aur Taani ek dusre ko pasand karne lagte hai aur Raj Taani ko apne saath bhagake lejaana chaahta hai. Suri Bobby ki baat maanke Taani ko sab sach bataa-deta hai. Taani Suri se pyaar karne lagti hai aur abhi dono apne Honeymoon par hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Surinder Suri / Raj ) , Anushka Sharma( Taani ) , Vinay Pathak( Bobby ) , Kajol( Cameo ) , Lara Dutta( Cameo ) , Bipasha Basu( Cameo ) , Preity Zinta( Cameo ) , Rani Mukherji( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Ye film bikul Parle-G biscuit ke jaisi hai " SIMPLE YET SATISFYING ". Aditya Chopra ek dhamaakedaar film ke saath wapas aaye hai. Anushka Sharma apni pehli film mai chaa gyi hai. Chulbula Raj aur Bhola Suri dono kaafi entertaining hai. Bollywood aur Shahrukh fans ke liye 1000% recommended !!!
