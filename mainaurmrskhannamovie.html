Film ki कहानी


Samir aur Raina pehli baar church mai mile thay aur Love at first Hogya tha dono ko. Samir aur Raina ab Melbourne mai rehtey hai. Samir ka kaam-dhandha thoda manda pada hai. Samir naye desh kaam ki talaash mai jaa raha hai aur Raina ko bin bataye uski Delhi ki ticket krwa di hai, Raina issey naraaz hai aur Samir ke jaane ke baad wo Melbourne airport par apni dost Nina ki madad se ek job dhoond leti hai. Raina ko bas Samir ka intezaar hai. Airport par uski dosti hoti hai Aakash se jo ussey Mrs Khanna bulaata hai, Aakash Raina ko chaahta hai par dosti mai hi khush hai, Samir Raina ko lene Airport pahuch jaata hai aur Aakash aur uske dost Harsh ko apne saath rehene ko invite karta hai. Samir ko Raina pe shak ho raha hai Aakash ko lekey par dheere-dheere saare shak dur hojaatey hai. Raina Samir ke saath flight pakad-kar ek nayi zindagi mai entry kar rahi hai. Raina ko Aakash ka khat milta hai jisme likha hai ki Aakash ussey Mrs Khanna bulaata tha kyonki wo khud Aakash Khanna hai. Aakash ko apni Mrs Khanna ka intezaar nahi karna padega kyonki abhi ek ladki se uski nazre mili hai...
