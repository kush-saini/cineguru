Talaash-2012


 6.6 / 10

Directed by Reema Kagti


Supernatural Drama Crime


Box-Office Success


One of its Own Kind

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Inspector Suri ki jo apni biwi Roshni aur betey karan ke saath behad khush thay par Karan ki ek haadse mai maut hojaati hai, iske liye Suri khudko kasoorwar thehraata hai. Roshni aur Suri maano khush rehna hi bhool chuke hai. Suri actor Armaan ki maut ka case suljha raha hai. Ek raat Suri ki dosti ek night-girl Rosie se hoti hai, Suri Rosie ki madad se case mai naye breakthrough paa raha hai. Case solve ho chuka hai aur Suri Armaan ke dost Sanjay ko arrest karke lejaa raha hai ki tabhi Rosie gaadi ke saamne hawa mai se prakat ho jaati hai aur Suri ki gaadi samudra mai chali jaati hai par Rosie ki madad se woh bach jaata hai aur Sanjay maara jaata hai. Suri ye samaj chuka hai ki Rosie ek aatma thi aur Rosie ki woh dost jo kuch saal pehle gayab ho gayi thi woh Rosie khud hi thi. Suri Rosie ki aatma ko mukti deta hai. Suri khudko maaf kar chuka hai aur apni biwi ke saath khushi se jee raha hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Suri ) , Kareena Kapoor( Simran ) , Rani Mukherji( Roshni ) , Rajkummar Rao( Devrath ) , Nawazuddin Siddiqui( Taimur ) , Sheeba Chaddha( Nirmala ) , Vivan Bhatena( Armaan ) , Shernaz Patel( Frenny )

Honest Overview



"Kabhi-kabaar unsuni ankahee raaho pe chalke bhi jawaab mil jaatey hai iss film mai dikhaya gaya hai". OCCULT ka element film ko Mysterious aur Thrilling banata hai. Rani aapko bilkul alag avatar mai dikhengi. You can see 2 stars in making Nawazuddin and Rajkummar quite evidently. A 1000% MUST WATCH FOR LOVERS OF UNUSUAL CINEMA !!!
