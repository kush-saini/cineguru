Film ki कहानी


Krishna ek Chotey se gao mai apni daadi ke saath rehta hai aur din bhar gao mai matar-gashti karta ghumta rehta hai. Singapore se aayi hui Priya se uski dosti hojaati hai. Priya Krishna ko Sheher jaake apna Naam banaane ki salaah deti hai. Krishna Priya ki baat maankar uske paas Singapore chala aaya hai. Kuch din baad ROhit ko apne pitaah ke zinda aur Dr.Siddhant Arya ki kaid mai hone ki khabar milti hai. Krishna Siddhant Arya ki Allah se Meeting fix karwake apne Pitah ROhit Mehra ko bacha leta hai. Krishna wapas gao pahuchkar apni daadi ko surprise kardeta hai.
