Pad Man-2018


 8.0 / 10

Directed by R.Balki


Drama Comedy


Massive Box Office


Story of a Man on a Mission

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Laxmi ki jo apni biwi ko mahawari(chums) ke waqt Pad istemaal karne ki salaah deta hai. Biwi ke na maan-ne par Laxmi khud hi jugaad se pad banaane ki Machine banadeta hai aur Saste daamo mai pad bechne lagta hai. Laxmi ko America mai bhi sammanit kiya jaata hai. Ab Laxmi ka sapna hai desh ke har koney tak pad ko pahuchanaa...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Laxmi ) , Sonam Kapoor( Pari ) , Radhika Apte( Gayatri ) , Amitabh Bachchan( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Laxmi aapko BURNING DESIRE ka peechcha karna aur Jugaad(Desi Management) karne ki art sikhaata hai. Agar aap SELF-BELIEF develop karna chaahtey hai toh iss film ko FOCUS ke saath dekhein. Ye film GLUCON-D ki tarah aapko instant ENERGY degi. Zindagi mai har Pehli baar kare gaye kaam ki khushi ko Movie ne perfectly Convey kiya hai . YE Movie aapko apne andar TRANSPORT karne ki capability rakhti hai because of its ENTICING screenplay & Akshay ji's happy vibe. 1000% STRONGLY RECOMMENDED to those jinhe ENTREPRENEUR ban-na hai !!!
