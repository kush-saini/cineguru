Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Laila ki jo Digvijay Singh ke Hotel mai Cabaret Dancer hai .Ek din Laila ko apni marti hui maa ka phone aata hai aur Laila ko pata chalta hai ki woh ek anaath hai. Laila ka premi Karan laila ki asli maa ko dhoondhne mai uska saath deta hai. Talaash karte hue woh dono Razia se milte hai. Razia , laila aur Karan teen ladkiyo Barkha, Raaj aur Salma ke baare mai batati hai jo ek 6 mahine ki Sitara ko Razia ke paas chchod gaye thay.Laila hi Sitara hai.Digvijay Singh Laila ko baahar nikaal deta hai.Gunde laila ka peecha krte hai par Raja Arjun usey bahca leta hai. Diwali ke jashn mai koi Sitara ki bezzati krdeta hai tabhi Barkha aake Sitara ko apna leti hai aur usey batati hai ki woh hi Sitara ki maa hai, tabhi Sitara ko pata chalta hai ki uske pita Sunil America mai hai. Raaj aur Salma Sitara se milne pahuchte hai tabhi Digvijay ke gunde un teeno ko kidnap karlete hai.Karan Barkha aur Raja Arjun ke saath waha pahuchta hai.Sunil bhi tabhi ekdum se apne pariwar ko bachane pahuch jata hai. Ek gunda Karan ko goli maarne hi wala hota hai ki khud Digvijay Singh gunde ko goli maarke ladai khatam krdeta hai aur Karan aur Laila ko aashirwad deta hai.
