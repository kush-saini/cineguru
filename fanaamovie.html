Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai humesha muskuraati-khilkhilaati Zooni ki jo dekh nahi sakti. Zooni laal-kiley pe ek dance show keliye apni saheliyo ke saath Dilli aayi hai jaha uski mulakaat hoti hai Rehan tour-guide se. Rehan Zooni ke beparwah husn pe marmith-ta hai aur Zooni ka dil bhi Rehan keliye dhadak raha hai. Dance Performance ke agle din Rehan ke bomb-blast mai maare jaane ki khabar milti hai. Zooni ki aankhon ka ilaaj hojaata hai aur ab woh kaale rang ke saath duniya ke har rang ko dekh sakti hai. Rehan ek militant organisation ke liye kaam karne wala ek mastermind hai aur usne hi Delhi mai blast karwaya tha. Rehan apne agle mission mai ek nuclear trigger karne keliye Kashmir mai hai. Rehaan Indian Army mai ghuske apni unit ko khatm karke Trigger haasil karleta hai par bachte-bachaatey huey zakhmi hojaata hai. Rehan madad maangne keliye ek ghar ka darwaaza khutt-khuttata hai aur darwaza kholti hai Zooni. Zooni ka ek beta hai jo uski aur Rehan ke pyaar ki nishaani hai. Rehan aur Zooni firse ek hojaate hai. Zooni ke abba aur unke dost Colonel Rehan ka sach jaan gaye hai aur Rehan dono ko maut ke ghaat utaar deta hai. Zooni ko Rehan ka sach pata chal jaata hai, Rehan trigger leke bhaagne ki koshish kar raha hai par Zooni desh keliye shauhar ki kurbaani dedeti hai Rehan ki jaan lekar aur ek aakhri baar apne Rehan ko gale laga rahi hai...
