Horror Anthology

Film ki कहानी


Iss kahaani mai 6 kahaaniya hai:- 1. Pehli kahaani hai Karan aur Anjali ki jo raat mai kahi jaa rahe hai aur Karan ab ek bhoot ban chuka hai aur Anjali ko iss baat ki khabar nahi hai. 2. Dusri Kahaani hai Chain-Smoker Anil ki jo Non-Smoking hotel Good Health mai aake rukaa hai aur kuch mahine baad smoking chchod kar wahi kaam karne lagta hai par kyu ?. 3. Teesri kahaani hai Dayashankar Pandey ki jo apne dost ki dukhad hatya ko yaad karke paagal hojaata hai. 4. Chauthi kahaani hai Gayatri ki jo bazaar se bahut sastey seb laai hai aur agle din paati hai uska pati aur poori duniya gayab ho chuki hai. 5. Paanchvi kahaani hai John jo bhoot banne ka mazaak karta hai aur kuch aakhri sawalo ke jawaab paa chuka hai. 6. Chchathi kahaani hai Purab ki jiski ek anokhi takaat uski zindagi ko humesha keliye badal deti hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Anil ) , Boman Irani( Hotel Owner ) , Vivek Oberoi( Amar ) , Nana Patekar( John ) , Sohail Khan( Karan ) , Antara Mali( Anjali ) , Shilpa Shetty( Gayatri ) , Rajpal Yadav( Seb wala ) , Aftab Shivdasani( Purab ) , Isha Koppikar( Abhilasha )

Honest Overview



Ye film uss Sitaarey ke jaisi hai jiski chamak ko kisi ne Notice nahi kia hai. Beautifully Shot films with chutki bhar Horror and chutki bhar Weirdness jo aapko Hairan kardegi. Ye film ek alag era ki Horror film hai jo agar aap 50 saal baad bhi dekhenge toh itni hi Dilchasp(Amusing) lagengi. Boman Irani apni quirky(Vichitra) performance ke zariye aapko amaze(ashcharyachakit) kardengey definitely. An Absolute Delight for the 90's kids. 1000% RECOMMENDED to people born after 2000's !!!
