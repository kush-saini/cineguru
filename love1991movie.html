Film ki कहानी


Prithvi ko apne sharaabi baap ki hatya karne keliye baal sudhaar graha mai daal diya jaata hai. Sudhaar graha se nikalney ke baad bhi Prithvi ka gussa shaant nahi hota. Prithvi ko Maggie naam ki ladki se pyaar hojaata hai. Maggie Prithvi ko apne maa-baap se milwati hai par Prithvi ka criminal itihaas dekh-kar Maggie ki maa Stella iss rishtey ko thukra deti hai. Prithvi aur Maggie ki zid dekh-kar Stella Prithvi ko peetne keliye gundey bhijwa deti hai par iss haatha-payi mai Maggie buri tarah zakhmi hogai hai aur apni zindagi keliye lad rahi hai. Prithvi ki duaye rang laati hai aur bhagwan Maggie ko uski zindagi lauta dete hai. Prithvi ki khushi ka koi thikaana nahi.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prithvi ) , Revathi Menon( Maggie ) , Rita Bhaduri( Stella ) , Amjad Khan( Guruji )

Honest Overview



Pyaar aur Duao mai kitni taakat hoti hai film mai ye dikhaya gaya hai. Youth ko ye film bahut pasand aaegi. Apne life-partner ke saath iss film ko dekhiye. Kaafi Simple Sweet aur Romantic movie. HIGHLY RECOMMENDED !!!
