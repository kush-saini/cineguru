Film ki कहानी


Don Quixote ek badmaash hai jo President ko agwa krke aur President ke humshakal ka istemaal karke Bhaarat ko khule aam neelaam karna chahta hai. Hero abhi Mumbai aaya hai uski mulakaat Miss India se hoti hai, dono ek khoobsurat raat bitaate hai. Don Quixote ke bete Prince ki nazar Miss India pe padhti hai par Miss India uski taraf muh uthaake bhi nahi dekhti. Miss India ko Hero ke tumor ka pata chalta hai. Quixote dhangho mai maara jaata hai. Hero aur Miss India president ko bachane pahuchtey hai. Prince unhey building se giraa deta hai par ve Jhande ka parachute banake bus ke upar land krte hai aur Prince girke mar jaata hai. Hero Miss India ko batata hai usey brain tumor 1.5 saal pehle tha par ab voh theek hai .

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Hero ) , Deepa Sahi( Miss India ) , Amrish Puri( Don Quixote ) , Jaaved Jaffrey( Prince Don ) , Anupam Kher( President ) , Kader Khan , Paresh Rawal

Honest Overview



Agar ye film aap nahi bhi dekhenge toh bhi koi dikkat nahi hai. Film ki kahaani kaafi dheeli hai . Agar fizool ka samay ho toh hi iss film ko dekhiye. Kisi se dushmani ho toh usey ye film dikhayi jaa sakti hai .
