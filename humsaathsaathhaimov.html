Hum Saath Saath Hai-1999


 8.1 / 10

Directed by Sooraj Barjatya


Family Drama


Blockbuster


Emotional RollerCoaster

Film ki कहानी


RamKishan aur Mamta ke chaar bachche hai Vivek,Prem,Vinod aur Sangeeta .kahi saal beet chuke hai aur ab Sangeeta aur Vivek dono ki shaadiya ho chuki hai bache hai toh Prem aur Vinod. Saara kaarobaar ab Vivek hi sambhaalta hai. Prem videsh se padhai puri krke aaya hai aur ussey apne pitah ke dost Pritam ji ki beti Preeti pasand aa jaati hai, Preeti ko bhi koi aitraaz nahi hai. Samjho rishta pakka ho hi gaya hai. Mamta ke kuch dost uske kaan bhardete hai ki Vivek ko business ka chairman na banaya jaye kyonki wo sautela hai. Mamta ko buddhi brahm hojaata hai aur Vivek se iss baare mai baat karti hai, Vivek muskurata hua bhagwan Ram ki tarah maano apne vanvaas ke liye chaldeta hai aur Rampur ki factory sambhaalne chala jaata hai. Prem maano Lakshman ki tarah apne bade bhai Vivek ko chaahta hai aur company sambhalne se inkaar kardeta hai. Prem shaadi tabtak nahi krega jabtak Vivek wapas aakar company ko nahi sambhaalengey. Mamta ko apni galti ka ehsaas hojaata hai aur vo apne bete se maafi maangti hai. Everything is fine now aur sab behad khush aur pehle se zyada kareeb aa chuke hai. Ab sab Saath Saath Hai !!

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Sonali Bendre( Preeti ) , Mohnish Behl( Vivek ) , Tabu( Sadhna ) , Saif Ali Khan( Vinod ) , Karisma Kapoor( Ninja ) , Reema Lagoo( Mamta ) , Alok Nath( RamKishan ) , Satish Shah( Pritam ) , Rajiv Verma( Adarsh ) , Sadashiv Amrapurkar( Dharamraj ) , Shakti Kapoor( Anwar )

Honest Overview



Aankhon mai aansoo , Chehre pe muskaan aur ek ajeeb si khushi mehsoos hoti hai iss film ko dekh kar. Emotions ki tsunami hai ye film. Please apne purey pariwar ke saath baithke dekhiye. Ye film hume batati hai - Rishto ki ahamiyat aur Pyaar waqt se bhi zyada balwaan hota hai. Film bohot hi sweet aur heart-touching hai. Must Watch Before you Die !!!!
