Film ki कहानी


Radhe bada hi seedha-saadha Gunda hai jo bas burey logo ki haddiyan todna hi pasand karta hai. Radhe Jahnvi naam ki ladki ko pasand karta hai par Jahnvi ussey awara samajhti hai par Radhe ka bhala-dil dekh-kar ussey chaahne lagti hai. Radhe ek tharki policewale Talpade ko bhi sabak sikhaata hai jiski Jahnvi pe gandi nazar hai. Commissioner Ashraf ne Don Ghani Bhai ko jail mai daaldiya hai par apni beti ko zinda dekhne keliye Ashraf Ghani ko jail se nikaal deta hai. Ghani Bhai bahar nikalte hi Radhe ke dost Ajay aur Ajay ke pitaah Shrikant ko maar-deta hai. Radhe ko baadme pata chalta hai ki wo uska asli bhai aur saga baap tha jo Ghani ke haatho maare gaye. Radhe Ashraf ki beti chudakar le-aata hai aur Talpade aur Ghani ko bhagwan ke paas pohochaa-deta hai ek-ek goli khilaakar...
