How A Girl's life went Upside Down

Film ki कहानी


Sarika bahut bholi hai aur apne boyfriend Karan ko bachane keliye jail chali jaati hai 7 saal keliye. Karan ek terrorist hai jisne Sarika ko fasaya hai apne operation keliye. Sarika jail mai apni maasumiyat khokar Bekhauf banjaati hai aur ab Karan se badla lena hi uski zindagi ka maqsad ban chuka hai. Sarika jail mai apni dost Pramila ki madad se bhaag nikalne mai kaamyab hojaati hai. Sarika Karan ke sabhi dosto ko maut ke ghaat utaardeti hai aur Karan ki zindagi mai wapas laut-ti hai. Karan ka Sarika pe shuq pakka ho chuka hai. Maut se ek nazdeeki mulaqaat mai Sarika aur Karan bach jaatey hai par uske baad Sarika bandook ki nok par Karan ko sunsaan jagah lejaakar ek bhayanak maut maarti hai. Sarika wapas jail laut chuki hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Urmila Matondkar( Sarika ) , Saif Ali Khan( Karan ) , Aditya Shrivastav( Kamlesh ) , Seema Biswas( Malti ) , Zakir Hussain( Sanjiv ) , Pratima Kazmi( Pramila ) , Rasika Joshi( Gomti ) , Sheebha Chaddha( Anita )

Honest Overview



Darri hui Sarika se lekar apne shikaar ki ghaath lagae baithe Kisi Khoonkhaar Magarmachch ke jaisi Sarika se nazre hataana UPSC clear karne se zyada mushkil hai. Sarika ki aankho se darr ka udhtaa hua rang Urmila ji ne bakhoobhi dikhaya hai. Dil ki dhadhkan ko badhaa-dey(HEART-POUNDING) aisa ACTION aur kisi Kamzor ko Khatarnak bantey dekhne ki Universal Appeal film ko DHAMAAKEDAAR banadeti hai. A MUST WATCH BEFORE YOU MEET GOD. 1000% VERY Very Strongly RECOMMENDED for GEn-next jo 90's mai har jawaan dhadaktey dil ki dhadhkan Urmila Matondkar ji ke kaam se anjaan hai !!!
