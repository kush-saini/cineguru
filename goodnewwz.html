Directed by Raj Mehta


Drama Comedy


Monstrous Box Office


Edifies about IVF

Film ki कहानी


Varun Batra ek Businessman hai aur apni Biwi Deepti ke saath behad khush hai. Bas ek ghum hai dono ki zindagi mai ek bachche ki kami aur uss kami ko khatam karne keliye dono IVF ka sahaara letey hai. Honey Batra aur unki biwi Monika bhi issi pareshaani se jhoonjh rahe hai aur same IVF hospital mai aa pahuchey hai. Hospital ki ek bhool ki wajah se dono Batra's ki zindagi ek doosre ke saath aise ulajh jaati hai ki 9 mahiney tak alag nahi ho paati. Aisa kyu hua ye jaan-ne keliye film dekhein...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Varun ) , Kareena Kapoor Khan( Deepti ) , Diljit Dosanjh( Honey ) , Kiara Advani( Monika ) , Adil Hussain( Anand ) , Tisca Chopra( Sanya ) , Mohit Raina( Cameo ) , Anjana Sukhani( Richa )

Honest Overview



Ek kilkaari sunne keliye tarastey huey har Pati-Patni ki bebasi ko film mai bakhoobhi dikhaaya gaya hai. Maa-Baap ban paaney ki Asmanjas ko Hasee-Thaahako ki Band-Aid se cover kiya gaya hai iss film mai Beautifully. A Yaadgaar Light-Hearted Comedy after a long time in Hindi Cinema. Warning- Gharwalo ke saath thoda awkward ho sakta hai dekhna but please ye Movie sabko dikhaein. 1000% STRONGLY RECOMMENDED to Rural Population especially !!!
