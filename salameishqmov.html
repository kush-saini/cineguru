Film ki कहानी


Yeh 6 kahaaniya ek-doosre se judi hui hai- 1. Pehli kahaani hai Kamna ki jo Bollywood mai bade roles karna chahti unlike the item songs jo woh abhi karti hai. Kamna ka sapna hai Karan Johar ki film mai kaam karne ka par Rahul ke pyaar keliye wo Film karne ka mauka cchchod deti hai.2. Doosri Kahaani hai Jia aur Shiven ki. Shiven shaadi ke naam se darr gya aur sab chchod ke jaane ka khayal uske mann mai hai par Jia ka pyaar ussey wapas leaata hai Jia ki baaho mai. 3. Teesri kahaani hai Vinay ki jo apni biwi Seema se pyaar karta hai par Anjali ki jawani aur husn pe mar-mitaa hai. Vinay haath-paer jodke apni biwi ko mana hee leta hai. 4. Chouthi kahaani hai Ashutosh aur Tehzeeb ki. Tehzeeb ek haadse mai apni yaad-daasht kho deti hai par Ashutosh ka pyaar aur sabr unke rishtey ko pehle ki tarah pyaar se bhardeta hai. 5. Paanchvi kahaani hai Taxi Driver Raju ki jo ek gori mem Stephanie ko uske aashiq se milwaane ke jugaad mai laga hai aur pyaar kar baitha hai ussi gori-mem se. Woh ladka dhokebaaz nikalta hai aur Raju ko apni Rani milgayi hai. 6. Chchati kahaani hai shaadi-shudaa jodey Ram aur Phoolvati ki jinka milan har-baar ek mazaak ban kar reh-jaata hai par ek din Bhagwan bhi unko green signal dedeta hai...
