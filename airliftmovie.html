Ode to Indian Government

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Kuwait mai rehne wale Indian Businessman Ranjit Katiyal ki jo apni biwi aur beti ke saath ek khush-haal zindagi bitaah rahe hai. Kuch dino Bad Iraqi Sena Kuwait pe hamla kardeti hai aur Sadko pe maar-kaat machi hui hai. Ranjit apne saath Kuwait mai fasey sabhi bhartiyo keliye Indian Embassy se madad maangta hai par niraasha hi uske haath lagti hai lekin Air India Ummeed ki Kiran banke sabhi Bhartiyo ko surakshit India paucha deti hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Ranjit ) , Nimrat Kaur( Amrita ) , Inaamulhaq( Major Khalaf ) , Kumud Mishra( Sanjeev ) , Purab Kohli( Ibrahim ) , Avtar Gill( Amrit )

Honest Overview



Burey Waqt mai Insaan ke kirdaar(Character) ki mazbooti aur Dil dehla de aisi darindgi bhi aapko iss film mai dekhne ko milegi. Ye film Indian Government ko Bollywood ki taraf se Tribute hai. Just Like Wine Akshay umr ke saath Better ho chukey hai aur ye film iss baat ka Proof hai. Shining Performance by Kumud Mishra ji. Popcorn ke Bowl ko sirhaaney rakh ke iss film ka lutf uthaaiye. 1000% HIGHLY RECOMMENDED to every True Desh-Bhakt !!!
