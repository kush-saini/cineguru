Yeh Hai Jalwa-2002


 5.8 / 10

Directed by David Dhawan


Comedy Romance


Box Office Flop


Music by Himesh Reshamiya

Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Raju ki jo ek kaamyab businessman hai. Raju ne apni maa ko bachpan mai khodiya tha aur uska baap bhi unke pariwaar ko chodke chala gaya tha. Ek din apne baap ko news mai dekh-kar uski khushi ka thikaana nahi. Raju apne pitah se milne London chala jaata hai. Raju Mittal ki mulaakat finally Pitah-Shri Rajesh Mittal se ho hi jaati hai. Rajesh Raju ki saari baatey jhutlaa deta hai. Raju apne papa ko 7 din ka waqt deta hai warna Aathve din wo khud Rajesh ke pariwar ko apne baap ka guzra hua kal yaani apni vajood ki sachchai bata-dega. Rajesh apne lawyer dost ki madad se Raju ko jail mai dalwa hi deta hai ki tabhi Purshottam Mittal yaani Rajesh ke pitah Raju ko chchudva detey hai aur apne ghar bulatey hai. Jis parivar se ussey nafrat thi ,in kuch dino mai Raju ko ye sab log apne se lagne lagtey hai. Rajesh ka accident hogya hai aur ussey kidney ki zarurat hai ,Raju gumnaami mai kidney donate krdeta hai. Agle din Rajesh khud hi Raju ke baarey mai apne pariwar ko sab sach bata deta hai. Sab Raju ko apna-letey hai aur Raju ab kabhi anaath nahi kehlaaega.

Film ke कलाकार

Rishi Kapoor( Rajesh ) , Salman Khan( Raju ) , Amisha Patel( Sonia ) , Rati Agnihotri( Rajesh'patni ) , Shammi Kapoor( Boss ) , Anupam Kher( Robin ) , Kader Khan( Raju's Daadaji ) , Sharad Kapoor( Vicky ) , Kiran Kumar , Jaspal Bhatti

Honest Overview



Film ke gaane "Chunari Khankayi Re" aur "O Jaane Jigar" Out of this world hai. Baap apne khoye huye bachche ko dhoondta hai ye suna hoga par iss film mai ulta hai Beta apne khoye huye baap ko dhoondhne nikla hai. Rishi Kapoor aur Salman ki judalbandi kaafi mazedaar lagegi. Anupam Kher aur Kader Khan ne dumdaar abhinay kiya hai. EK Surprise Cameo bhi hai film mai. One of the best films in David Dhawan Comedy Universe !!!
