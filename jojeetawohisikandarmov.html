BoxOffice Hit


Coming-Of-Age Story

Film ki कहानी


"Ramlal" Model School mai Coach hai aur uske do betey hai Ratan aur Sanju , dono bilkul hi alag-thalag hai. Ratan aagyakaari zimmedaar aur Sports mai no.1 apne pitah ka favourite beta hai. Wahi doosri taraf Sanju yaaro ke saath samay ko barbaad karta hai aur fail hota rehta hai jisse Ramlal bahut pareshan hai. Ramlal ka sapna hai ki Ratan Inter-School Sports Competetion mai medal laye. Competition ke din kada mukaabla hota hai par Ratan bas ek position se Rajput College ke Shekhar se haar jaata hai. Sanju Queen's College ki Devika ko impress karne keliye apne pitah ke mehnat ke paise uspe udaa-deta hai. Devika ko Sanju ka sach pata chalta hai toh voh Shekhar ke saath apnaati hai Sanju ko chchod-kar. Shekhar aur Ratan ki ladai hojaati hai jisme Ratan buri tarah se zakhmi hojaata hai aur agle Competetion mai hissa nahi le paayega. Sanju Ratan ke liye Competetion mai hissa lekar Shekhar ko haraane ka nischaiy kar chuka hai aur Competetion ki tayaari kar raha hai. Kuch seconds ke fark se Sanju competetion jeet-jaata hai. Ratan aur Ramlal ko Sanju pe garv hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Sanju ) , Ayesha Jhulka( Anjali ) , Deepak Tijori( Shekhar ) , Mamik Singh( Ratan ) , Pooja Bedi( Devika ) , Kulbushan Kharbanda( Ramlal ) , Asrani( Dubey ) , Ajit Vakhani( Principal ) , Anjan Shrivastav( Commentator )

Honest Overview



"Jawaani ki bevkoofiyan aur Zimmedaari ka sabak" iss film mai dikahaya gaya hai. "Apni Mehnat se dia hua jawab hi sabse sahi jawab hota hai" ye film humhe sikhaati hai. Hum sabne apne andar kabhi na kabhi Sanju ke kirdaar ko zaroor jiya hoga. WITHOUT ANY DOUBT - A MUST WATCH BEFORE YOU DIE !!!
