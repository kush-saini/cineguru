Film Poster

Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam-1999


 7.9 / 10

Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali


Drama Romance


Box Office Hit


Shot in Gujarat & Rajasthan

Film ki कहानी


Sameer garmi mai thandi hawa ke jhoke ki tarah aaya hai aur purey Darbaar pariwar ka chaheeta hogya hai. Sameer ki gayaki ke sab hi kayal hai. Darbar pariwar ke sabse bade Pundit Darbar ki beti Nandini Sameer ki achchi dost banjaati hai. Dheere-Dheere ye dosti ek maasum pyaar ka roop leleti hai, Iss pyaar ka gala uss din ghut gaya jis din Pundit ne Nandini aur Sameer ko ek-saath dekha tha. Nandini ki shaadi Vanraj ke saath taha ho gayi hai aur Sameer ko Nandini se kabhi na milne ka aadesh diya gaya hai. Sameer vapas Italy laut jaata hai. Vanraj mai ek achcha pati banne ke saare gun hai par ussey iss baat ka andaaza hai ki Nandini kisi aur se pyaar karti hai. Vanraj Nandini ko uske aashiq Sameer ke paas lejaata hai. Italy mai Nandini ke saath ek haadsa hojaata hai jisme Vanraj Nandini ka bohot khyaal rakhta hai. Nandini Sameer se milti hai par ussey ab apne pati ki mahaanta aur bade dil se pyaar hojaata hai. Vanraj ko uska pyaar aur Nandini ko apna jeevansaathi mil jaata hai. Nandini-Vanraj behad khush hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Sameer ) , Aishwarya Rai( Nandini ) , Ajay Devgn( Vanraj ) , Zohra Sehgal( Daadi ) , Vikram Gokhale( Pandit ) , Smita Jaykar( Amrita ) , Sheeba Chaddha( Anupama ) , Vinay Pathak( Tarun ) , Helen

Honest Overview



Ye film kisi khoobsurat Italian cuisine jaisi hai ki bas dekhte hi jao. Kisi movie ko sundar(Spectacular) aur bhavya(Grand) kaise dikhaya jaata hai aap ye Sanjay Leela Bhansali ji se seekh sakte hai, Hats off sir. Salman bohot charming lag rahe hai aur Nandini ki chanchal adaa se aapko pyaar hojaega. Iss film mai dikhaya gaya hai ki pyaar mai kuch gawaya ya haasil nahi kiya jaata. Recommended very highly for lovers of Cinema !!!
