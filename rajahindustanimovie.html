An Unusual Love Story

Film ki कहानी


Aarti apne pitah aur sauteli maa ke saath rehti hai ek aalishan ghar mai. Sauteli maa Aarti ki jaeydaad hadapna chaahti hai apne bhai ke saath milke. Aarti chuttiyon mai Palankhet ghumne jaana chahti hai kyonki Palankhet uski asli maa ki nishaani hai. Palankhet pahuchtey hi Aarti taxi karti hai aur driver Raja Hindustani ussey ghuma raha hai aur uska mann behla raha hai. Raja ko iss memsaab se pehli nazar mai hi pyaar hogya hai. Raja ke chachi-chacha Aarti ko apne ghar mai rehne ka nyota dete hai aur chacha-chachi ke saath rehke Aarti bhi behad khush hai. Ek tezz barsaat Raja aur Aarti ko kareeb leaati hai aur pyaar ke bandhan mai baandh deti hai. Raja Aarti se shaadi karleta hai aur uske kehne pe sheher jaake Aarti ki duniya ka hissa banne ki koshish karta hai par ek galatfaimi ke chalte Aarti se bhari mehfil ke saamne jhagadke wapas Palankhet aajaata hai. Raja ko khabar milti hai ki Rani maa ban chuki hai. Apne bachche ko dekhne keliye betaab Raja bachche ko kidnap kar Palankhet leaata hai apne daada-daadi ke paas. Aarti Palankhet pahuch jaati hai. Raja-Aarti ek-dujhe ko phir se paa letey hai...
