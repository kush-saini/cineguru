Dil Se-1998


 8.6 / 10

Directed by Mani Ratnam


Thriller Romance


Box-Office Hit


2 National Film Awards

Film ki कहानी


Amar Varma All India Radio mai kaam krta hai aur kaam ke silsiley mai Delhi se Assam Chala jaata hai. Kaam se break leke Amar cigarette peene keliye bahar nikalta hai aur Amar ki nazar ek khoobsurat ladki pe padti hai Jo agli train Mai teen ladko ke saath chali jaati hai. Amar asaami logo Ka interview lekar radio par uss ladki se mulaqat ke baare mai bata raha hota hai ki kuch dino baad Amar vohi ladki se baat karne mai kaamyab hojata hai aur pata lagata hai ki ladki Ka naam Meghna hai. Meghna ko Amar ki madad se naukri mil jaati hai aur vo Amar ke Ghar hi raha rahi hai. Amar ko Meghna pe shak hone lagta hai aur vo Meghna se sach ugalwata hai . Meghna Ka asli naam Moina hai aur vo army ki rape victim hai, Moina Liberation group Ka hissa hai aur Independence day pe army aur president par suicide attack Karne wali hai. CBI shak ke chalte Amar ko puchtaach keliye pakad leti hai. Amar Moina ko rokne keliye waha se bhaag nikalta hai . Moina se milke Amar Shaadi krne ki baat krta hai aur Moina ko suicide krne se rokna chahta hai. Amar zid pe ada hai par Moina apna badla leke hi marna chaahti hai. Amar Moina ko gale laga leta hai. Moina ke sharir se laga bomb phat jaata hai aur dono pyaar mai apni jaan dedetey hai.

Honest Overview



Sab kuch chhodo aur jaake ye movie dekho. Mani-Ratnam ek genius hai . Film har maainey mai perfect hai. Mazedaar picture aur A.R rehman ki awaaz ka jaadoo , Definitely recommended !!
