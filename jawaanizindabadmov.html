Film ki कहानी


Shashi ladkiyon ke maamle mai sharmila hai aur Dahej pratha ke sakht khilaaf bhi hai. Shashi Sugandha ke dahej ke bhooke samdhiyo se uski shaadi tudwaa-deta hai aur Sugandha ke pitah Gopinath se Sugandha ka haath maangta hai lekin Sugandha aadmi ki jaath se hi nafrat karne lagi hai aur prastaav thukraa deti hai. Pyaar Shashi ko besharam bana hi deta hai aur woh Sugandha ko apne pyaar ka ehsaas dilaane mai kaamyab hojaata hai. Shashi aur Sugandha ki shaadi hojaati hai jissey Shashi ki maa dukhi hai kyonki shaadi mai koi dahej nahi aaya hai. Shashi tigdam bhidaake apni behen Rama ki shaadi uske premi Ravi se karwadeta hai par Ravi ke maa-baap dahej liye bina Rama ko bahu ka darja nahi detey aur Ravi-Rama ko ek doosre ke nazdeek nahi aane detey. (Goverdhan)Ravi ka baap Garbhvati hochuki Rama ko kitchen mai jalaake maarne ki saazish rachta hai par galti se Ravi ki maa uss haadhse ka shikaar hojaati hai. Goverdhan Shashi ko maarne keliye gundey bhejtey hai jo Ravi ko hi zakhmi kardetey hai. Ussi hospital mai Rama ko ladki hui hai. Ravi aur Ravi ki maa ka ye hashr dekh-kar Goverdhan sabke saamne apni galtiyon ka praischith kartey huey apne khandaan mai dahej kabhi naa leney ki kasam khaata hai...
