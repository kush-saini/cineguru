Ra.One-2011


 6.6 / 10

Directed by Anubhav Sinha


Action SuperHero


Box Office Hit


Shahrukh in Superhero avatar

Film ki कहानी


Shekhar London mai apni biwi Sonia aur bete Prateek ke saath rehta hai aur video games banata hai. Shekhar Prateek ke liye ek aisa game banata hai jisme Villain Ra.One Hero G.One se zyaada taakatvar hai. Takneeki gadbadi ki wajah se Ra.One zinda hojaata hai aur asli duniya mai aajata hai. Ra.One Shekhar ko maar deta hai. Sonia Prateek ke saath wapas India chali jaati hai. Shekhar ke dost G.One ko zinda kardete hai. G.One Prateek aur Sonia ki raksha kar raha hai. Ra.One G.One ko maarne India pahuchta hai. Prateek apne pitah Shekhar ki ek baat yaad karke Ra.One ko haraane ka tarika nikaal leta hai jiski madad se G.One Ra.One ko humesha keliye khatam kardeta hai. Sonia aur Prateek London laut jaatey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Shekhar / G.one ) , Kareena Kapoor Khan( Sonia ) , Arjun Rampal( Ra. One ) , Priyanka Chopra Jonas( Cameo ) , Sanjay Dutt( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Film ke action scenes aur Visual Effects top-notch hai. Bachcho ko ye movie zaroor pasand aaegi. One Time Watch and a great timepass movie !!!
