The Lunchbox-2013


 8.5 / 10

Directed by Ritesh Batra


Slice-of-Life Romance


Mammoth Box-Office


An Entertaining Melancholy

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Saajan Fernandez ki jo bas apne retirment ka intezaar kar rahe hai. Office ke aakhri dino mai unke Tiffin ki adhla-badhli hojaati hai jiske zariye unki dosti Ila se hojaati hai jiska dabba galti se Saajan ke paas pahuch raha hai. Ila apni shaadi se khush nahi hai aur Saajan bhi apni biwi ke jaane ke baad tanhaa ho gaya hai. Ila aur Saajan ek doosre se milne ke yakeen ke saath nikal padein hai iss ummeed mai ki ek din unke Raaste milengey...

Film ke कलाकार

Irrfan Khan( Saajan ) , Nawazuddin Siddiqui( Aslam ) , Nimrat Kaur( Ila ) , Denzil Smith( Saajan's Boss ) , Lilette Dubey( Ila's maa ) , Shruti Bapna( Mehrunisa )

Honest Overview



Jaise "JEERE ki Tadke se Daal mai Zaayka aata hai waise hi Comedy mai jab tak INNOCENCE(Bholapan) na ho Hasee-Thahaako(Laughter) nahi aata- " Iss film mai Daal ki SAADGI(Simplicity) aur Thehraav bhi hai aur aapke Mann ko Dheeme-se(Mildly) Tickle(Gudhgudhi) karne wali Zindagi ki UNCERtainity(Aspasht-tah) bhi hai. VERDICT- Iss film ki Cast keliye "ACTING is Rice-Plate Eating". 1000% Very Strongly RECOMMENDED to Every Genuine Cinema Lover !!!
