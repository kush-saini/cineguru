Film ki कहानी


PK ek alien hai aur Dharti pe aake apni Spceship ko bulaane ka Remote kho chuka hai. PK iss anjaan duniya ke taur-tareeke seekh raha hai. Log ussey kehte Tunhe pee rakhi hai kya ? aur logo ke issi sawal se uska naam PK ban gaya. PK ko bhagwan ki daan-peti se paise churaane keliye jail mai daal diya jaata hai. Reporter Jaggu ko PK bahut fascinating lagta hai aur wo PK ko lekar ek show banati hai jisme PK Logo ki Shradha aur Aastha ka faida uthaane walo pe sawal uthaata hai. Jaggu ki madad se PK ko apna remote Tapasvi Maharaj se wapas mil jaata hai. Pk Jaggu ko apni shaktiyo ke zariye uske aashiq Sarfaraz se milwa-deta hai jissey galat-faimi ke chalte Jaggu ki shaadi toot gayi thi. PK Jaggu se pyaar karne lagta hai par ussey kabhi nahi bata paega . PK space-ship mai baith kar wapas chala jaata hai. Jaggu PK pe ek kitaab likhti hai. PK kuch saathiyo ke saath dharti pe wapas aaya hai. TO BE CONTINUED...
