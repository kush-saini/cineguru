Hello-2008


 4.6 / 10

Directed by Atul Agnihotri


Supernatural Drama


Flop at Box Office


Based on novel One Night @ the Call Center

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ek raat ki jisne Call center mai kaam karne wale 6 logo Shyam,Varun,Radhika,Priyanka, Colonel aur Esha ki zindagi ko badal diya hai. Lekin ussey pehle peeche chalte hai- Shyam aur Varun ek badi website pe kaam rahe hai, Radhika ko uske pati ne dhoka diya hai, Colonel ke apne betey ne ussey nakaar diya hai, Priyanka ki maa uski shaadi kisi NRI se karana chaahti hai par wo Shyam ko chaahti hai. Sabka boss Bakshi kuch logo ko naukri se nikaalne wala hai jisse pareshan hokar sab ek drink keliye bahar jaatey hai aur tootey huey pool mai fasjaatey aur gaadi zara bhi hili toh maut pakki. Unhe God ka Call aata hai jo unhe khud pe barosa aur bure waqt ne na darne ki salha detey hai. Kuch hi der mai madad aa jaati hai aur sab bach jaatey hai. Sab khud pe bharosa karke apni zindagi badal detey hai. Shyam aur Varun apni company khol-letey hai, Esha ek NGO mai hai, Radhika apne pati ko chchod chuki hai aur ab khush hai, Priyanka aur SHyam ki shaadi hojaati hai, Colonel apne potey se milne videsh chale jaate hai...

Honest Overview



Yeh film aam aadmi ki zindagi ki takleefo ko darshaati hai aur LIFE ka sabse important message bhi dekar jaati hai. YE film especially Youth ke liye hai. 1000% recommended to anyone who has lost confidence in Life, This movie would really help and don't forget to read the novel too!!!
