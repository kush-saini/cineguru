A Different Love Story

Film ki कहानी


Inspector Siddharth apni biwi Sonia se alag ho chuka hai aur kaafi chidhchidha ho gaya hai uska dost James bhi iss baat se pareshan hai. Siddharth raat mai ek akeli ladki Anna ki madad karta hai. Anna Siddharth ko apni taraf aakarshit karleti hai. Siddharth Drug Raid ke paise Anna ko dedeta hai uske Cancer ke ilaaj keliye. Siddharth Anna ke kehne par ussey akele milne chala jaata hai. Waha pahuchke ussey Anna fraud hai ye pata chalta hai, Anna ne Siddharth par bandook taan rakhi hai ki tabhi Sonia waha pahuchkar Anna ko goli maardeti hai. Siddharth aur Sonia ko apne pyaar ka ehsaas phir se hojaata hai aur dono apne rishtey ko ek nayi shuruat detey hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Siddharth ) , Shamita Shetty( Sonia ) , Udita Shetty( Anna ) , Ninad Kamat( James ) , Sameer Kochhar( Sean )

Honest Overview



Agar abhi BREAK-UP hua hai toh film ke Songs dil ke ghaav pe "BAND-AID" ka kaam karengey. Film aapko Zeherning deti hai "Apsara ke peeche naa bhaagne ki kyonki vo Vishkanya bhi ho sakti hai". Movie ka Screenplay Kachchuey(Tortoise) ki tarah Slow hai par Entertainment ki race mai film ko jitah-deta hai. Ye film har Male ke Fantasy World mai Open Door ke jaisi hai. 1000% RECOMMENDED to Adolescents !!!
