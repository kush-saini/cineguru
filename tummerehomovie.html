Film ki कहानी


Shiva bachpan mai hi apne maa-baap se bichad gaya tha par ek Sapere Baba ne ussey apna bachcha samajh ke pala aur Tantra-Vidya bhi sikhaai. Shiva ab bada ho chuka hai aur Paro se prem karne laga hai. Paro ke pitah iss rishtey ko apni manzoori nahi detey aur Shiva ko pitwaane keliye gundey ko bhej-detey hai par SHiva unhe peet deta hai. SHiva ko naagin dass leti hai par Tantrik ke jaadu ke aage kamzor padkar Naagin khoon wapas choosleti hai. Shiva firse zinda hojaata hai aur gao ke riti-riwaz ke mutaabik Paro se shaadi karleta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Shiva ) , Juhi Chawla( Paro ) , Ajit Vachani( Paro's papa ) , Sudhir Pandey( Thakur ) , Kalpana Iyer( Naagin )

Honest Overview



Ye bas ek Movie nahi hai Balki Ojha-baba ya Tantrik banne ka Crash-Course hai. Agar aapko in fields mai excel karna hai toh zaroor iss film ko dekhiye. The Movie is Actually strangely interesting in its sheer Stupidity. Agar kisi ko sazaa deni ho toh ussey ye film dikhaai jaa sakti hai kamre mai bandh karke. MERELY A ONE TIME WATCH FOR AAMIR-JUHI FANS !!!
