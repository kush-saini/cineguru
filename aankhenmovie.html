Aankhen-2002


 7.0 / 10

Directed by Vipul Shah


Robbery Thriller


Box Office Hit


Exceptional Story

Film ki कहानी


Vilasrao Jefferson Bank ke sabse mehnati employee Vijay Singh Rajput ko apne Hinsak swabhav ke liye naukri se nikaal diya jaata hai. Vijay apni beizzati ka badla lene keliye Vilasrao Jefferson Bank mai Robbery ka plan karta hai. Vijay andho ki trainer Neha Shrivastav ke bhai ko kidnap karke ussey apne plan ki trainer banata hai taaki woh purey Desh se chuney gaye teen andho - Arjun, Vishwas aur Ilihaas se Robbery karwa sakein. Robbery kaamyab hojaati hai aur Arjun Vishwas Ilihaas ko jewelry chupaane keliye bhejdetey hai. Rajput Ilihaas ko maardeta hai tabhi Neha Rajput ke plan ko barbaad karne keliye apni jaan leleti hai. Arjun aur Vishwas awaaz sunke Rajput se ladd jaatey hai. Tabhi Police waha pahuch jaati hai aur Arjun Vishwas apne andhepan ki wajah se Police ki nazro mai nirdosh saabit hojaatey hai. Shak ke pinaah pe Rajput ko police pakad leti hai. Arjun-Vishwas ko Rahul aur Chupayi hui jewelry mil jaati hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Amitabh Bachchan( Vijay Singh Rajput ) , Akshay Kumar( Vishwas ) , Arjun Rampal( Arjun ) , Paresh Rawal( Ilihaas ) , Sushmita Sen( Neha ) , Paresh Ganatra( Sailesh ) , Kashmera Shah( Cameo ) , Bipasha Basu( Cameo ) , Aditya Pancholi( ACP Thakur )

Honest Overview



Film ka Background Score behad ENTRANCING hai. Vijay Singh Rajput ka Obsession kaafi INTENSE hai aur aapko Screen ke Chumbak ki tarah Chipkaa kar rakhne mai kaamyab hota hai. Paresh aapko hasaaengey bhi aur rulaaengey bhi behetreen dialogues ke zariye. Dialogue writer ek STANDING OVATION ke hakdaar hai. 1000% VERY STRONGLY RECOMMENDED unko jinhe Adhbut kahaaniyan pasand hai !!!
