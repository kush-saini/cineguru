Yuvraaj-2008


 4.3 / 10

Directed by Subash Ghai


Musical Drama


Flop at Box Office


Shot at beautiful locations

Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Deven ki jo apni Premika Anushka se shaadi karna chaahta hai par wo ek naakaamyab gayak hai isliye Anushka ke papa Dr. Banton ussey pasand nahi karte. Dr Banton ussey kuch dino ki mauhlat detey hai ameer banne keliye, Agar wo ye kar paya toh Anushka ke saath uski shaadi pakki. Deven ke pitah guzar jaatey hai aur saari daulat Gyanesh ke naam kar jaatey hai. Gyanesh Deven aur Danny ka bhai hai aur specially challenged hai. Deven aur Danny dono hi Gyanesh ko behla fusla ke uski daulat hadapna chaahte hai. Gyanesh ek behetreen gifted singer hai aur humesha khush rehta hai. Anushka Gyanesh ko gaaney ka mauka deti hai aur wo kamaal ki performance deta hai. Deven aur Danny apne bhai se sachcha pyaar karne lagtey hai aur unki sauteli maa ke burey iraado se Gyanesh ko bachaatey hai. Gyanesh ko urgent ilaaj ki zarurat pad jaati hai par Dr Banton ek hi shart par ussey bachaaenge agar Deven Anushka se shaadi na karey , Deven maanjaata hai. Gyanesh ka ilaaj hojaata hai aur Dr Banton Deven se impress hokar Anushka aur uske rishtey keliye raazi hojaate hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Deven ) , Boman Irani( Anushka's Dad ) , Zayed Khan( Danny ) , Anil Kapoor( Gyanesh ) , Katrina Kaif( Anushka ) , Mithun( Mirza ) , Anjan Shrivastav( Mamaji )

Honest Overview



Aksar ek machli purey talaab ko ganda karti hai par yah ulta hai kyonki iss talaab ko ek hi machli ne ganda hone se bachaya hai aur wo machli hai Anil Kapoor ji .Iss dull aur boring film mai Anil Kapoor hi hai jinke scenes dekhne mai mazaa aata hai aur foreign ki dilkash locations. Recommended for Anil Kapoor fans!!!
