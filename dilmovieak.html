Film ki कहानी


Raja apne pitah ke paise paani ki tarah bahaata hai jissey Pita SHri Hazari Prasad pareshan aa chukey hai. Hazari Prasad motey taazey dahej ke liye ek Raheez Mehra Seth ko fasaleta hai ye bolkar ki woh Beshumar daulat ka maalik hai. Raja pehli mulakat mai hi Madhu se nafrat karne lagta hai. Madhu aur Raja ek dusre ko mushkilo mai daalne ke plan banaatey rehtey hai par ek din hadh hojaati hai jab Madhu Raja pe balaatkar ka ilzaam lagaati hai, Raja ko College se nikaal dia jaata hai par Raja Madhu ko daraa ke chchod-deta hai. Madhu apne ilzaam wapas leti hai aur uss din se Raja se pyaar karne lagti hai aur Raja ke mann mai bhi ghantiya baj rahi hai. Raja aur Madhu ki sagaai ke din Hazari Prasad ki sachchai saamne aa jaati hai aur rishta toot jaata hai. Raja aur Madhu poori duniya se ladke apne pyaar ko mukammal karne mai kaamayab hojaatey hai...
