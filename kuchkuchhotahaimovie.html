Kuch Kuch Hota Hai-1998


 7.9 / 10

Directed by Karan Johar


Drama Romance


All Time BlockBuster


Karan Johar's 1st movie as director

Film ki कहानी


Rahul Khanna aur Anjali Sharma ki dosti ke charche poorey college mai mashoor hai. Anjali mann hi mann Rahul se pyaar karti hai. Tabhi college mai ek nayi ladki Tina ki entry hoti hai , Rahul Tina ko chaahne lagta hai aur Tina bhi Rahul ko pasand krne lagti hai. Anjali college chhodke Rahul se bahut dur chali jaati hai.Rahul aur Tina ki shaadi hojati hai, shaadi ko kahi saal guzar chuke hai . Tina ab iss duniya mai nahi hai aur Rahul-Tina ki ek beti hai jiska naam vo Anjali rakhte hai. Anjali Khanna aksar apni maa Tina ki likhi chitthiyo se baatein karke apni maa ko yaad karti hai.Chitthiyo ki madad se Anjali khanna dusri Anjali ke baarey mai jaan-jaati hai aur apne papa aur Anjali sharma ko milaaney ka faisla karti hai. Anjali khanna us hee summer camp mai jaati hai jaha Anjali Sharma padhaati hai , Rahul apni beti se milne camp pahuchta hai aur apni dost Anjali Sharma ko waha paata hai , Dosti pyaar ka roop lene lagti hai. Anjali sharma ki sagai Aman se teh ho chuki hai, Sagai se pehle Rahul Anjali ko apne dil ki baat bataata hai, Anjali asmanjas mai hai. Aman yeh jaanta hai ki Rahul hee Anjali ka sachcha pyaar hai . Aman apni sagai todke Anjali aur Rahul ki shaadi karwadeta hai. Anjali ko apna khoya hua pyaar mil jaata hai

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Overview



Bichda hua pyaar wapas miljaane ki khushi aur pyar ko khone ka dukh iss film mai Karan Johar ne bohot khoobsurati se dikhaaya hai. Emotional aur youthful gaaney , khoobsurat locations . Shahrukh-Kajol-Rani ke fans zaroor dekhe !!
