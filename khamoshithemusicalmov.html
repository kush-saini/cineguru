Khamoshi-1996


7.8  / 10

Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali


Drama Musical Romance


Decent Box Office


Heart-Touching story

Film ki कहानी


Joseph aur uski biwi Flavvy dono hi goongey-beherey hai par bahut khush rehtey hai, Unki khushi mai izaafaa karne unke ghar janam leti hai unki pyaari beti Annie. Annie sun or bol dono sakti hai ye jaankar Joseph khushi se naach uth-ta hai. Annie ke gaaney ka shawk uske bhai ki zindagi ke saath khatam ho jaata hai. Kahi saal beet chukey hai aur Annie ab sayaani ho chuki hai aur Raj naam ke ladke ne uskey dil pe dastak di hai. Annie Raj ke pariwar ko apne pariwar se milwati hai. Joseph iss rishtey ko nahi apnaata hai. Annie bina shaadi ke maa banne waali hai , Joseph Annie ko ghar se nikaal deta hai. Annie aur Raj shaadi karlete hai par Joseph aur Flavvy waha maujood nahi hotey. Annie ko ek ladka hota hai. Jab Joseph apne potey ko dekhta hai toh beeti hui sab baatey bhool jaata hai aur Raj ko apne pariwar ka hissa maanleta hai. Annie ka accident hojaata hai par uparwala Joseph ki dil ki dua-ey sunkar Annie ko hosh mai laa-deta hai. Joseph bahut khush hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Raj ) , Manisha Koirala( Annie ) , Nana Patekar( Annie's father ) , Seema Biswas( Annie's mother ) , Himani Shivpuri( Raj's mother ) , Raghubir Yadav( Willie )

Honest Overview



Pyaar ko zubaan ki nahi bas pyaar ki hi zaroorat hoti hai aur ye film dekh-kar aapko ye samajh mai aayega. Film ke gaane 'Baaho ke darmiya' aur 'Aaj mai upar' aap kuch dino tak gungunaate rahenge. Nana Patekar aur Manisha Koirala ne karishmayi(Miraculous) acting ki hai. Ye film apne kisi pyaare ke saath baith kar dekhiye aur iss film ko aankhon se kum dil se zyada dekhi-yega. Brilliant direction by Mr Sanjay Bhansali. 1000% recommended and Deserves every second of your attention !!!
