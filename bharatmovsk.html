Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Bharat ki jo Hindu-pakistan dangoh mai apne baap aur behen ko chuka hai aur Delhi pahuch jaata hai apni Jamuna bua ke paas aur unki dukaan sambhaalne lagta hai. Bharat apne dost Vilyati ke saath ek circus ka hissa banke duniya ko hairat-angez kartab dikhaata hai. Kahi saal beet chuke hai aur Jamuna Bua ji bhi guzar chuki hai. Aarthik samasyao ke baavjood bhi Bharat dukaan nahi bechna chaahta. Kismat Bharat ko apni barso pehle khoyi hui behen Gudiya se milwaadeti hai aur iska zariya banti hai Bharat ki premika Kumud Raina. Bharat apni maa ke guzarne ke baad bilkul akela ho chuka hai aur bas apne pitah ka intezaar kar raha hai kyonki unhone wada kiya tha ki woh ussi dukaan pe aakar Bharat se milenge. Bharat apni premika Raina se shaadi karleta hai aur ab pitah se milne ki ummeed kho chuka hai par Bharat issey zindagi ka ek kadva-sach maanke zindagi se samjhauta kar chuka hai...

Honest Overview



Film mai Salman ka acting talent kuch jhalko mai dekha jaa sakta hai. Sunil Grover ne apni supreme Acting skills se iss saadhi daal ko daal-makhani banadiya hai. Salman-Sunil ki jugalbandi kaafi Comical hai. Nora Fatehi aur Disha Patani ke fans zaroor dekhein iss film ko. A ONE TIME WATCH !!!
