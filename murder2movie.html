Film ki कहानी


DHeeraj deh vyapaar karne wali ladkiyo ko apne ghar bulaake unko Berahami se maarta hai aur uska agla shikaar hai ek Bholi bHali ladki Reshma jo paiso ki killat ki wajah se iss Gorakh dhandey mai aa gayi hai. Reshma Dheeraj ko zakhmi karke bhag nikalti hai. Maram patti keliye bahar nikalne par Dheeraj ko Police dhar leti hai, Dheeraj apne jurm ko kisi bhadhdhe mazak ki tarah Police ko sunaata hai lekin Saboot na hone ki wajah se bach jaata hai. Inspector Arjun Dheeraj ka peecha kartey huey uske ghar pahuchta hai aur ek paapi ko Nark mai pahuchaa deta hai...

Emraan Haashmi( Arjun ) , Jacqueline Fernandez( Priya ) , Prashant Narayanan( Dheeraj ) , Sudhanshu Pandey( Sadaa ) , Bikramjeet Kanwarpal( Ahmed ) , Sulagna Panigrahi( Reshma )
