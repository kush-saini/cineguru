Success at Box Office


Based on Emperor Ashoka

Film ki कहानी


Chandragupta Maurya apne saamrajaya ko apne bete Bindusara ke haath mai saunpna chaahte hai par uska pota Rajkumar Asoka uski talwaar ki maang karta hai , Chandragupta Asoka ko batata hai ki ye talwaar barbaadi aur vinaash laegi. Kahi saal beet chuke hai , Asoka ab jawan hai aur abhi ran-bhoomi se jeetke lauta hai . Asoka ka chachera bhai Susima usey maarne ki koshish karta hai aur dono mai ladai chidjaati hai, Chandragupta Asoka ko vanvaas kaatne ka aadesh dete hai. Asoka ki mulaqat Kalinga ki Rajkumari Kaurwaki aur uske bhai Rajkanwar Arya se hoti hai jo apne bharosemandh rakshak Bheema ke saath Kalinga se bhaag aaye hai. Asoka Chandragupta ke aadesh se Ujjain ki ladai ladne jaa raha hai par pehle Kalinga jaake Kaurkawi se milna chahta hai par Kalinga pahuchke ussey Bheema se pata chalta hai ki Kaurkwai aur Arya ab iss duniya mai nahi hai. Susima ke bheje hue hamlavar Asoka ko ghayal kardete hai . Devi ,ek baudh ladki uski jaan bachati hai . Asoka Devi se shaadi karleta hai aur wapas samraajaya mai laut-aata hai. Susima ke aadmi Asoka ki maa Dharma ko marwadete hai aur Raja Bindusara bimaari se marjaate hai. Maurya saamrajaya ki gaddhi haasil krne keliye Susima Asoka ko peeth-peechey se maarna chahta hai par Virat ke haatho maara jaata hai. Asoka Kalinga par humle ka elaan kardeta hai aur iss baat se anjaan hai ki Kaurwaki aur Arya Kalinga mai hai . Ghamaasaan yudh hota hai , Asoka Kalinga ki ladai jeet jaata hai . Ran-bhoomi par Arya Asoka ke haatho mai dum tod deta hai aur Kaurwaki bhi ghayal hai. Asoka ko Chandragupta ki baat yaad aati hai aur vo apni talwaar ko paani mai faikne ke baad Baudh dharm ko apna-leta hai .

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Asoka ) , Kareena Kapoor( Kaurwaki ) , Ajith Kumar( Susima ) , Hrishita Bhatt( Devi ) , Danny Denzongpa( Virat ) , Rahul Dev( Bheema )

Honest Overview



Shahrukh-Kareena ki chemistry ekdum electrifying hai. Dumdaar Action , Chatpatey gaaney aur khoobsurat visuals . Agar Raja-Maharajao ke zamaaney mai pahuchna chaahtey ho toh iss movie ko zaroor dekho . Movie anth mai ek zaroori message bhi deti hai. Popcorn lekar baith jao iss movie ko dekhne !!!
