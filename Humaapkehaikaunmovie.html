Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Prem aur Rajesh ki jinhe Kailashnath ne apni aulaad ki tarah paala hai. Dono bachche bade ho chuke hai aur Rajesh ka rishta Kailash ne apne dost Siddharth ki beti Pooja ke saath taha kardiya hai. Shaadi ka maahol hai aur Prem ka dil chulbuli Nisha pe aa jaata hai jo ki Pooja ki behen hai. Shaadi huey kaafi samay beet chuka hai aur Pooja ab ek maa hai par bhagwan Pooja ko apne paas bulaaletey hai. Ghar ke badey Nisha aur Rajesh ki shaadi krwane ka faisla kartey hai par Rajesh ko ek khat ke zariye Nisha aur Prem ke dil ka haal pata chalta hai aur Rajesh ki badaulat dono pyaar karnewaalo ki dhoom-dhaam se shaadi hojati hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Madhuri Dixit( Nisha ) , Mohnish Bahl( Rajesh ) , Renuka Shahane( Pooja ) , Anupam Kher( Siddharth ) , Reema Lagoo , Alok Nath( Kailash Nath ) , Laxmikant Berde( Lallu Prasad )
