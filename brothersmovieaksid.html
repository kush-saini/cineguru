Brother V/s Brother

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai do bhaiyo ki Monty aur David. Monty aur David kahi saalo se nahi miley hai. Monty apne dad Gary ko jail se wapas ghar laa raha hai. Gary David se milne uske ghar pahuchta hai par David abhi puraaney ghaavo ko bhula nahi hai aur Gary ko bhaga deta hai. Apni beti ke ilaaj ke paiso keliye David fir se Street Fighting karne lagta hai. Ek bahut badi Championship Right 2 Fight India mai pehli baar hone jaa rahi hai. David aur Monty dono usme hissa letey hai aur poori lagan se Tayyari mai jut gaye hai. Championship ka final match hai unn do jano mai jinki rago mai ek hi khoon daudtaa hai - Monty aur David. Apne dil pe patthar rakh-ke David Monty se maafi maang kar ussey hara deta hai. Ye ladaai purey pariwaar ko firse paas le aayi hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( David ) , Siddharth Malhotra( Monty ) , Jackie Shroff( Gary ) , Shefali Shah( Maria ) , Jacqueline Fernandez( Jenny ) , Ashutosh Rana( Suleiman ) , Kiran Kumar( Peter ) , Kulbhushan Kharbanda( Principal ) , Kareena Kapoor( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Movie dekhte waqt Aansoo aur Goosebumps aane ki GUARANTEE hai. FIlm Physical(Shaaririk) aur Psychological(Maansik) ladai ka pehle kabhi na dekha gaya Amalgamation(Mishran) hai. Film mai dher saara Action hotey huey bhi Dal-Baati Churme jaisi saadhgi hai. Apne Bhai-Behen ke saath baithkar iss film ko ek baar zaroor dekhein. Film ke gaaney Playlist-Worthy hai. 1000% RECOMMENDED to All !!!
