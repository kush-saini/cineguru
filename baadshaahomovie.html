Film ki कहानी


Maharani Gitanjali apne Kiley ka khazaana Sarkaar ke Haatho lagne ke bachaana chaahti hai. Ye kaam Gitanjali apne wafadaar Bhavani ko saunpti hai. Bhavani apne saathi Dalia, Sanjana aur Guruji ke saath milkar Khazaaney ko sarkar ki naak ke neechey se udaa leta hai. Inspector Seher aur Gitanjali ne milke Bhavani ko fasaya hai iss baat ki bhanak Bhavani ko lag chuki thi isliye usne Saara Sona gareebo mai batwa diya hai. Gitanjali ka saara plan chaupat ho chuka hai...
