Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai teen bhaiyo ki - Harry Roy aur Max. In teeno ki zindagi mai lady love bhi hai, Harry Pooja se, Roy Neha se aur Max Kriti se pyaar karta hai. Teeno couples Sitamgarh pahuchtey hai jaha unhe Aakhri Pasta milta hai. Aakhri Pasta teeno bhaiyo ko yaad dilaata hai ki 600 saal pehle wo Raajkumar thay Harry tha Bala, Roy tha Bangdu, Max tha Dharamveer, Teeno bhai 600 saal pehle likhi hui unki Bhavishya-vaani ko sach banadetey hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Bala ) , Riteish Deshmukh( Bangdu ) , Bobby Deol( Dharamputra ) , Kriti Kharbanda( Meena ) , Pooja Hegde( Mala ) , Kriti Sanon( Madhu ) , Chunkey Pandey( Aakhri Pasta ) , Johnny Lever( Giggly ) , Rana Daggubati( Pappu )

Honest Overview



Ye film uss Puppy ke jaisi hai jo apni poochch pakadhne keliye gol-gol ghoom raha hai, Angrezi mai boley toh ( A DOG Chasing its Tail). Iss puppy ko dekh kar hasee bhi aati hai aut taras bhi. Apne Topper Dost ko Exam se pehle ye film dikhaa-kar Uska IQ low kiya jaa sakta hai. Baasi dialogues and BORING Screenplay. Iss film ko dekhne se achcha YouTube pe TheRanveerShow ke Mind-Blowing episodes suniye. NOT RECOMMENDED to people who Respect themselves !!!
