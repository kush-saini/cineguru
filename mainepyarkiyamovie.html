Maine Pyar Kiya-1989


 8.0 / 10

Directed by Sooraj Barjatya

All Time BlockBuster


Salman's 1st time as Hero

Film ki कहानी


Karan gao mai kaam karne wala ek mechanic hai aur business karne Dubai jaa raha hai aur apni beti Suman ko apne jigri dost Kishan ke paas chchod jaata hai. Kishan ka beta Prem Suman se dosti karleta hai. Dheere-dheere dono ki dosti pyar ka roop le rahi hai, Prem ki maa Kaushalya Suman ko apni beti ki tarah pyar karti hai par Kishan ko iss rishtey se aitraaz hai. Karan Dubai se wapas lauta hai aur Kishan ke jhoothe ilzaam sunkar Suman ko saath lekar wapas gao aajata hai. Prem apni Suman ka haath maangne Karan ki chaukat tak pahuch jaata hai par Karan ki shart hai ki Prem ko apni mehnat se paise kamane honge. Karan choti-moti naukriya karta hai aur paise kamaleta hai. Prem Karan aur Kishan apne dushman Ranjeet aur Jeevan ko milkar peet dete hai. Saare giley-shikvey khatam ho chuke hai aur Prem-Suman pyaar ke bandhan mai humesha ke liye bandh chuke hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Bhagyashree( Suman ) , Alok Nath( Karan ) , Rajeev Verma( Kishan ) , Reema Lagoo( Kaushalya ) , Ajit Vachani( Ranjeet ) , Mohnish Bahl( Jeevan )

Honest Overview



" Rishtey jodhne mai kitne saal lag jaatey hai par Rishto ki dor ek pal mai hi toot jaati hai ". Film mai pehle pyaar ki innocence(maasumiyat) dikhayi gayi hai. Iconic dialogues aur Superhit melodius gaaney. Overall film behad Entertaining hai , MUST WATCH FOR SALMAN BHAI KE FANS !!!
