Film ki कहानी


AatankWadi Omar Zafar chalaaki se Sainik Viren Nanda ko maardeta hai. Kohinoor London se chori ho chuka hai aur purey Bharat mai hungama mach gaya hai. Wahi doosri taraf Harleen Shimla mai apni Job se pareshan hai aur Akelepan se pareshan hai. Dating site ke zariye Harleen ki mulaakat hoti hai Rajveer se, Rajveer pe hamla hojaata hai tab Harleen ko pata chalta hai Rajveer ne hi heera churaya tha. Rajveer aur Harleen ab ek dusre ke saath fass chukey hai. Rajveer Kohinoor ki madad se Omar Zafar tak pahuch chuka hai aur ussey maarke apne bhai Viren ki maut ka badla leleta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Hrithik Roshan( Rajveer ) , Katrina Kaif( Harleen ) , Jimmy Shergill( Viren ) , Danny Denzongpa( Omar Zafar ) , Javed Jaffrey( Hamid ) , Pawan Malhotra( Zorawar ) , Vikram Gokhale( Narayanan )

Honest Review



Film ke Action Scenes mai "Hip-Hop Music" ke jaisi Intensity hai aur "DISCO feels" background score ek SERIOUSLY EXCITING screenplay create kardeta hai. Ye film aapki "DREAMS ki Duniya ka Entry Ticket" hai aur har DayDreamer ko zaroor pasand aaegi. Honsestly "Film kisi High Budget 2:30 ghantey ke TIK-TOK ke jaisi hai - ENTERTAINING but NO Depth". Youth ko film pasand aaegi. 1000% RECOMMENDED to Every NIBBA-NIBBI Couples !!!
