Bajrangi Bhaijaan-2015


 7.9 / 10

Directed by Kabir Khan


Drama Comedy


Box-Office Success


Heartfelt and A Feel Good Movie

Film ki कहानी


Ek Pakistaani ladki hai jo hindustan mai kho gayi hai. Hanuman-bhakt Pawan Chaturvedi urf "Bajrangi" uss ladki ko apne pita ke dost Pandey ji ke yaha rakh leta hai aur usko Munni naam se bulaata hai, ladki bechari goongi hai isliye kuch bata bhi nahi sakti hai. Pawan ko Pandey ji ki beti Rasika se pyaar hogya hai par Pandey ji ek kaamyab ladka chaahtey hai Rasika keliye aur Pavan ko kuch dino ki mauhlat detey hai kaamyab banne keliye. Munni doosre mulk Pakistan ki ladki hai ye pata chalne ke baad Pandey ji ussey ghar se nikaalne ka faisla kartey hai, Bajrangi bhi thaan leta hai Munni ko pariwaar se milwake rahega aur nikaal padta hai apni zindagi ke sabse nek aur mushkil safar pe Chand Nawab Reporter ki madad se, Maar-Pitaai sab jheltey huey Bajrangi Munni ko Pakistan mai uske ammi-abba se milwa-deta hai par police ussey pakad leti hai. Police Bajrangi ki nek-dili dhek kar ussey Border paar karwa detey hai aur Munni/Shaheeda jor se chilla ke Bajrangi ko pukaar ti hai aur dono khushi se galey lag jaatey hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Bajrangi ) , Harshaali Malhotra( Munni ) , Nawazuddin Siddique( Chand Nawab ) , Kareena Kapoor( Rasika ) , Meher Vij( Munni's maa ) , Sharat Saxena( Rasika's papa ) , Alka Kaushal( Rasika's mother ) , Adnan Sami( Cameo ) , Om Puri( Maulana )

Honest Overview



Ek bandhan sa judjaata hai aisi filmo ke saath, Aap involve hojaate ho film mai, Aap kirdaro keliye dua kartey ho aur unki takleefey aapko mehsoos hoti hai. Ye film aapke andar chupey emotions ko invoke karti hai. Beautiful Cinematography. Pehle pyaar ki asmanjas aur Pyaar ka ek apna dharam hota hai ye film mai bakhoobi dikhaya gaya hai. This film is truly Kabir Khan's MONALISA. A MUST WATCH FOR INDIAN CINEMA LOVERS !!!
