Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Raj Mathur ki jo peshey se Civil engineer hai aur naukri ki talaash mai Mumbai aaya hai ek rishtedaar ka pata lekar . Pate par pahuch kar Raj ko ye pata chalta hai ki uss rishtedaar ko gaye hue toh kayi saal beet gaye. Haalaat ka maara Raj ghoomta-firta ek Mandir pahuchta hai aur wahi raat bitaane ka faisla krta hai. Mandir mai Raj ki mulaakaat ek raah-chalte kalakaar Jai se hoti hai jo Raj ko dost ka darja aur apne ghar mai panaah deta hai. Renu jo ek Construction company ke maalik Chaabria ki secretary hai ,Raj ko naukri dilwane mai madad karti hai. Raj aur Renu ki dosti pyaar mai badal jaati hai.Raaj jee lagake kaam krta hai aur tezi se kaamyabi ki seediyan chadh jaata hai.Daulat-Shaurat ki duniya Raj ko badal deti hai.Chaabria ki beti Sapna Raj ko chahne lagti hai par Renu ke liye Raj ka pyar dekhke Sapna ka dil toot jaata hai. Raj ke dushman uske dwara banaya gya bridge girwa dete hai.Raj ko ehsaas hote hai ye chaka-chaundh ki duniya uske liye nahi hai aur voh naukri chchod deta hai.Raj Renu ke paas wapas lautna chahta hai.Jai dono pyaar krne walo ko milwa deta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Raj ) , Juhi Chawla( Renu ) , Nana Patekar( Jai ) , Amrita Singh( Sapna ) , Navin Nischol( Chaabria ) , Neeraj Vora
