Kurbaan-1991


 6.0 / 10

Directed by Deepak Bahry


Action Romance


Box Office Hit


Salman-Ayesha's 1st movie together

Film ki कहानी


Maan Singh Prithvi Singh se zameen ka case haar chuka hai aur badla lene keliye Daaku Panna Singh se Prithvi ke poorey pariwar ko marwa deta hai. Prithvi Maan Singh ke parivaar pe hamla kardeta hai aur uska dost Inspector Suraj Singh ussey salakhon ke peechey daal-deta hai. Suraj Maan Singh ka Bhai hai. Prithvi jail se bhaagjaata hai aur apni beti Chanda ke saath shaant zindagi bita raha hai. Suraj Singh ka beta Akash Chanda se pyaar karne lagta hai. Suraj aur Prithvi apni dushmani ke chalte iss rishtey ko nahi apnaatey aur apne bachcho ko ek-doosre se nahi milne de rahey. Akash aur Chanda ek dusre se milne mai kaamyab hojaate hai par Panna Singh unhe kidnap karleta hai. Suraj aur Prithvi Panna Singh ke aadmiyo ko maarke Suraj-Chanda ko bachaaney mai kaamyab hojaatey hai aur galat-faimiya dur karke Akash-Chanda ka rishta apna-letey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Akash ) , Ayesha Jhulka( Chanda ) , Sunil Dutt( Prithvi Singh ) , Kabir Bedi( Suraj Singh ) , Gulshan Grover( Himmat Singh ) , Goga Kapoor( Panna Singh ) , Bharat Kapoor( Maan Singh )

Film mai dikhaya gaya hai ki kaise galat-faimiyo se rishto mai kadvaahut aa jaati hai. Timepass ke liye movie mast hai!!!
