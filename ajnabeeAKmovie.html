SuperHit


Akshay Kumar's Best Performance Ever

Film ki कहानी


Raj aur Priya ek seedhi saadhi zindagi bita rahe hai Switzerland mai. Unki zindagi mai entry hoti hai unke naye padosi Vicky aur Sonia ki. Dono jodey(couples) mai gehri dosti hojaati hai. Raj-Priya aur Vicky-Sonia Mauritius ghoomne jaatey hai jaha Raj ko Vicky aur SOnia ke galat iraado ki bhanak lag jaati hai aur wo Priya ko lekar wapas aajata hai. Badi Mushkil se Vicky aur Raj mai sulaah hojaati hai. Kuch din baad Vicky Raj ko Sonia ke murder case mai fasa deta hai. Raj Court se bhaagne mai kaamyab hojaata hai aur Priya ki madad se Vicky ka peecha kartey huey Vicky ki dhokadhadi se kamayi hui rakam ko wapas Bank mai transfer karwa deta hai. Raj Vicky ko dhoondh hi leta hai aur dekhta hai ki Sonia zinda hai, phir Vicky saara game plan Raj ko samjhaata hai ki jis ladki ko usne INSURANCE ke PAISO keliye maara tha wo uski biwi Sonia Bajaj thi aur jisse Raj jaanta hai wo Neeta hai. Raj aur Vicky mai ladaai chchidh jaati hai jisme Vicky maara jaata hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Vicky ) , Bobby Deol( Raj ) , Bipasha Basu( Neeta ) , Kareena Kapoor( Priya ) , Mink Brar( Sonia ) , Johnny Lever( Banu ) , Dalip Tahil( Priya's papa ) , Sharat Saxena( Insurance Detective )

Ye film Karma ke insaaf ke baare mai bataati hai aur Zariye ki ahmiyat bhi sikhaati hai. AKshay iss Role mai behad "DESPICABLE" lagey hai apni SOUND performance ki wajah se.
