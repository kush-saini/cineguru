Film ki कहानी


Aditya ek rahees Business Man IshwarChandra ki bigdi hui aulad hai aur aksar apni maa se laaparwahi ke taaney sunta hai. Adi paiso kitni mehnat se kamaye jaatey hai ye nahi samajhta. Ishwar Cancer ki bimaari se mar raha hai aur ye baat bas Adi ki maa Sumitra ko pata hai. Adi iss baat se anjaan hai kyonki Ishwar Adi ko humesha khush dekhna chaahta hai. Adi Nattu Seth ki beti Pooja se shaadi karke ussey ghar leaata hai. Ishwar Adi ko apne paise khud kamaane keliye bolkar ussey ghar se bahar nikaal deta hai. Pooja pait se hai aur Adi ko apni zimmedaari ka ehsaas ho chuka hai. Adi apni jaan par khel kar apne pehle paise kamaata hai aur Apni biwi ka dhyan rakh raha hai. Adi Talent Show ki tayaari mai lagaa hai aur Adi apne baap se dur hota jaa raha hai. Adi Competetion jeet jaata hai aur ab Apne pairo par khada ho chuka hai. Ishwar ki tabehat behad kharab ho rahi hai par woh apne potey ko khilaane ki apni ichcha poori karke dum tod-deta hai. Adi apne betey ka naam apne pitah ke naam pe rakhta hai Ishwar...
