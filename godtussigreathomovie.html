God Tussi Great Ho-2008


 6.6 / 10

Directed by Rumi Jaffrey


Comedy Drama


Box Office Flop


Movie had bad luck

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Arun Prajapati urf AP ki, puri duniya bhagwan ko poojti hai aur ye bhaisaab apni har takleef pe Bhagwan ko gaali detey hai. AP ke show ka pehla episode hi flop hojaata hai. Office mai sab uska mazaak uda rahe hai bas uski dost Alia ke alawa jissey mann hi mann wo pyaar karta hai. Ye sab kaafi nahi tha ki uski musibat badhaane aa jaata hai Rocky jo uske naye show Jhoot Bole Kauwa Kaatey ka saara credit le jaata hai. AP ko naukri se nikaal dia jaata hai. AP ko naukri keliye ek call aata hai waha pahuchkar uski mulaakat hoti hai GOD se. GOD AP ko apni jagah sambhalne ka mauka detey hai tab jaake AP ko samajh aata hai ki GOD ki job kitni mushkil hai par GOD ki bachchi aakri shakti ki boond se wo Rocky ka parda-faash kardeta hai aur Alia ko apni dil ki baat batadeta hai....

Honest Overview



"Jis ko khuda ne jitna diya hai wo utne ke hi layak hai na zyada na kum, isliye bhagwan se naraaz hone ka koi matlab nahi". Rocky ka kirdaar aapko bahut pasand aaega. MAZEDAAR film from the starting to end. Popcorn ke saath iss film ko enjoy karein. DONT JUDGE THIS FILM BY ITS BOX OFFICE !!!
