Maya Memsaab-1993


 7.5 / 10

Directed by Ketan Mehta


Mystery Drama Passion


Flop at Box Office


National Film Award for Feature film

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ek khoobsurat aur jawaan ladki Maya ki jo ek aalishaan bungley mai apne pitaah ke saath gao mai rehti hai. Ek din Maya ke pitaah ko daura padta hai. Maya doctor ko phone karke ghar bulaati hai. Dr. Charu Das apni cycle lekar waha pahuchta hai aur Pitaahji ko ilaaj batata hai. Uss din ke baad bhi Charu Das ka waha aana-jaana laga rehta hai Maya ko dekhne keliye. Maya aur CharuDas ki shaadi hojaati hai. Ab kahi saal beet chuke hai, Charu Das apne mareezo mai itna magn rehta hai ki Maya ko waqt nhi de paata. Maya ki shaaririk zaroortey poori nahi ho paati. Maya jismaani pyaar keliye tarasti hai. Maya ki mulaakaat ek naujawan Rudra se hoti hai aur unka najais rishta banjaata hai. Ye rishta jaldi toot jaata hai. Rudra se bhi jawaan ek ladka Lalit , Maya ki zindagi mai aata hai aur unka havas se bhara shaaririk sambandh ban jaata hai. Maya jism ki bhook ko chchodke ab sachcha pyaar chaahti hai aur Lalit se bhi rishta tod deti hai. Khaalipan mitaane keliye Maya mehengi cheezo par paisa luta-tey huye apna ghar Lalaji ko girwi rakhdeti hai. Ek din Maya sadak par ek rahasyamayi paani ke baare mai sunti hai jo peeye jaane par nek-dil waale logo ki ek ichcha poori kardeta hai. Paani peetey hi Maya tej chamakti hui hawa mai gayab hojaati hai. Jaanch-padtal krne wale achambey mai hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Deepa Sahi( Maya ) , Farooq Sheikh( Charu Das ) , Raj Babbar( Rudra ) , Shahrukh Khan( Lalit ) , Paresh Rawal( Lalaji ) 

Honest Overview



Agar aap Bollywood masala filmein dekhna pasand krte hai toh ye film apke liye nahi hai. Dark aur intense cinema pasand krnewalo keliye ye movie bani hai.Iss film mai ek shaadishuda aurat ki tanhaai aur pyaar ki kabhi khatam na hone wali talaash ko bakhoobi dikhaya gaya hai. Deepa Sahi ne kabil-e-tareef kaam kiya hai. Definitely Recommended !!
