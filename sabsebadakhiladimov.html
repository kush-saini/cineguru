Sabse Bada Khiladi-1995


 5.1 / 10

Directed by Umesh Mehra


Action Drama


Box Office Hit


Finely Weaved Story

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Vijay ki jo apni maa ke gunhegaar Amar Singh ko jaan se maarna chaahta hai par Amar Singh kathgharey mai koi saboot naa hone ki vajay se bach jaata hai. Amar ek theth gavar Lallu banke apni pehchaan Jamana Das se banakar unke yaha naukri karne lagta hai. Jamana Das coma mai hai aur apni beti Sunita ki shaadi Lallu se hotey huye dekhna chaahte hai. Sunita apne premi Amit(Amar Singh ka beta) ke saath milke Lallu ko jaan se maar-deti hai. Sunita aur Amit ke hosh tab udd jaatey hai jab wo Lallu ko Inspector ki wardi mai dekhte hai, Lallu Amit ko batata hai ki ye uska plan tha jisme Jamana Das aur Sunita miley huey thay. Amit ko Lallu ki maut ke jurm mai faasi ki sazaa hojaati hai aur Amar Singh apne haatho Lallu ke pariwar pe kiye har apradh ko kubool leta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Lallu ) , Mamta Kulkarni( Sunita ) , Mohnish Behl( Amit ) , GUlshan Grover( Kekada ) , Avtar Gill( Jamana Das ) , Sadashiv Amrapurkar( Amar Singh ) , Anjana Mumtaz( Gomti ) , Dina Pathak( Daadi )

Honest Overview



"Humesha eith ka jawab paththar se nahi dia jaata balki Dimaag ki tez dhaar ko Hathyaar banake hamla karna hota hai" ye film humhe sikhaati hai. "Maang meri bharo" aur "Har dil mai hai rab basta" ye dono gaaney film ko thoda Behtar banatey hai. JUST A DECENT ONE TIME WATCH !!!
