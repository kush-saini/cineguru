Shakti-2002


 6.9 / 10

Directed by Krishna Vamsi


Action Drama


Average Box Office


Very Interesting Story

Film ki कहानी


Shekhar aur Nandini apne bete Raja ke saath Canada mai rehte hai. Shekhar Bihar mai apne pushtaani ghar wapas laut-tah hai. Shekhar ke pitah Narsimha ladai-dangho mai humesha lage rehte hai , Ek din aise hi kisi jhagde-fasaadh ko rokne ki koshish mai Shekhar maara jaata hai. Nandini apne bete ko lekar wapas jaana chaahti hai par Narsimha uske raaste mai chattan banke khada hai. Narsimha ki biwi aur beti Nandini ki bhaagne mai madad karte hai. Nandini ek ajnabi Jai se milti hai jo unke liye Narsimha ke aadmiyo se ladta hai aur maar diya jaata hai. Narsimha apne potey ko bahut pyar karta hai aur apni biwi ki baat maankar Nandini aur Raja ko Canada jaane ke liye aazad kardeta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Karisma Kapoor( Nandini ) , Nana Patekar( Narsimha ) , Sanjay Kapoor( Shekhar ) , Deepti Naval( Shekhar's mother ) , Divya Dutta( Shekhar's sister ) , Vijay Raaz , Prakash Raj , Tiku Talsania , Shahrukh Khan( Jai )

Honest Overview



Shahrukh ne film mai chota par zaroori role nibhaya hai. Karisma ki acting kaabil-e-tareef hai aur woh kaafi khoobsurat bhi lag rahi hai. Karisma Kapoor ke fans iss film ko zaroor dekhe. Film ka gaana " Ishq Kameena " superhit hai. Overall great timepass movie with dumdaar dialogues and acting !!!
