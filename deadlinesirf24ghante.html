When Karma Gives you a Tight Slap

Film ki कहानी


Viren ek Mashoor Heart Surgeon hai aur abhi ek zaroori kaam se Sheher se bahar aaya hua hai. Kuch der baad ussey apni biwi Sanjana se khabar milti hai ki unki beti Anishka kidnap ho chuki hai. Kidnapper firauti ki rakam ko badhaatey hi jaa rahey hai. Viren ko chakhma dekar Kidnapper paise bhi lejaatey hai aur bachchi ko wapas bhi nahi lautaatey. Viren aur Sanjans puri tarah se toot chukey hai ki tabhi Anishka unhe wapas miljaati hai par kaise ? ye jaan-ney keliye film dekhein...

Film ke कलाकार

Irrfan Khan( Krish ) , Konkona Sen( Sanjana ) , Rajit Kapur( Viren ) , Zakir Hussain( Kabir ) , Sandhya Mridul( Roohi ) , Rajeev Verma( Commissioner )

Honest Overview



Film ka Message "Hamdard Safi Syrup" ke Ghoot ki tarah Kadhvahat(Bitterness) se bhara hua hai par Insaan ki Rago mai behtey huey EGO(Ahankaar) roopi Impurity ka safaya karne ki Capability(shamtaa) rakhta hai. FIlm "Jaisi karni Waisi Bharni" philosophy ko ek Tezz-Taraar aur THRILLING Screenplay ke zariye dikhaati hai. VERDICT -"You will find Answers to Many Questions of LIFE". 1000% RECOMMENDED to Hard-Core Irrfan Khan fans !!!
