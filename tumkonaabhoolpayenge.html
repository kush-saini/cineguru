Tumko Na Bhool Payenge-2002


 5.2 / 10

Directed by Pankaj Parashar

Box Office Flop


Diya and Salman's 1st movie together

Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Veer ki jo apne maa-baauji ke saath rehta hai ek chote se gao mai. Veer ki shaadi Thakur ki beti Muskaan se hone wali hai. Kuch gundey Shaadi mai bhung daalne aa jaate hai par Veer unko taabad-tod peet-deta hai. Veer ko kuch dino se apni beeti zindagi ki dhundhli yaadein pareshan kar rahi hai. Baauji Veer ko uska sach bata dete hai ki vo ek zakhmi bina yaad-daasht ka ladka tha jisse unhone apne shaheed bete Veer ka naam diya aur paala. Veer ko Mumbai jaakar pata chalta hai ki uska asli naam Ali hai. Ali apne puraaney dost Inder se milta hai aur apni purani maashuka Mahek se bhi. Ali ko yaad aata kis tarah Inder ne Chief Minister ko maarke , gawah mitaane keliye uski jaan leni chaahi thi. Mehak ke paas ek tape jisme mai Ali ki begunhaai aur Inder ke gunhegaar hone ka saboot hai. Inder Ali ko maarne waha aa pahuchta hai aur batataa hai kis tarah wo Ali ke zinda hotey huye kabhi Awwal (1st) nahi ho payega zindagi ke kisi bhi pahloo mai. Ali Inder ko jannat pahuchaa deta hai. Iss maar-peet mai Mehak alla ko pyaari ho jaati hai. Mehak ki tape TV pe chalai jaati hai, Ali ab behgunhaa aur swantantra naagrik hai. ALi/Veer gao wapas apni Muskaan ke saath SAAT phere le leta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Veer/Ali ) , Sushmita Sen( Mehak ) , Dia Mirza( Muskan ) , Inder Kumar( Inder ) , Sharat Saxena( Veer's papa ) , Mukesh Rishi( Manoj ) , Anjan Shrivastav( CM ) , Alok Nath( Rahim ) , Rajpal Yadav( Lallan ) , Johnny Lever( Taantrik )

Honest Overview



Ye film Kareley ke jaisi hai, Kisi ko pasand aaegi kisi ko shayad na pasand aaye. Hero-Villain ka fighting scene bahut badiya create kiya gaya hai. Rajpal Yadav ki dialogue delivery aur comic timing Impeccable hai. This movie only for Bhaijaan ke bhakts !!!
