Swades-2004


 8.9 / 10

Directed by Ashutosh Gowariker


Social Drama


Decent at Box-Office


2 National Film Awards

Film ki कहानी


Mohan NASA mai kaam krta hai par usko apni maa samaan Kaveri Amma ki chinta sataati rehti hai . Mohan Kaveri Amma ko apne saath USA lejaane keliye India aata hai. Apne desh ki khoobsurati uske mann ko moh leti hai. Mohan ko pata chalta hai ki Amma uski bachpan ki dost Gita ke paas Charanpur mai rehti hai. Geeta Charanpur mai ek school chalaati hai gaowaalo ki haalat sudhaarne keliye aur ladkiyo ko education dene keliye. Gita ka vyaktitva Mohan ko pasand aajaata hai. Mohan pados ke gao Kodi jaata hai Gita ke paise Haridas se wapas lene keliye, Haridas ko do waqt ki roti bhi naseeb nahi hoti aur neechi jaati ka hone ki wajah se koi uski madad bhi nahi karta. Dukhi man ke saath Mohan Charanpur pahuchta hai. Mohan Charanpur ke liye kuch karna chahta hai . Mohan Charanpur mai ek hydro-electric power plant lagata hai jissey 24 ghante bijli ki suvidha hojaati hai.Amma Gita ko chhodkar USA nahi jaana chahti toh Mohan akele USA wapas chal jaata hai par Swades ussey wapas bula raha hai aur Swades ki pukar sunke Mohan USA chhodke Bharat aajata hai aur Vikram SaraBhai Space Center mai naukri krne lagta hai. Mohan apne gao mai Amma ke paas bahut khush hai .

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Overview



Ye film Bharat ke liye aapke pyaar ko dus gunha bada degi. Shahrukh ne laajawab acting ki hai . Ye movie aam bollywood movies jaisi nahi hai par zaroori message deke jaati hai. Ashutosh Gowariker ji ko dhanyawad aisi khoobsurat film banane keliye . Picture ek classic hai , Must Watch before you die !!!
