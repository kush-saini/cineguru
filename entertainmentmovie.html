Film ki कहानी


Akhil ko abhi pata chala hai ki vo anaath nahi balki Swarg Vaasi PannaLal Johri ki aulaad hai. Akhil apne dost Jugnu ke saath Bangkok aa gaya hai apna haq maange. Akhil ko pata chalta hai ki Pannalal ne apni saari daulat Entertainment naam ke apne kuttey ko dedi hai. Akhil aur Jugnu Entertainment ko khatam karne ki koshish kartey kartey uske bholepan se pyaar karne lag jaatey hai. Pannalal ke rishteydaar Karan aur Arjun Entertainment ko maar detey hai par Akhil ki duayein Entertainment ko wapas leaati hai...
