Film ki कहानी


Yudi London ka ek kaamyaab lekhak hai par kahi saalo se kaam nahi kar raha hai, bus party-sharty aur masti hi karta rehta hai. Yudi ki kitaab ab BestSeller nahi rahi hai ussey pachchaadh diya hai nayi writer Aanchal Reddy ki kitaab ne. Yudi ko Mashoor Hero Armaan ne ek film likhne keliye Offer diya hai. Iss film ko likhne keliye Yudi Inspiration ki talaash mai hai aur Aanchal se uski nazdeekiyan bhi badhti jaa rahi hai lekin Aanchal pyaar keliye abhi tayyar nahi hai aur wapas India jaa rahi hai. Yudi Aanchal ke saath bitaaey har lamhe ko panno mai utaarke Script tayyar kardeta hai. Armaan Yudi ki script ko film keliye pakka karleta hai. Yudi India pahuchkar Aanchal se apne pyaar ko ek naya Jeevan deta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Yudi ) , Ileana D'Cruz( Aanchal ) , Govinda( Armaan ) , Ranvir Shorey( Montu ) , Kalki Koechlin( Vishakha ) , Kareena Kapoor( Cameo ) , Preity Zinta( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Ye film har Writer ki dukhti rag pe haath rakhti hai aur "Bas PAL mai Jeene" ka sabak sikhaati hai. Agar aap ek Writer ya koi Creative Professional hai toh Apne 1st Creative piece ko banaane ka ehsaas iss film ko dekh-kar fir se Taro-Taaza kar saktey hai. Ye kahaani Gen-Z ke DISCO lifestyle ka Shidhdhat wale Pyaar mai HeartWarming Transition dikhaati hai. The film Triggers Emotional Intelligence & a Sense of Spirituality. Apne Partner ke iss film ko zaroor dekhein. Iss film mai aap "JALEBI jaisi tedey Rudy ko Seedha hotey dekhenge". 1000% RECOMMENDED to NIBBA-NIBBI's !!!
