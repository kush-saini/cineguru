Film ki कहानी


Kanji Lal ji Mehta ek vyaapari hai jo bhagwan ki moortiya bechta hai par bhagwan mai hi vishwas nahi karta hai. Kanji ki dukaan bhukamp mai girjaati hai. Insurance Company Kanji ki madad nahi karpaati isliye Kanji bhagwan par case kardeta hai. Poorey desh mai ye khabar sansani ki tarah fail jaati hai aur Kanji apne parivaar se alag hokar poori duniya se taaney sun raha hai. Kanji ki zindagi mai entry hoti hai Krishna Vasudev Yadav ki jiski madad se Kanji iss case ko jeet jaata hai. Kanji ko pata chalta hai ki Krishna asli mai Bhagwan hai jo uski poori zindagi aur nazariye ko badal ke jaa chuka hai...
