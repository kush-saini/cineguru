Film ki कहानी


Prem apne Kapoor Khaandaan ka chiraag hai aur Yaaron ka Yaar bhi. Prem ke issi yaarane se Kapoor khaandaan pareshan hai aur uske liye ladki dhoondke uski Shaadi karwana chaahta hai. Pooja Prem se milne wali hai par kisi galatfaimi ke chalte nahi milpaati aur issi baat ka faayda uthaake Sanjana Pooja banke Prem se milti hai aur Kapoor family ka dil jeet leti hai. Prem ko Sanjana ka sach pata chal chuka hai par pichle kuch dino mai unki achchi dosti ho gayi hai aur dosti Pyaar mai tabdeel ho gayi hai. Sanjana aur Prem ki shaadi teh ho gayi hai par Sanjana ka khaandaan iss rishtey ko nahi apnaana chaahta. Prem bohot jugaad lagake Sanjana ke pariwar ko pataata hai. Ab Sanjana ban chuki hai Sanjana Kapoor...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Asin( Sanjana ) , Mahesh Manjrekar( Prem's Papa ) , Manoj Joshi( Bharat ) , Puneet Issar( Ishwar ) , Sharat Saxena( Suraj ) , Akhilendra Mishra( Amar ) , Paresh Rawal( Balli ) , Sudesh Lehri( Lehri )

Honest Overview



Overly Dramatic film but also Entertaining in bits and pieces. Great performance by Asin and just the right amount of tolerable over-acting. Film Popcorn Worthy hai aur pariwar ke saath enjoy kari jaa sakti hai. RECOMMENDED TO SALMAN FANS !!!
