Film ki कहानी


Raja ek nakaara aur aalsi ladka hai jo apne jyotish maama ki baaton ke anusaar chalta hai. Mama ke anusaar Raja ek saal baad kaamyab honjaega par iske liye Raja ko kisi ladki se dosti nahi karni hai. Ek din Crorepati Badri Prasad ki beti Shalu ki gaadi se takkar khaake Raja hospital pahuch jaata hai. Shalu ko tootey-footey Raja se pyaar hojaata hai par Shalu ke pitah ko mehnati damad chahiye Raja jaisa kaamchor nahi. Badri Prasad Raja se ek laakh rupay lekar apni beti ka haath dene ka waada karta hai. Raja pehelwan Kaalu ko harake Shalu ke pitah se sharth jeet jaata hai. Raja aur Shalu ab Honeymoon par hai...
