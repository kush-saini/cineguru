Film ki कहानी


Dushant Jaya Sujit aur Nisha bachpan ke dost hai. Dushant ek kaamyab singer ban-na chaahta hai aur Jaya ki sahayata se ekdin vo mukaam haasil kar hi leta hai. Sujit aur Nisha chaalaki se Jaya aur Dushant ko alag karke Nisha ke baap Kamlesh ki madad se Dushant ki daulat hadapna chaahte hai . Dushant ko iss baat ki bhanak lag jaati hai. Rahasyamayi halaato mai Nisha , Dhingra aur Sujit ka khoon hojaata hai. Inspector ke mutaabik saare khoon ek hi insaan se kiye hai. Khooni ka parda-faash hojaata hai aur pata lagta hai ki khooni Jaya ka baap hai jisne insaaf ko apne haatho mai lekar apni beti ki maut ka badla lene keliye un teeno ka maut ke ghaat utaar diya tha .
