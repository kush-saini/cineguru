Box Office Success


King Khan and Bhaijaan together

Film ki कहानी


Laxmi ek vidhwa hai aur apne bahcho Radha aur Prashant ke saath mushkil se guzaara kar rahi hai. Laxmi sadak se ek anaath ko ghar le aati hai aur uska naam Suraj rakhti hai. Radha Suraj ko apne bhai ki tarah apne leti hai. Kahi saal beet chuke hai aur ab Suraj ek kaamyab singer hai, Radha ki shaadi ek ameer businessman Gopal se hojaati hai, Gopal dil ka bahut achcha hai par ussey Radha ka Suraj se baat karna bikul pasand nahi hai . Gopal ko lagta hai ki Suraj aur Radha ke beech mai koi naajaaiz rishta hai. Gopal Suraj ko akele mai baat karne keliye bulaata hai aur uske saamne bandook taan deta hai , Gopal Suraj se kehta hai ki tum mujhe hi maardo kyonki mujhe apni biwi ka dusre mardo se baat karna pasand nahi hai. Suraj waha se chala jaata hai aur faisla karta hai Radha ki toot-ti hui shaadi bachaane keliye ussey kabhi nahi milega. Suraj ki premika Suman Gopal ko Suraj-Radha ke bhai-behen ke rishtey ke baarey mai samjhdeti hai. Radha ko suicide krne se rokne ke baad Gopal Radha se maafi maangta hai aur dono saare giley-shikvey bhulaa kar ek nayi pyaar bhari zindagi ki shuruat karte hai.
