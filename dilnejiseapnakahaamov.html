Film ki कहानी


Rishabh ek Ad-Agency mai kaam karta hai aur Pari ek Doctor hai. Sach ki kehte hai ki opposites attract ,indono ke saath bhi aisa hi hua. Attraction ke baad Pyaar hua aur Pyaar ke baad Shaadi. Pari ka sapna hai gareeb bachchon keliye Hospital banana par zindagi jald hi Pari ka saath chchod deti hai. Rishabh ke naa chaahne ke baad bhi Pari ki aakhri wish ke liye uska Dil donate krdiya jaata hai Dhani naam ki ek ladki ko. Dhani aur Rishabh ki kareebiya badhti hi jaa rahi hai par Pari ko khone ka ghaav abhi bhi haraa hai. Dhani ka heart fail ho raha hai par Rishabh ki duaye Dhani ko zinda rakhne mai kaamyab hojaati hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Rishabh ) , Preity Zinta( Pari ) , Bhumika Chawla( Dhani ) , Rati Agnihotri( Dr.Shashi ) , Helen( Dhani's daadi ) , Renuka Shahane( Rishabh's behen ) , Aasif Sheikh( Tripathi ) , Riya Sen( Kamini ) , Delnaaz Irani( Dhillo )

Honest Overview



Film mai kuch bhi badhiya nahi hai. LackLuster performances by the Actors and Poor ScreenPlay. NOT EVEN WORTH A SECOND OF YOUR TIME. DO ghante ki neend is a BETTER OPTION than this WATCHING THIS MOVIE !!!
