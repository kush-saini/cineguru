Film ki कहानी


Gunda Goli imaandar Inspector Arjun Joglekar ko chalaaki se maar-deta hai. Arjun ka bhai Karan Joglekar Goli pe case karna chahta hai aur gawah Mona ko bhi gawahi dene keliye mana leta hai. Mona ki hifaazat keliye Karan sheher se bahut dur chale jaata hai ussey lekar. Goli Helicopter se waha pahuch jaata hai aur Mona ko goliyo se bhoon deta hai. Karan ki aakhri ummeed bhi ab khatam ho chuki hai. Ek gundey Damodar Bhai ki dhulaai kartey waqt Karan ki mulaqaat Actor Deepak se hoti hai, Deepak uske andaaz ka deewana ho gya hai aur uske ghar mai kuch din rehne aa gya hai uski personality ko study karne keliye. Karan ki behen Shivangi aur Deepak ek doore se pyaar karne lagtey hai. Karan Mona ki behroopiya Basanti ko bina yaad-daasht ki Mona banaake Goli ke paas bhejta hai aur ek din Goli ko bhagwan ke paas pahucha deta hai. Ab Karan ka badla poora ho chuka hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Karan ) , Saif Ali Khan( Deepak ) , Shilpa Shetty( Mona/Basanti ) , Rageshwari( Shivangi ) , Shakti Kapoor( Goli ) , Kader Khan( Ramlal ) , Johnny Lever( Dhansukh ) , Mukesh Khanna( Arjun ) , Bena Banerjee( Karan's maa ) , Goga Kapoor( Damodar )

Honest Review



Saif Ali Khan ki innocent-face Comedy aur Akshay Kumar ke High-Octane Action scenes aapko entertain karne ka kaam kardengey. Dialogues "WHISTLE" worthy aur film " POPCORN BUCKET " worthy hai. 1000% HIGHLY RECOMMENDED TO EVERY BOLLYWOOD MASALA LOVER !!!
