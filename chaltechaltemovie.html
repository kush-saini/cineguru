Chalte Chalte-2003


 8.2 / 10

Directed by Aziz Mirza


Romance Drama


Box Office Hit


Shot in India,Greece and Athens

Film ki कहानी


Raj ek truck company ka maalik hai aur Priya ek kaamyab fashion designer hai . Priya aur Raj ki ek truck accident mai mulakaat hoti hai, dono wapis ek shaadi mai miltey hai aur unme dosti hojaati hai. Dheere Dheere dono pyaar mai padh jaatey hai. Priya ki sagaai ho chuki hai , Raj Priya ko manaane Greece pahuchta hai . Priya bhi Raj se pyaar krti hai aur wo dono shaadi karlete hai. Raj paiso ki kami se joonjh raha hai , Priya jhuth bolke usey apne dost Sameer ki madad se paise dedeti hai. Raj ko iss baat ka pata chalta hai aur dono ka jhagda hojaata hai. Raj Priya ko manaane uske ghar pahuchta hai par ussey bezati krke bhagaa diya jaata hai. Priya wapis Greece jaakar zindagi ki nayi shuruat krne jaa rahi hai par Raj airport pohoch kar khudko badalne ka waada krte huey Priya ko rokne ki koshish karta hai aur Priya ko ek taabeez deta hai. Hataash hokar Raj wapas ghar pahuchta hai aur paata hai Priya uske intezaar mai baithi hai. Dono premi fir se miljaatey aur apne pyaar bhare andaaz mai nok-jhok karne lagtey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Raj ) , Rani Mukherji( Priya ) , Satish Shah( Manubhai ) , Lilette Dubey( Priya's aunt ) , Johnny Lever , Jas Arora( Sameer ) , Aditya Pancholi , Rajeev Verma
