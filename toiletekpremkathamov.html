Toilet: Ek Prem Katha-2017


 5.4 / 10

Directed by Shree Narayan Singh


Drama Comedy


Gigantic Box Office


Daunting Story

Film ki कहानी


Keshav apne bauji ki har baat maanta aaya hai chaahey wo kitne bhi AndhVishwas se bhari ho. Keshav ka taanka bhida hai Jaya ke saath. Apne pariwaro ki sehmati se dono shaadi karletey hai. Shaadi ke pehle din Jaya ko baaki aurato ke saath gao mai dur jaake number 2 karna padhta hai kyonki Keshav ke ghar mai Sandaas nahi hai. Jaya aur Keshav alag hojaatey hai kyonki Jaya ek modern ladki hai jo aise ghar mai 2 minute bhi nahi reh sakti jaha ek toilet bhi naa ho. Sarkar Toilet banaaney ki anumati dedeti hai Keshav ki arzi ko maankar. Ab Keshav ke ghar mai Sandaas aur Biwi dono hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Keshav ) , Bhumi Pednekar( Jaya ) , Sudhir Pandey( Vimalnath ) , Anupam Kher( Dinanath ) , Divyendu Sharma( Narayan ) , Shobha Khotey( Daadi )

Honest Review



Film apne naam jitni hi Alag-Thalag hai. Biwi ki naraazgi se aadmi badalta hua dekha hoga par Biwi ki naraazgi se samaaj ko badaltaa hua aap iss film ke zariye dekhengey. Ye movie Humare desh ki Rudiwaadhi(Conservatory) soch mai Badhlaav laane ki ek Pehel hai. Ye film uss Kitaab ke jaisi hai jo gyaan aur manoranjan dono ko apne panno ke andar samatey huey hai. Poorey pariwar ke saath zaroor dekhein. 1000% RECOMMENDED to Rural Population of India !!!
