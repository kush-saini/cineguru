Dabangg 2-2012


 5.1 / 10

Directed by Arbaaz Khan


Action Comedy Drama


BlockBuster


Salman Khan's type of Cinema

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Chulbul Pandey ki jo ab Kanpur shift ho gaye hai bade sheher ki gandhgi saaf karne keliye. Chulbul ek bachche ko kidnapers se bachata hai aur un kidnapers ki dhulayi kardeta hai. Pura Kanpur darta hai Thakur Bachcha Singh se. Chulbul Bachcha Singh ke bhai Gendha ko jaan se maardeta hai ladki chchedney ke jurm mai. Iss baat se Bachcha aur uska bhai Chunni til-mila uthey hai aur khoon ka badla khoon se lena chaahte hai. Iss badle ke shikar bantey hai Chulbul ki biwi Rajjo aur CHulbul ka bhai Makkhi, Rajjo ka bachcha girjaata hai chot ke kaaran. Chulbul ke badle ki aag mai Bachcha aur Chunni jal jaatey hai aur ab Kanpur Swach ho chuka hai Pandeyji ki badaulat...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Mannu ) , Sonakshi Sinha( Rajjo ) , Arbaaz Khan( Makkhi ) , Prakash Raj( Bachcha Singh ) , Vinod Khanna( Prajapati ) , Nikitin Dheer( CHunni ) , Deepak Dobriyal( Gaindha ) , Manoj Pahwa( Anand Mathur )

Honest Review



Chulbulgiri bharey action scenes mai mass-appeal hai par Dialogue writing mai kaafi aalas kiya gya hai. Prakash Raj aur Deepak-Dobriyal ne hi poori film mai ACting(Abhinay) ko izzat di hai baaki sabne bas dialogues boley hai. LITTLE BIT OF DISSAPOINTMENT par Gaaney aur Fight Scenes film ko pakau banne se bachaatey hai. A ONE TIME WATCH !!!
