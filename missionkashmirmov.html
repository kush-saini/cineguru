Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Altaf ki jisne apne abba-ammi ko apni aankhon ke saamne Goli-Bari mai Martey huey dekha hai. SSP Inayat Khan Altaf ko apne ghar mai jagah aur betey ka darja deta hai. Altaf jaan Chuka hai ki Inayat ke dwara ki gayi andhi Firing mai hi uske Maa-Baap aur Behen maare gaye thay. Altaf ghar chchodkar bhaag jaata hai. 10 saal beet chukey hai aur Altaaf Aatankiyo keliye kaam karne laga hai. Inayat ko Altaf ke sangathan ke Mission kashmir ke baarey mai Udhti-Khabar mili hai. Apne Pitah ki baat maankar Altaf Mission Kashmir ke operation ko Dhamaake se tehes-nahas karke Achchai ki raah par firse laut aaya hai...
