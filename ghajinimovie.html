Huge Box-Office

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Sanjay Singhania ki jo ek kaamyab businessman hai. Sanjay ek Kalpana naam ki ladki se naraaz hai kyonki Kalpana uske saath apna naam jodkar mashoor bane ki koshish kar rahi hai. Sanjay Kalpana se ladne jaata hai aur uske pyaar mai padh-jaata hai par apni haisiyat ki sachchai nahi batata aur ek struggling actor hone ka naatak karke uska dost banjaata hai. Sanjay Kalpana ko apne dil ki baat bataadeta hai. Kalpana Ghajini ke haatho maari jaati hai aur Sanjay ko bhi Ghajini ke haatho gehri chot pohochti hai jissey ussey short-term memory loss hojaata hai. kalpana ki maut ka badla lena hi ab Sanjay ki zindagi ka maqsad hai. Ghajini ke aadmiyo ki haddiyon ko tod-taa fodtaa Sanjay Ghajini ki ohr badh raha hai. Ghajini aur Sanjay ki ladaai ke thodi der baad Sanjay sab bhul gaya hai, mauke ka faida uthaake Ghajini Sanjay pe ghaatak hamla karta hai, Sanjay ko Kalpana ki dhoondli yaadein aa jaati hai aur vo Ghajini ko ussi berehmi se maarta hai jaisey Kalpana ko maara gaya tha. Sanjay ab dimaagi roop se swasth hai aur Kalpana ki yaadon ko apne mann mai sanjoye jee raha hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Sanjay ) , Asin( Kalpana ) , Pradeep Rawat( Ghajini ) , Jia Khan( Sunita ) , Tinnu Anand( Kalpana's boss ) , Sunil Grover( Cameo ) , Riyaz Khan( Inspector )

Honest Overview



Film mai Aamir kisi ticking time bomb ke jaise lag rahey hai. Action scenes mai vein-popping intensity hai. Aamir apne brilliant abhinay ke zariye ek beraham cold-blooded Psycopath dikhne mai kaamyab huye hai. AN EPIC STORY OF VENGEANCE JO AAPKI ATTENTION KA HAR SECOND DESERVE KARTI HAI !!!
