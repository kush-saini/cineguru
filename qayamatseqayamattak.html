Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak-1988


 6.9 / 10

Directed by Mansoor Khan


Romance Musical


BlockBuster at Box-Office


Aamir Khan and Juhi Chawla's Debut Film

Film ki कहानी


Raj college paas kar chuka hai aur farewell party mai ek khoobsurat gaana gaake sabka entertainment karta hai. Raj ke pitah Dhanraj Singh apne betey se kahi saal baad milke bahut khush hai. Raj kisi zaroori kaam se Dhanakpur gaya hai aur wo ek ladki Rashmi ko gundo se bachaata hai, dono kaafi samay saath mai bitaatey hai aur pyaar kar baithe hai. Raj ke pitah Rashmi ko bata detey hai ki iss janam mai uski aur Raj ki shaadi naamumkin hai kyonki Dhanraj aur Rashmi ke pitah Randhir ki khaandaani dushmani hai. Iss sab se befikar hoke Raj aur Rashmi bhaag jaatey hai apni choti ki pyaar ki duniya basaane. Randhir apni beti ke saamne uske rishtey ko maan-ne ka dhong karta hai aur Raj ko maarne keliye gundey bhej chuka hai. Dhanraj bhi waha pahuch chuka hai, ladai chchid chuki hai aur issi ladai mai Rashmi ko goli lag jaati hai, Raj apne pait mai chaaku ghopke apni Premika se fir miljaata hai aur apne pyaar ko sadaa keliye amar karjaata hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Raj ) , Juhi Chawla( Rashmi ) , Dalip Tahil( Dhanraj ) , Alok Nath( Jaswant ) , Beena Banerjee( Saroj ) , Reema Lagoo( Kamla ) , Makrand Deshpande( Baba ) , Goga Kapoor( Randhir )

Honest Overview



Jab Junoon ki hadein paar hojaati hai toh kya haal hota ? - yehi film mai dikhaya gaya hai. Ye film Modern Heer aur Ranjha ki kahaani hai. Refreshing and Innocent performances by Aamir Khan and Juhi Chawla. A 1000% DEFINITIVE WATCH FOR EVERY BOLLYWOOD DEEWANA/DEEWANI !!!
