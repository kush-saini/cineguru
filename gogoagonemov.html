Go Goa Gone-2013


 7.2 / 10

Directed by Raj and D.K


Action Comedy Zombies


Box Office Success


Bollywood's First Zombie Flick

Film ki कहानी


Hardik aur Luv apni zindagi se pareshan hai aur apne bholey dost Bunny ko behla fusla ke Masti karne Goa le jaatey hai. Luv ko Goa ki Rave Party mai jaane ka invitation milta hai. Teeno Rave Party mai pahuchkar Gaano pe jhoom rahey hai. Agle din uth-kar Teeno apne chaaro taraf Bejaan se logon(Zombies) ko paatey hai jo Zinda logo ke maans ko khaa rahey hai. Unki mulaqaat Boris se hoti hai jiski badaulat teeno Baal baal Zombies se bachtey hai. Bunny Zombies ki ek ajeeb kamzori pata laga leta hai jiski Madad se sab Island se nikalne mai kaamyab hojaatey hai lekin abhi picture baaki hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Boris ) , Kunal Khemu( Hardik ) , Vir Das( Luv ) , Anand Tiwari( Bunny ) , Soha Ali Khan( Cameo ) , Puja Gupta( Luna )

Honest Overview



Ye Movie aapko Definitely GT(Good Trip) degi( If you Know YOU KNOW !). "TRIPPY as HELL". SAIf Ali Khan apne Bizzare(Ajeeb) Accent ke saath Funniest Avatar mai dikhe hai. Ye film Aapki Favourite DISH ke jaisi hai - "You Never Want it to END". Apne CHAI-SUTTA friends aur Bum Bum Bhole Lovers ke saath iss film ko zaroor dekhein. It's One of those Films jiske khatam hotey hi You badly want a Sequel. Ye film hasaaney ke alawa Problem Solving Mindset bhi sikhaati hai ye aap film Film ko bareeki se dekh kar samjhengey. Raj and D.K iss film ke saath Youngsters ke Favourite Director ban jaaengey. 1000% EXTREMELY RECOMMENDED to Every Cinema Lover !!!
