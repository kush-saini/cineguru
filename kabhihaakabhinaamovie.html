Kabhi Haan Kabhi Naa-1994


 8.2 / 10

Directed by Kundan Shah


Romance Comedy


Box Office Success


Filmfare Award for Shahrukh Khan

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Sunil ki. Sunil ek band mai music bajaata hai aur padai-likhai mai uska mann nahi lagta hai. Sunil ke pitah aur sab log ussey naakara samajtey hai siwaye Father Briganza ke jo Sunil mai ek nek-dil aur achcha insaan dekhte hai. Sunil Anna se pyar krta hai aur ussi ke sapne dekhta hai. Anna band mai wapas aa jaati hai aur Chris ko pasand karne lagti hai. Sunil Chris aur Anna ko alag krne ki koshish karta hai aur iss harkat keliye ussey band se nikaal diya jaata hai. Chris aur Anna shaadi karna chahte hai par Chris ke maa-baap iss rishte ko inkaar krdete hai. Anna bahut dukhi hai, Sunil Anna aur Chris ko wapis milwata hai aur jaise-taise unki shaadi pakki krwa deta hai. Dukhi mann ke saath Sunil shaadi mai jaata hai aur shaadi ke baad Sunil ki mulaaqat ek ladki se hoti hai aur ab pyar ka ek naya kissa chaalu ho chuka hai.
