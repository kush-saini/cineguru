Very Unique Storyline

Film ki कहानी


Vikram ek vakeel hai aur apni biwi Kavita aur bete Guddu ke saath rehta hai. Vikram bhagwan ko nahi maanta wahi doosri taraf Kavita bohot pooja-paath krti hai. Guddu aur uski premika Salina ka road pe haadhsa hojaata hai jisme Salina ki aankhon ki roshni chali jaati hai. Salina ke maa-baap Guddu ko kasoorwaar thehraate hai . Guddu Salina ko apni aankhein dena chahta hai par Vikram iss baat se inkaar krdeta hai. Ek din Vikram aur Guddu buri tarah bimaar hokar hospital mai bharti hojaate hai. Kavita bhagwaan ke charno mai girke apne suhaag aur apni aulaad keliye 5 din bina paani piye vrat rakhti hai. Dua rang laati hai aur Vikram-Guddu bach jaate hai par Kavita bhagwaan ko pyaari hojaati hai lekin jaate jaate Kavita Salina ko apni aankhein deke uski zindagi mai roshni kar jaati hai. Guddu aur Salina apne 2 bachcho ke saath khushi-khushi zindagi bitaate hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Guddu ) , Manish Koirala( Salina ) , Deepti Naval( Kavita ) , Mukesh Khanna( Advocate Vikram ) , Ashok Saraf( Baliya )

Honest Overview



Ye film aapko rulaa sakti hai. Shahrukh ke chahne wale iss film ko zaroor dekhe. A good timepaas movie !!
