Ye kahaani hai Raj Malhotra aur Pooja Malhotra ki jinhone apne bachcho Ajay, Sanjay ,Rohit aut Karan ko bade laad-pyaar se paala hai. Bachche ab bade ho chuke aur ek safal zindagi jee rahe hai. Raj ab retirement ke baad apne bachcho ke saath rehna chaahta hai. Bachche mann hi mann nahi chahte maa-baap ko apne saath rakhna aur maa aur baap ko alag-alag bete ke paas rakhne ki tarkeeb sochte hai taaki pareshan hokar Maa-Papa wapis apne puraaney ghar chale jaaye. Raj aur Pooja alag alag rehene lagte hai aur unhe apne bachcho ke ghar pe sab milta hai siwaaey Maa-Baap ke darje aur Aadar-Sammaan ke. Raj aur Pooja kahi mahino baad milte hai aur aisa lagta hai maano dono kabhi bichde hi na ho. Ek anaath bachcha Alok jisko Raj-Pooja ne paala aur aulaad ki taraah chaaha uss din unhe miljaata hai. Alok ki biwi Arpita se milke ve dono behad khush hotey hai. Raj ne Pooja se judaai ke waqt mai apne Aaansooyo ko kalam se panno mai utaara tha jo ki aaj ek bestselling novel "Baghban" ban chuki hai. Baghban ke samman ke liye ek function mai Raj-Pooja jaate hai jaha unke 4 betey unse maafi maangney aa pahuchtey hai par Apne pati ka dukh ek biwi kabhi nahi bhool sakti.
