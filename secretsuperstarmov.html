Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai 15 saal ki Insia jo gaana chaahti hai par apne Abbu ke darr se kuch nahi karpaati. Insia Youtube pe logon ko gaata dekh khud bhi yahi karna chahti hai aur Ammi ke sujhaav se Burkha pehenke chehra dhaktey huey Youtube pe gaaney upload karti hai Secret Superstar ke naam se. Insia ki video viral hojaati hai aur uske paas Controversial Music Director Shakti Kumar ke yaha se gaaney ka offer aata hai. Insia apne dost Chintan ki madad se Shakti Kumar tak pahuch jaati hai ek melodious gaana record karti hai. Insia apne Ammi-Abba ke divorce ke papers tayyar karwaati hai kyonki Abba Ammi ko choti choti baato pe maartey hai. Insia ke Abba Saudi Arabia jaakar naukri karne jaa rahey hai poorey pariwar ko lekar. Airport pe pahuchke apni beti ke sapno keliye Ammi talaaknaame pe sign kardeti hai aur bachcho ko Saath lekar apne pati ko chchod deti hai humesha keliye aur teeno Shakti Kumar ki madad se ek Awards Show mai jaatey hai. Stage pe Monali Thakur Secret Superstar ko stage pe bulaati apna award dene keliye. Insia stage pe pahochkar Award ka asli hakdaar Apni Ammi ko bataake sabke saamne unse kas ke lipat jaati hai aur taaliyon ki gadgadahat se pura Show goonjh uth-taa hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Shakti Kumar ) , Meher Vij( Insia's ammi ) , Zaira Wasim( Insia ) , Raj Arjun( Insia's abba ) , Mona Ambegaonkar( Lawyer ) , Tirth Sharma( Chintan ) , Shaan( Himself ) , Monali Thakur( Herself )

Honest Review



Ye kahaani " Atoot Hausla aur Bekhauf hokar apne Sapno pe Vishwas karne ka zaroori sabak dekar jaati hai". Shakti Kumar ka befikr aur rangeen mijaaz aapko zaroor entertain karega. Meher Vij aur Zaira Wasim ne behad Moving Performances di hai Toh thodey Aansuo(TEARS) keliye tayyar hojaaiye. Bachpan ke pehele pyaar ki innocence ko film mai bakhoobi dikhaya gaya hai. Gripping Screenplay and Direction by Advait Chandan. 1000% STRONGLY RECOMMENDED TO EVERY DAY-DREAMER !!!
