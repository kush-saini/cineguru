 7.5 / 10

Blockbuster

Film ki कहानी


Yashwardhan Raichand apni patni Nandini aur apne bete Rahul aur Rohan ke saath rehta hai . Rahul London se padhai poori karke aata hai aur Chandni Chowk ki ek ladki Anjali se pyaar kar baith-ta hai. Rahul apne pitah se shaadi ki ichcha zaahir karta hai par Yashwardhan ladki hi haisiyat dekh-kar iss rishtey ko thukra deta hai. Rahul ghar-bhaar chodke Anjali se shaadi se karleta hai. Kahi saal beet chuke hai aur Rohan boarding school se wapas ghar aaya hai, ussey pata chalta hai ki Rahul bhaiya ghar chhod-kar jaa chuke hai. Rohan poorey pariwar ko firse milaane ka faisla krta hai. Rohan pata lagata hai ki Rahul ab London mai hai aur padai ke bahane London chala jaata hai aur Anjali ki behen Pooja ka dost banke Rahul aur Anjali ke ghar mai rehne lagta hai. Rohan apne maa-baap ko London bulaake bichde baap-bete ko milwane ka plan karta hai, Nandini apne bete Rahul ko dekh-kar bhaawuk hojaati hai.Saare raz khul chuke hai aur Rohan Rahul se wapas ghar chalne ko kehta hai aur Rahul maan-jaata hai. Yashwardhan aur Rahul ek dusre se apni naraazgi jataate hai aur fir gale lag jaate hai. Yashwardhan Anjali ko apna-leta hai. Saara pariwar mil-julkar khushi khushi jeevan bitaate hai.
