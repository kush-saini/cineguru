Underdog Story

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Ishaan Awasthi ki jo dhang se padh likh nahi paata hai aur humesha school mai khudko punished hi paata hai. Kahi saalo se Ishaan fail ho raha aur uske Teachers bhi ussey pareshaan hai. Ishaan ka bhai Yohan school mai har cheez mai awwal aata hai Sports se lekar padhai tak. Ishaan ke maa-baap ussey pareshan hokar uska daakhila Boarding School mai kara detey hai par waha jaake uska aur bura mazaak udtaa hai. Art teacher Ram Shankar Nikumbh Ishaan mai khud ko dekhta hai aur Ishaan ke maa-baap se milkar unko batata hai Ishaan ke Dyslexia ke baare mai aur unhe bachche ke dheet hone aur takleef mai hone ka fark samjhaata hai. Wapas school pohochkar Ram Ishaan ko padhaaney lagta hai ek alag andaaz mai. Ram Ishaan ki imagination ko bahar leaata hai ek painting competetion ke zariye jisme Ishaan poorey school ke saamne jeet-taa hai. Ishaan ko khoya hua aatm-vishwas vaapas aajata hai. Poorey school mai Ishaan ki drawing ke charchey hai. Ishaan ke maa-baap uski tarakki se bohot khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Ram Shankar Nikumbh ) , Darsheel Safary( Ishaan ) , Tisca Chopra( Ishaan's Maa ) , Vipin Sharma( Ishaan's Papa ) , Tanay Chheda( Rajan ) , Sachet Engineer( Yohaan ) , Meghna Malik( Teacher ) , Sonali Sachdev( English Teacher )

Honest Overview



" Agar Ummeed ka diya jalaya jaaye toh Ujaala kiya sakta hai Ghup andhere mai bhi" ye film humhe sikhaati hai. RAM SHANKAR NIKUMBH ka kirdaar behad relatable hai aur har Adhyapak ke liye ek Adarsh bhi hai. Film dekhte huey emotion ki vishaal-kaey leher aaegi jo aapki palko ko bhiga degi MAA gaaney ke zariye. Ishaan ke imperfection uske kirdaar ko manoranjak banatey hai. " AAP APNA BACHPAN FIR SE JEE SAKTEY HO ISS FILM KE ZARIYE ". A MUST WATCH BEFORE YOU DIE !!!
