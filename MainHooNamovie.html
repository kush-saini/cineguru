Main Hoon Na-2004


 9.2 / 10

Directed by Farah Khan


Action Drama


Super Hit


Based on India-Pakistan

Film ki कहानी


Project Milaap ke tahat Bharat aur Pakistan jung ke kaidiyo ko azaad karke ek nayi dosti ki shuruat karna chahte hai. Raghavan Project Milaap mai rukaavat daalne keliye Major Bakshi par jaanlewa humla kardeta hai , Bakshi ko bachate hue Brigadier Shekhar Sharma ko goli lag jaati hai. Shekhar antim saansey ginta hua apne bete Major Ram ko uske naajaiz hone aur uske sautele bhai aur maa ke baare mai batata hai . Gen Bakshi apni beti Sanju ki rakhwali keliye Ram ko Sanju ke college mai undercover roop se student banke jaane ka order dete hai. Ram Sanju se dosti kar leta hai aur apne sautele bhai Laxman ke ghar mai rehne lagta hai. Laxman aur uski Maa ko iss baat ki khabar nahi hai ki Laxman aur Ram ek hi pitah ki auladey hai. Raghavan college mai professor ke bhes mai aata hai aur ek din poorey College ko bandhi banakar Major Ram se Project Milaap ko rokne ki maang karta hai. Ram apni jaan par khelke students ko bachaleta hai. Ram aur Raghavan mai jumke ladai ho rahi hai, Ram Raghavan ke body par lage bombs ki pin nikaal kar Laxman ki madad se Helicopter par chchad jaata hai, Kuch second baad ek dhamaake ke saath Raghavan maara jaata hai. Project Milaap kaamyab hojaata hai. Laxman aur uski Maa Ram ko pyaar sahit apna-lete hai .

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Ram ) , Sushmita Sen( Chandni ) , Suniel Shetty( Raghavan ) , Amrita Rao( Sanju ) , Zayed Khan( Lucky ) , Kirron Kher( Lucky's mother ) , Naseeruddin Shah( Ram's father )  , Bindu( Mrs. Kakkad ) , Boman Irani( Principal ) , Satish Shah , Murli Sharma( Khan )

Honest Overview



Farah Khan ne college ko ek khoobsurat sapne jaisa dikhaya hai . Film mai badiya humour aur dhasoo action hai. Sabhi gaaney ek se badhke ek hai . Suniel Shetty ne behtreen acting ki hai. Kam shabdo mai bole toh " Kaafi Teekhi Aur Chatpati " movie . Definitely Watch this movie before you die !!!
