Jolly LLB 2-2017


 6.9 / 10

Directed by Subhash Kapoor


CourtRoom Comedy


SuperHit


Justice Prevails again

Film ki कहानी


Jolly ek chaalu aur chotey-motey kaam karke paise kamaane waala Vakeel hai. Jolly ki beimaani ki wajah se Hina marjaati hai jo apne pati ko insaaf dilaana chahti thi. Iss baat se Jolly ke naam par khoob keechad uchalta hai. Apne zameer aur Insaniyat ke khaatir Jolly Hina ka case ladne lagta hai. Opposition ka Vakeel Pramod Mathur badi shaatir cheez hai. Jolly ki har koshish ko Pramod apne tajurbey se naakam karta jaa raha hai ki tabhi Jolly apna ek aakhri gawah pesh karta hai jissey Saara paasa palat jaata hai aur case jeet kar Jolly logo ki aankon mai apne liye izzaat firse dekh paa raha hai. Aakhir kaar Jolly Hina ke shauhar ko insaaf dilwane mai kaamyab hua...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Jolly ) , Saurabh Shukla( Sunderlal ) , Huma Qureshi( Pushpa ) , Annu Kapoor( Pramod ) , Kumud Mishra( Suryaveer ) , Manav Kaul( Iqbal Qasim ) , Inaamulhaq( Iqbal Qadri ) , Sanjay Mishra( Guruji ) , Sayani Gupta( Hina )

Honest Review



"Doobi hui Izzat aur khoye huey chayn ko firse paaya jaa sakta hai Atoot Sankalp aur Gagan-Chumbhi Hausley ke saath" ye film humhe sikhaati hai. Film mai Rishvat khori aur Aatankvad jaise gambhir mudhdho par roshni daali gayi hai par ek Vyanga(Satire) ke saath. Pehli film jaisa Nervous Excitement create nahi kar paayi hai ye movie parantu Unexpected Twists & Turns ki badaulat aapko Entertain karegi zaroor. Purey parivar ke saath dekhiye. 1000% STRONGLY Recommended to Akshay Kumar fans !!!
