International Khiladi-1999


 5.4 / 10

Directed by Umesh Mehra


Action Suspense Drama


Average Box Office


Shot in Canada

Film ki कहानी


Payal Cameraman Focus ke saath UnderWorld Don Devraj ka Interview lene ka naamumkin kaam karne ka thaan chuki hai. Payal aur Focus badi mehnat ke saath Devraj ka interview lene mai kaamyab hojaate hai. Devraj ko Payal ka ziddipana aur bholapan bhaa jaata hai aur Payal bhi Devraj ko chaahne lagti hai. Payal ka dost Amit uske mann mai Devraj ke khilaaf nafrat bhardeta hai. Payal ka Bhai Ravi maara jaata hai aur uski maut ka badla lene keliye Payal Devraj pe apne rape ka jhootha case banati hai. Adaalat Devraj ko faansi ki sazaa sunaati hai. Devraj jail se bahar nikalne mai kaamyab hojaata hai. Devraj Payal ko sach batata hai ki usne Ravi ko nahi maara hai. Amit aur DOn Thakraal ne milke Devraj ko fasaya hai ye baat Devraj ko Amit aur THakraal hi batatey hai. Amit Thakraal ko maarke Devraj ko zakhmi kardeta hai. Devraj Amit ko maarke Payal ko bacha leta hai. Devraj aur Payal shaadi karke sukoon se apni zindagi ji rahe hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Devraj ) , Twinkle Khanna( Payal ) , Rajat Bedi( Amit ) , Vivek Shauq( Ravi ) , Gulshan Grover( Thakral ) , Mukesh Khanna( Bismillah ) , Johnny Lever( Focus ) , Asrani( Payal's boss ) , Avtar Gill( Vakeel ) , Shahbaz Khan( Commissioner )

Honest Overview



Payal ki Chanchal Bekhauf adaa aapko pasand aaegi. FIlm ke Action Scenes OUTSTANDING hai. Devraj ka kirdaar Buraai ki aandhi mai jaltey huey Achchai ke deepak jaisa hai. UNDOUBTEDLY film ke sabse Fascinating Scenes hai THAKRAAL ki kadak awaaz mai DIALOGUE-BAAZI. Film mai Charlie's Angels ka Cameo miss mat karna, Ye baat aap film dekh-kar samajh jaengey aur zaroor hasengey. Poori Film mai RAJAT BEDI ne BEHETREEN performance di hai, Simply Marvellous. FIlm mai JUST ENOUGH SUSPENSE HAI AAPKO ENGAGE RAKHNE KELIYE. 1000% RECOMMENDED TO DIE-HARD AKSHAY KUMAR FANS !!!
