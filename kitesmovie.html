Film ki कहानी


Jay LasVegas mai rehne wala ek Dancer hai aur paiso keliye nakli shaadiya karta hai apne dost Robin ki madad se. Jay apni student Gina(Beti of Bob Grover) se pyaar ka natak kar raha hai paiso keliye. Gina ka bhai Tony apni biwi Natasha se Jay ko milwataa hai par Natasha unhi ladkiyo mai se ek thi jissey Jay ne nakli shaadi ki thi. Natasha aur Jay ek dusre se pyaar karne lagtey hai aur Casino se paise loot ke bhaag jaatey hai. Natasha ke gao pahuchkar dono Shaadi karletey hai par Kismat unn dono ko alag kardeti hai par kaise ye jaan-ney keliye film dekhein...

Film ke कलाकार

Hrithik Roshan( Jay ) , Barbara Mori( Linda ) , Kangana Ranaut( Gina ) , Kabir Bedi( Bob ) , Anand Tiwari( Robin )

Honest Overview



Film mai Pehle Pyaar ke (Sparsh)Touch aur Passion(Junoon) ko perfectly Camera mai kaidh kiya gaya hai. Kabhi apne Life-Partner ke saath Fight(Jhagda) ho toh iss Film ko zaroor dekhein (Nazdeekiyan fir se badh jaengi) You will End Up Holding Hands.
