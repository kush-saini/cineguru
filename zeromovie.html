Film ki कहानी


Bauaa kadh mai chota hai jiski wajah se uski shaadi nahi ho rahi hai. Aafiya naam ki ladki Bauaa ko pasand aa jaati hai. Shaadi taha ho chuki hai par Bauaa shaadi ki raat hi Aafiya ko chchodke Babita Kumari se milne bhag aata hai. Babita Bauaa ko apne paas kaam pe rakh leti hai par ek din Bauaa Babita ke mehemaano se badtameezi karta hai aur Babita Bauaa ko ghar se nikaal deti hai. Bauaa Aafiya se milne New York chala jaata hai aur ussey pata chalta hai ki Aafiya ki shaadi ho rahi hai, Aafiya se maafi maagne ke liye Bauaa Mars pe chala jaata hai. Aafiya ne shaadi nahi kari aur kahi saalo se Bauaa ke lautne ka intezaar kar rahi hai. Bauaa abhi kisi samudra mai land kiya hai aur zinda hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Bauaa ) , Anushka Sharma( Aafia ) , Katrina Kaif( Babita ) , Mohammad Zeeshan Ayub( Bauaa's friend ) , Sheeba Chaddha( Bauaa's mother ) , Thigmanshu Dhulia( Bauaa's father ) , Brijendra Kala( Agent )

Honest Overview



Film ke Visual Effects aur Music next-level hai. Film bohot cute aur funny hai par 2nd half mai rail patri se utar chuki hai. Aanand.L Rai ko koshish karne ke liye purey 100 number diye jaane chahiye. OVERALL MOVIE VISUALLY BEHAD KHOOBSURAT HAI !!!
