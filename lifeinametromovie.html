Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani se milkar bani hai jo aapas mai judi hui hai. Shikha aur Ranjit ki shaadi-shudha zindagi mai sab kuch hai bas Pyaar ke wahi dusri taraf Shikha ki behen Shruti apne perfect Humsafar ko dhoondh rahi hai, Shruti ka dost Monty ussey zindagi khul ke jeena sikhata hai. SHikha ka bhi affair chal raha hai. Shruti ki roommate Neha aur Shruti ke Jijaji ka affair chal raha hai par Shruti office ke ladke Rahul ko chahne lagi hai. Shikha ki Aunty Vridh-Aashram se bhaag gayi apne jawani ke pyaar Amol ke saath. In sabhi kahaniyo mai kisi ka pyaar chchin jaata hai aur kisi ko Mil jaata hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Irrfan Khan( Monty ) , Konkona Sen( Shruti ) , Shilpa Shetty( Shikha ) , Kay Kay Menon( Ranjeet ) , Kangana Ranaut( Neha ) , Sharman Joshi( Rahul ) , Nafisa( Shivani ) , Dharmendra( Amol ) , Shiney Ahuja( Akash ) , Manoj Pahwa( Doctor )

Honest Overview



For all those jinhe Safar(Journey) aur Raasto ki tanhaai se Love(Mohabbat) hai, iss film ke Songs Solo Trip ke waqt Soul-Searching(Man ke andar jhakne) keliye Definitely Suney jaa sakte hai. Movie ke dialogues aapki Waah-Waah deserve kartey hai. Film Pyaar aur Regret(Pachtave) ko bakhoobhi ENCAPSULATE(Google plz it's a Cool word) karti hai. VERDICT-" A MasterPiece jo aapke STRESS ko Vanish(Gayab) kar sakti hai". A Must Watch Before you DIE !!!
