Hum Tum-2004


 7.9 / 10

Directed by Kunal Kohli


Romance Comedy


Box Office Success


Beautifully Knit Story

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Karan Kapoor ki. Karan ek Cartoonist hai aur ladkiyo ko Use & Throw Ball Pen ki tarah samajhta hai. Ek flight ke dauraan Karan ki mulaaqat usoolwaadi Rhea se hoti hai, Dono achche dost ban jaatey hai aur Saath mai Amsterdam bhi ghoom rahey hai jabtak Karan ek badtameezi karke Rhea ke haatho ek karaara tamaacha nahi kha leta hai. Kuch saal baad firse Rhea aur Karan ki mulaqat hoti hai. Rhea ka pati Sameer ab iss duniya mai nahi hai aur Rhea behad akeli hai, Karan Rhea ko firse jeena sikhaa-deta hai aur dono ka ek-dusre keliye pyaar badhta hee jaa raha hai. Kismat ko shayad ye milan manzoor nahi tha aur Karan ab kahi saal baad apni kitaab Hum-Tum ke udhghaatan par Rhea se fir miljaata hai aur dono premi ek dusre ko baaho mai bhar letey hai. Karan aur Rhea shaadi karke behad khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Karan ) , Rani Mukherji( Rhea ) , Kirron Kher( Bobby ) , Jimmy Shergill( Mihir ) , Rishi Kapoor( Arjun ) , Isha Koppikar( Diana ) , Rati Agnihotri( Anju ) , Abhishek Bachchan( Sameer )

Honest Overview



Ye film XX aur XY chromosome( Google if upar se gaya...) ke Beech ke farq ko apne Creatively Animated Visuals ke zariye darshaati hai. " Pyaar raasta dhoondh hi leta hai Waqt ki anginat(Uncountable) saazisho ke baavjudh bhi " film ye sikhaati hai. Karan ka kirdaar Charismatic Gentleman aur Selfish Playboy ka perfect embodiment hai aur Har Ladka khudhko Karan se relate kar sakta hai on a very personal Level. Delicious Screenplay aur Attractive performances by everyone. 1000% RECOMMENDED to every Couple !!!
