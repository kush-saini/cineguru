Film ki कहानी


Sapna apne 3 maamao ke saath rehti hai Vicky mama, Bhola mama aur Prabhu mama. Teeno hi ekdum anokhe kiradaar hai . In teeno ki khushi keliye Sapna ke liye aane wale rishtey kabhi kaamyab nahi hotey kyunki ek ko ladka pasand aaya toh dusre mama ko dikkat , dusre ko pasand aaya toh teesre ko dikkat. Isi wajah se Sapna kunwari baithi hai. Sapna Europe ki trip pe chali jaati hai aur waha uski mulaqat Raja se hoti hai. Trip ke shuruat mai Sapna ko Raja bada ajeeb sa lagta hai par dheere-dheere thoda apna sa lagne lagta hain aur Pyaar dono ki zindagiyo ko mila deta hai. Ab Raja ko India jaakar saare maamao ko impress karna hai, Kaise hoga ye sab par aashiqui ke sapne dekhe hai toh kurbaaniya bhi deni padengi aur aise hi Raja ne teeno maamo ko pata liya. Raja aur Sapna ab shaadishuda hai aur khush bhi .

Honest Overview



Kamaal ka music composition aur peeche dilkash foreign locations- Deadly Combo . Karishma aur Salman ki onscreen jodi amazing hai. Bhaijaan ke fans ko film bahut pasand aaegi. Great movie to watch timepass keliye. Masala entertainer David Dhawan style !!!
