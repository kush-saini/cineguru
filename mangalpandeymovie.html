Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani 1857 ki sachchi ghatnao pe aadharit hai aur Mangal Pandey iss kahaani ka mukhya kirdaar hai OBVI0USLY. Mangal British Sena mai ek Sipahi hai aur Angrezo se nafrat karta hai sivaey Captain Gordon ke jiski jaan usne kuch saal pehle ek Jaanleva ladai ke dauraan bachaai thi. East India India Company sipahiyo ke liye naye hathyar laayi hai. Naye hathyaaro mai Maas ka istemaal hone ki khabar Jungle mai Aag ki tarah gao mai fail chuki hai. Mangal Company ke khilaaf Vidhroh kardeta hai jiskeliye ussey Sazaa-e-Maut milti hai. Apne aakhri palo mai bhi Mangal ki aankho mai khauf ka koi naamo-nishaan bhi nahi hai. Purey gao ke saamne Mangal Pandey ko Ped se latka-kar Faasi dedi jaati hai. Mangal ki maut Angrezo ke khilaaf ek bade ANDOLAN ki shuruat hoti hai...
