Love Aaj Kal-2009


 6.8 / 10

Directed by Imtiaz Ali


Romance Comedy Drama


Box Office Hit


Saif Ali Khan in Double Role

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Jai aur Meera ki. Dono London mai rehte hai aur apne rishtey se behad khush hai par Jai ke sapne ussey Meera se alag kardetey hai. Jai San Fransisco mai apni dream job paakar bhi khush nahi hai. Jai ki mulaaqat ek Sardaarji Veer Singh ji se hoti hai, Veer ji Jai ko apni mohabbat ke qissey sunaatey hai aur uske Dil mai Meera keliye pyaar ko firse jagah detey hai. Meera ki shaadi ho chuki hai. Meera apne pati Vikram ko sab sach batadeti hai. Vikram Meera ko khush dekhna chaahta hai isliye Ussey Talaak leleta hai. Jai India pahuchkey apne Sapno ki raani Meera se Milkar Pyaar ka izhaar karta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Jai/Veer ) , Deepika Padukone( Meera ) , Rishi Kapoor( Old Veer ) , Neetu Singh( Old Harleen ) , Giselli Monteiro( Harleen ) , Rahul Khanna( Vikram ) , Vir Das( Shonty )

Honest Overview



Ye film 70s ke dheemi aanch pe pakne wale Pyaar ki khoobsurati aur 2000s ki Timeline ke Instant Mobile Recharge jaise Pyaar ki Unstability dikhaati hai aur "Chaahe Zamaana koi saa bhi ho Pyaar na kabhi badhla hai aur naa kabhi badhlega" film mai bakhoobhi dikhaya gaya hai.
