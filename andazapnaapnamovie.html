Film ki कहानी


Amar aur Prem dono apna ghar chchodke ek romaanchak safar pe nikle hai pyaar aur paise ki talaash mai, Dono ek hi ladki Raveena se shaadi karna chaahtey hai. Amar aur Prem ek dusre se ek kadam aage rehne ki koshish karne ki koshish kartey hai par thodey samay baad dost banjaatey hai. Amar Raveena se aur Prem Karishma se pyaar karne lagta hai. Amar-Prem Badmaash Teja aur Crime Master Gogo ko kanoon ke hawale karke Teja ke bhai Ram ko insaaf dilaatey hai aur aakhir mai apni premikao se shaadi karletey hai.
