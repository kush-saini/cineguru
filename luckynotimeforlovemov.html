Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Lucky ki jo Russia mai rehne wali ek Indian ladki hai. School se ghar aate waqt ek din Lucky baal-baal Gundo se bachti hai aur tabhi uski mulakaat hoti hai Adi se jiski gaadi mai wo bina permission ke baith gayi hai. Adi apni gaadi se bohot lagaav rakhta hai. Adi aur Lucky ek aatankwadi hamle se bachtey-bachaate huye hazaaro musibato se ladtey huye aage badh rahe hai aur Colonel ki madad se wapis India apne pariwaro ke paas pahuchne ko tayyar hai. Lucky aur Adi apni-apni zindagiyo mai wapas jaane se pehle ek dusre ke ho-gaye hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Adi ) , Sneha Ullal( Lucky ) , Mithun Chakraborty( Colonel ) , Kader Khan( Doctor ) , Ravi Baswani( Lucky's father ) , Vikram Gokhale( Sekhri ) , Meher Vij( Lucky's friend )

Honest Review



Film ke gaaney suroo se labaa-lab bharey hai aur film THAKELe dialogues se. Film ki locations BREATH-TAKINGLY BEAUTIFUL hai aur film ko thoda EXCITING banati hai. BAS EK BAAR film ko dekha jaa sakta hai for its VISUAL SPLENDOUR alone!!
