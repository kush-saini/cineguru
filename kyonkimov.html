Kyon Ki-2005


 5.7 / 10

Directed by Priyadarshan


Romance Drama


Decent Box Office


Heart Breaking Story

Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Anand ki jo ek mental institute mai ek patient hai. Dr Sunil aur Dr Tanvi patients ko pyaar se theek karna chaahte hai par Tanvi pe papa aur Senoir Doctor Khurana patients ke saath sakht bartaav rakhte hai discipline keliye. Sunil Tanvi ko Anand ki diary padhne ko deta hai. Diary ke panno ki zubaani - Anand maya naam ki ek ladki ko chaahta tha. Bahut kehne ke baad Maya ussey haa kardeti hai. Maya ko mazaak karne ki aadat hai. EK din Maya ki mazaak karne ki aadat aur Anand ki laaparwaahi se Maya doob ke marjaati hai. Anand ye sadma bardaasht nahi kar paata aur apna dimaagi santulan kho baith-taa hai. Tanvi diary bandh karti hai aur Anand se uski kareebiyan badh jaati hai. Anand ko bhi Tanvi mai Maya dikhti hai. Dono ek dusre ke pyaar mai hai par Khurana ne Tanvi ke liye Karan ko chuna hai. Anand ka paagalpan badhta hi jaata hai, Sunil apne dost Anand ko iss haalat mai nahi dekh sakta aur takhiye se uski saansein rok kar Anand ko mukti de deta hai. Tanvi Anand ki maut ka sadma nahi jhel paati aur paagal hokar ab usi asylum ki patient hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Anand ) , Jackie Shroff( Dr Sunil ) , Kareena Kapoor( Dr Tanvi ) , Suniel Shetty( Karan ) , Rimi Sen( Maya ) , Om Puri( Dr Khurana ) , Manoj Joshi( Narayan ) , Asrani( Patient ) , Kurush Deboo( Patient )

Honest Overview



"Har pyaar ki happy ending nahi hoti" , kahi pyaar adhoorey bhi reh jaatey hai iss movie mai dikhaya gaya hai. Film ke gaaney ek anokha contrast create kartey hai film ke theme se. Salman Khan ne BRILLIANT acting ki hai. Salman ke fans ko unka ek alag roop dekhne ko milega. Overall THODI SAD par Pyaari Film. 1000% recommended to everyone jinhe thodi hatke films dekhne ka shauk hai!!!
