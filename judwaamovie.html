Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ACP Malhotra ke beto ki - Prem aur Raja . Bachpan mai Raja ko Ratanlal Tiger gundey ne kidnap karliya tha jisse wo anaath ho gaya. Raja bade hoke gali ka gunda ban gya jo apne dost Rangeela ke saath awaara-gardi karta ghumta hai. Raja ek anaath ladki ko apni behen bana leta hai. Tiger Raja ki behen ko chchedh deta hai. Raja apni behen ka badla lene keliye Tiger ko buri tara dhota hai. Kismat Raja ko uske Bhai Prem se milwa deti hai. Tiger Raja se badla lene keliye ACP Malhotra ko kidnap karleta hai. Prem aur Raja Tiger se ladkar apne baap ko bacha -letey hai aur ab Malhotra ko apna khoya hua pariwar mil chuka hai. Sab khushi-khushi apni zindagi bitaatey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Raja/Prem ) , Karishma kapoor( Mala ) , Rambha( Roopa ) , Dalip Tahil( Prem/Raj's father ) , Reema Lagoo( Prem/Raj's mother ) , Kader Khan( Mala's father ) , Shakti Kapoor( Rangeela ) , Anupam Kher( Inspector ) , Mukesh Rishi( Tiger )
