Dilwale-2015


 5.9 / 10

Directed by Rohit Shetty


Action Comedy Romance


Box Office Success


Shot in Bulgaria

Film ki कहानी


Dev Malik aur Randhir Bakshi rival gang ke leader hai aur ek dusre ki jaan ke dushman bhi. Bakshi ka beta Raj Malik ki beti Meera se pyaar karne lagta hai. Raj ka gold consignment raaste mai chori hojaata hai Meera ke dwara, Raj keliye Meera ka pyar bas ek dhoka tha. Kuch dino baad Raj Meera ko marne se bachake kabhi na milne ka wada karta hai. Kahi saal beet chuke hai aur Raj ek car modification ki dukaan chalata hai aur seedhi-saadhi zindagi bitah raha hai. Raj ka chota bhai Veer ko Ishita naam ki ladki se pyaar hojata hai jo ki Meera ki behen hai. Veer-Ishita apne rishtey ko bachane ke liye Raj-Meera ko fir se kareeb leaatey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Raj ) , Kajol( Meera ) , Kriti Sanon( Ishita ) , Varun Dhawan( Veer ) , Vinod Khanna( Raj's father ) , Kabir Bedi( Dev Malik ) , Varun Sharma( Sidhu ) , Boman Irani( King )

Honest Overview



Funny dialogues aur Behetreen Rohit Shetty level ka action. Shahrukh-Kajol ki iconic jodi par ek twist ke saath. Family Entertainer with great music !!!
