Film ki कहानी


Akshay apne Bhai Ajay ke paas Canada ussey milna jaa raha hai. Canada pahuchke ussey pata chalta hai Ki Underworld Don Madam Maya ne Ajay ko gaayab karwa dia hai. Akshay Madam Maya ki jaan bachakar unka vishwas jeet leta hai. Maya Akshay ko chaahne lagti hai par Akshay Maya ki behen Priya se pyaar karta hai. Akshay Maya ke aadmiyo ko maarke apne Bhai Ajay ko bachaake le jaa raha hai ki tabhi Madam Maya Akshay pe bandook taan deti hai par Priya Akshay ke saamne aajaati hai Akshay ko bachaane keliye. Maya khudko goli maarke Priya ka haath Akshay ke haath mai thamaati hui dum tod-deti hai. Akshay apne pyaar aur pariwar dono ko leke wapas India chala aaya hai...

Film ke कलाकार
