Majhdhaar-1996


 5.9 / 10

Directed by Esmayeel Shroff

Flop at Box Office


Malvika Tiwari's Bollywood Debut

Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai teen dosto ki :- Krishna, Radha aur Gopal. Kaamyabi Gopal ke kadam choomti hai. Gopal ki shaadi uske bachpan ke pyaar Radha se hojaati hai. Radha ab pait se hai aur Gopal baap banne ki khushi mai aajkal jhoomta rehta hai. Ek pyaari gudiya Radha aur Gopal ke pariwaar ka hissa ban chuki hai. Ek din Shanti ghar se gayab hojaati hai. Gopal ko kuch din baad Shanti se hi pata lagta hai ki uski beti Shanti aur Krishna ke najayaz rishtey ki nishani hai. Gopal ke pairo taley maano zameen khisak jaati hai. Deen-duniya chchodkar Gopal humesha keliye apni purani zindagi se naata tod deta hai. Kahi saal beet chukey hai aur Gopal aakhri saansey gin raha hai . Krishna apni beti Shanti ko Gopal se milwata hai aur Gopal ko hausla deta hai par maut Gopal ko gale laga hi leti hai. Krishna apne dost ki chitaa ko agni detey huey gumgeen hai. Yaha kahaani khatm hoti hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Gopal ) , Manisha Koirala( Radha ) , Rahul Roy( Krishna ) , Malvika Tiwari( Shanti )

Honest Overview



Yeh film dosti aur pyaar mai insaan ke tyaag ko darshaati hai. Gopal jaisi mahaan shaksiyat ab dekhne ko kaha milti hai. Manisha koirala ne superb acting ki hai. Ye film kaafi under-rated hai. Agar aapko dramatic filme pasand hai toh jaldi se isko youtube pe jaake dekhiye. Gaano ke lyrics aapko behad pasand aaengey. In short A Must Watch for its dhasoo acting. 1000% recommended !!!
