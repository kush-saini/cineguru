Patthar ke Phool-1991


 6.1 / 10

Directed by Anant Balani


Action Romance


Box Office Success


Raveena Tandon's Bollywood Debut

Film ki कहानी


Inspector Vijay Verma abhi Underworld king Balraj ka case lad raha hai. Vijay ka beta Suraj Balraj ki beti Kiran se pyar karne lagta hai aur iss baat se Vijay naakhush hai. Balraj ka saathi Goga uske mana karne ke baavjood bhi Vijay par hamla karwa deta hai. Vijay ab iss duniya mai nahi, Suraj apne pitah ki tarah ek Inspector ban chuka hai aur Balraj ka case leta hai. Goga Balraj ki jagah lena chahta hai aur Kiran ko kidnap karleta hai. Suraj aur Balraj Kiran ko bachaney pahuchtey hai. Suraj sabko maar giraata hai aur Kiran ko bacha-leta hai. Balraj police ko surrender kardeta hai. Suraj-Kiran ab saath hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Suraj ) , Raveena Tandon( Kiran ) , Vinod Mehra( Vijay ) , Reema Lagoo( Suraj's mother ) , Manohar Singh( Balraj ) , Goga Kapoor( Goga ) , Kiran Kumar

Honest Overview



Film ke gaaney " Kabhi Tu Chalia Lagta Hai " aur " Tumse Jo Dekhte Hi Pyar Hua " aapko kaafi pasand aengey. Salman ke fans iss movie ko zaroor dekhiye. HIGHLY RECOMMENDED !!!
