Biwi No.1 -1999


 7.1 / 10

Directed by David Dhawan


Comedy Family


Box Office Success


Nominated for many awards

Film ki कहानी


Prem ek kaamyab businessman hai aur uski biwi hai Pooja aur do bachche bhi hai. Pooja bohot bholi aur apne pati ko sant samajhti hai. Prem apni company ki ek model Rupali pe lattu hojata hai aur Pooja se jhooth bolkar Rupali se saath videsh ghoomne chala jaata hai. Prem ka dost Lakhan aur uska pariwar bhi waha pe hotey hai. Wapas India pahuchkar Lakhan ka beta ek tape chala deta hai jisme Prem Rupali ko gale laga raha hota hai. Prem jhoot bolkar Pooja ko manaleta hai par Karvachauth ki raat ko Pooja Prem ko Rupali ke saath rungey haath pakad leti hai. Prem ab Roopali ke saath rehta hai par kuch samay mai usey maashuka aur biwi ke beech ka farq samaj aajata hai aur wo Pooja se maafi maangta hai. Pooja badappan se usey maaf kardeti hai. Rupali Prem ko chchod deti hai aur apne purane premi ke paas chali jaati hai. Kahaani khatam , The End .

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Karishma Kapoor( Pooja ) , Sushmita Sen( Rupali ) , Anil Kapoor( Lakhan ) , Tabu( Lovely ) , Himani Shivpuri( Prem's mommy )

Honest Overview



Iss film mai dikhaya gaya hai ki Hindustani biwi agar rui(cotton) ki tarah (naazuk)soft ho sakti hai toh patthar ki tarah sakht bhi ho sakti hai. Iss film ki asli hero Karishma Kapoor hai jinhone bemisaal acting ki hai. Film ka background music(score) bahut hi pyara hai. Please Parivar ke saath iss film ko dekhiye. 1000% recommended !!!
