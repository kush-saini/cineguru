Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Ajay Sharma ki. Ajay ki zindagi ka maqsad hai Madan Chopra se apne baap ki maut ka badla lena. Chopra ki do betiyan hai Seema aur Priya. Ajay Seema ke dil mai jagah bana leta hai aur Vicky Malhotra banke Priya ko pyaar ke jaal mai fasaa leta hai.Ajay Seema ke pyar ki pariksha lene keliye usse suicide note likhwata hai.Seema ka sachcha pyar dekh kar Ajay seema ko agle din shaadi krne keliye Registrar office bula leta hai.Ajay Seema ko office building ki chchat se dhakel deta hai aur building se bahar nikalta hua logo ki bheed mai gayab hojata hai.Ajay Seema ka suicide note Chopra ko bhejta hai.Ghum mai dooba Chopra Inspector Karan se case band krne ko kehta .Priya ke kehne par Karan case ki tehqeeqat chalu rakhta hai. Vicky aur Priya ki shaadi hojaati hai. Apne raaz ko raaz rakhne keliye Ajay Seema ki dost Anjali ko maarke nadi mai fek deta hai. Chopra kuch dino keliye Vicky ko apni company ka maalikana haq de deta hai.Vicky aur Priya ki mulaakat asli Vicky Malhotra se hoti hai jo Ajay ka dost hai. Ajay Vicky ko pehchcanne se mana kardeta hai kyonki Vicky Ajay ko uske asli naam se pukaarta hai.Priya ko shaq hone lagta hai.Chopra ko wapis aake pata chalta hai Ajay ne uski company ko hadap liya hai. Priya Vicky ke ghar pahuchti hai aur Vicky ka sach jaanke uske hosh udd jaate hai. Vicky/Ajay waha pauch-kar Priya ko sab batata hai ki kis tarah Chopra ne Ajay ke baap ko dhoka dekar unki company hadapli thi, Ajay ke baap aur behen sadhme mai chal basey aur Ajay ki maa dimagi roop se bimaar hogayi. Priya ko apne baap ki galtiyon ka ehsaas hota hai. Chopra vaha pahuchta hai aur Ajay pe goli chala deta hai, Isi ladai ke beech Ajay ki maa ko chchot lg jaati hai. Gussey mai pagal Ajay Chopra ko marne daudhta hai. Apni sharir mai ghusi hui rod nikaal-kar vo Chopra ki jaan leleta hai. Ab Ajay ka maqsad poora ho chuka hai. Adhmara Ajay apni maa ki godh mai humesha ke liye sukoon se gehri din mai sojaata hai.

Honest Overview



Aisi filmein sadiyon mai ekbaar likhi aur banayi jaati hai. Shahrukh ne seene mai badle ki aag liye Ajay ka roop bakhoobi nibhaya hai. Film ke gaane kamaal hai. This is a definite movie to watch before you die !!
