Film ki कहानी


Inspector Amar shaanti se ek bus mai travel kar raha ha ki tabhi uss bus mai kuch badmaash ghus jaatey hai, Amar chalaaki se unn gundo ko bus se nikaal deta hai. Bus kuch der baad naashtey-paani keliye ruk jaati hai. Paas hi mai Amar ek gaadi pe Hamla rok deta hai sabhi badmaasho ko maar kar aur khatarnak Raghu ko arrest karleta hai. Vo gaadi Chief Minister ki hoti hai. Chief Minister Amar se impress hokar ussey ek naye Cash Fraud ke mission par bhejdetey hai. Fraud ka doshi Chaubey Polce Commisioner ki beti ko maarne ke jurm mai Amar ko jail bhijwa deta hai jaha uska dushman Raghu uska intezaar kar raha hai. Raghu se ladaai ke baad Amar jail se bhaag niklata hai aur Chaubey ko maut ke ghaat utaar-deta hai. Chief Minister Amar pe lagey saare ilzaamat hatakar ussey Sammaanit kartey hai...
