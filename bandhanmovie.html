Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Raju aur Pooja ki jo ki bhai-behen hai. Paiso se woh gareeb hai par dil ke bahut ameer hai. Pooja apne bhai ke liye tyaag karti hai aur Raju bhi apni didi ka bhakt hai. Thakur Suraj Pratap ko Pooja pehli hi nazar mai bhaa jaati hai aur Shaadi teh ho jaati hai . Pooja apne Bhai ko Thakur ji ki anumati se sasural lejaati hai. Raju ab jawaan ho chuka hai aur Apne jijaji ki bohot izzat karta hai aur unki shaan Mai kisi ki gustaakhi Nahi jhel Sakta. Vaishali naam ki ek nachaniya Thakur ji ko apne husn se rijha leti hai aur Thakur ji ussey shaadi krletey hai kyonki Pooja ek bhaanj hai aur Thakur ji ko apna vaaris chaahiye. Sooraj badi mushkil se apni behen ke rishtey ko bachane mai kaamyaab hojaata hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Raju ) , Jackie Shroff( Thakur ) , Rambha( Thakur's behen ) , Ashwini Bhave( Pooja ) , Shakti Kapoor( Billu ) , Ashok Saraf( Chillu ) , Mukesh Rishi( Gajendra ) , Shweta Menon( Vaishali ) , Aasif Sheikh( Inspector )
