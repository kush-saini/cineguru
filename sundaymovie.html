Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Sehar ki jissey bhulney ki bimaari hai. Sehar apni maa ke kehne par Inspector Rajveer se mil rahi. Dono mai dosti ho gayi hai. Sehar se saath ajeebo-gareeb haadhse ho rahe hai - Gundey uska peecha kar rahe hai aur Driver Ballu aur uska dost Mangat ussey kiraaya maangey jaa rahey hai. Sehar apni bhulaaey huey din ko yaad karne keliye Rajveer ki madad leti hai. Rajveer ke tez dimaag ki badaulat Sheher ka Khoonkhaar khooni pakda jaata hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Ajay Devgn( Rajveer ) , Ayesha Takia( Sehar ) , Irrfan Khan( Mangat ) , Arshad Warsi( Ballu ) , Anjana Sukhani( Ritu ) , Murli Sharma( Cameo ) , Ali Asgar( Minister ka Beta )

Honest Overview



Iss film ko na Dimaag se naa Dil se balki Pyaar(Love) se dekha jaana chahiye kyonki film mai kuch Pal(Moments) aate hai jab Actors keliye aapka Pyaar hi aapko movie jhelne ki Strength(Himmat) dega. BITCOIN ke Price(Daam) ki tarah film mai ENTERTAINMENT ka Level bahut Tezzi(Speed) se Upar-Neeche hota rehta hai. VERDICT-" Deserves a CHANCE". 1000% Mildy RECOMMENDED to Ajay-Arshad-Irrfan fans !!!
