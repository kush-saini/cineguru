Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai ek anaath Ram Jaane ki. Ram Jaane apne dost Murli ke saath mai chori karta hua pakda jaata hai aur Inspector Chewte usey jail mai daal deta hai. Jail se bahar nikal ke Ram Jaane Sameer Sanwla ke liye kaam karne lagta hai. Inspector Chewte Sameer ko maar deta hai aur Ram Jaane ko firse jail mai patak kar khoob peet-tah hai .Jail se nikalne par Murli Ram Jaane ko Apna Ghar naam ke ek anaathalaya lejaata hai jaha Ram Jaane apne bachpan mai bichdi dost Bela se milta hai aur pyar karne lagta hai. Baweja ke aadmi Apna Ghar par hamla kardete hai, Ram Jaane sab ko bahchake Baweja ko maut ke ghaat utaar deta hai. Baweja ki maut kaa badla lene Chewte apne aadmiyo ke saath Apna Ghar pahuchke maar-dhaad shuru krdete hai. Isi ladai ke beech ek bahchca mar jaata hai. Murli uss bekasoor ki maut ka zimmedaar Ram Jaane ko thehraata hai. Ram Jaane Apna Ghar chhodke chala jata hai. Ram Jaane ke dushman Bhau aur Bannu ussey maarne ki saajish rachte hai par Ram Jaane ko iski bhanak lag-jaati hai aur vo Bhau aur Bannu ki jaan lekar bachcho ki jaan bachaane Apna Ghar pahuhcta hai aur ek ghamasaan ladaai ke baad Chewte ko jaan se maar deta hai. Court Ram Jaane ko maut ki sazaa sunataa hai. Ram Jaane ki maut ke baad Murli uski di hui chitthi padhta hai aur ussey pata chalta hai Ram Jaane Murli aur Bela ko saath dekhna chahta tha.

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Overview



Film mai bharpur action hai. Shahrukh humesha ki tarah negative role mai kaafi charming lag rahe hai. Shahrukh khan ke chahne wale iss movie ko zaroor dekhe.
