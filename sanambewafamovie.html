Film ki कहानी


Sher Khan aur Fateh Khan barso puraaney dushman hai. Sher Khan ka beta Salman aur Fateh Khan ki beti Rukhsar ek-dusre se pyaar karne lagte hai. Sher Khan ye jaankar behad khush hai, shaadi ki taarik aate hi Fateh Khan apne beti ka haath dene keliye Sher Khan se bahut zyada dahej maangta hai par apne bete ki khushi ke liye Sher Khan maan jaata hai. Badla lene keliye Sher Khan Rukhsar ko ghar se nikaal-deta hai. Fateh Khan apni beti ki takleef dekh-kar aag-babula ho chuka hai aur Sher Khan ko jaan se maarne nikalta hai. Fateh aur Sher ke beech ghamasaan ladai chchidh chuki hai par Rukhsar tabhi apne bachche ko waha leaati hai. Sher aur Fateh uss nanni-si-jaan ko dekh kar pighal jaatey hai aur hathiyar fek-kar ek dusre ko gale laga-letey hai. Salman aur Rukhsar apne bachche ke saath ek khush-haal zindagi bitaatey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Salman ) , Chandni( Rukhsar ) , Danny Denzongpa( Sher Khan ) , Pran( Fateh Khan ) , Puneet Issar( Afzal Khan ) , Dina Pathak

Honest Overview



Pran aur Danny ki dumdaar dialogue-baazi film ko behad mazedaar banadeti hai. " Pyaar barso purani dushmani ko dosti mai badal sakta hai film mai ye dikhaya gaya hai ". Overall movie bahut badiya hai aur purey pariwar ke saath baith-kar dekhi jaa sakti hai !!!
