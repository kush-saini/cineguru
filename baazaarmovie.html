Suspense Drama


Box Office Success


Life's Vicious Games Portrayed

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Rizwan Ahmed ki jo Allahbad ka ek chota sa Broker hai. Rizwan apne pitah ke khilaaf hokar chala aaya hai Sapno ki nagri Mumbai mai Izzat aur Paise kamaane keliye. Rizwan apne tezz dimaag se naukri paa leta hai aur baaton se chhokri. Business Jagat ka baadshah Shakun Kothari apne kaam nikalwane keliye Rizwan ko apni dosti se jhaasa dekar fasaa deta hai par Rizwan Police ki madad se uska Bada plan fail kardeta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Shakun ) , Rohan Mehra( Rizwan ) , Radhika Apte( Priya ) , Chitrangada Singh( Mandira ) , Elli Avram( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Ye kahaani har uss Insaan ko Haunsla deti hai jo khud pe bharosa karne ka faisla kar chuka hai aur Kaamyabi ki seediyan chadhna Chaahta hai. KHAAKRE ke jaise Karaare Dialogues infused with Gujrati Boli ki Mithaas. Film India-Pakistan ke kisi Match ki tarah NERVOUS bhi karti hai aur saath hi EXCITE bhi. ROhan Mehra ki awaaz ka Unique Texture 90's ke daur ka jaadoo add karta hai Movie mai. VERDICT - "DHOKLE ki tarah LAZEEZ". 1000% STRONGLY RECOMMENDED to every DREAMER !!!
