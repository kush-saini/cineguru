Film ki कहानी


Shyam gao se aya hai naukri ki talash mai aur thehra hai Baburao Ganpat Rao Apte ke yaha. Baburao ke yaha ek dusra kiraedaar bhi hai Raju jo bina mehnat kiye ameer banne ke sapne dekhta hai. Raju aur Shyam ki ek-dusre se nahi banti aur Baburao humesha unke jhagde rukwata hai. Ek raat ko Shyam ko Kabira ka phone aata hai jisne DeviPrasad ki poti ko kidnap karliya hai aur Firauti ki rakam maang raha hai Shyam ko DeviPrasad samjhke. Raju apna dimaag chalake Asli DeviPrasad se paise lekar Kabira ko dekar Bachchi ko bachane ka plan banataa hai. Dher saari mushkilo se jhoonjhne ke baad Raju Shyam Baburao poti ko uske Daada se milwane mai kaamyab hojaatey hai aur paise bhi kama-letey hai. Teeno ab behad khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Akshay Kumar( Raju ) , Suniel Shetty( Shyam ) , Paresh Rawal( Baburao ) , Tabu( Anuradha ) , Gulshan Grover( Kabira ) , Kulbhushan Kharbanda( Devi Prasad ) , Om Puri( Khadak Singh ) , Asrani( Bank Manager ) , Mukesh Khanna( Inspector ) , Dinesh Hingoo( Chaman Jhinga ) , Namrata Shrivastav( Song Cameo )

Honest Overview



TAREEF KARU KYA USKI JISNE TUJHE BANAYA MATLAB "PRIYADARSHAN Ji" iss film ke Director jo tareef ke asli haqdaar hai. Baburao ka kirdaar aapko apni Innocence(Bholepan) bhari baaton se lotpot kardega par Zindagi mai choti-choti cheezo mai khushiya dhoodhne ki seekh bhi deta hai. UNFORGETTABLE performances by DINESH HINGOO ji & OM PURI ji. HAR 90's KID keliye ye film dekhna MANDATORY hai. A MUST WATCH BEFORE YOU DIE !!!
