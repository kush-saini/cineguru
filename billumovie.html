Shows the Slow Paced Life

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Billu ki jisne badi mehnat se apne Nai ki dukaan ko kaamyab banaya tha par aajkal uska dhandha mandha chal raha hai aur Paise ke laale padey huey hai, bachcho ke School ki fees dene mai bhi takleef aa rahi hai. Billu ki kismat tab badalti hai gao mai khabar failti hai ki Mashoor Abhineta Sahir Khan gaav aaya hua hai aur wo Billu ke bachpan ka dost hai. Billu Sahir se nahi milpaata hai jissey poorey gaav mai uski khilli udd jaati hai. Lekin fir kuch aisa hota hai ki Billu aur Sahir ki dosti ko poora gaav apni aankhon se dekhleta hai. Billu apni khoyi hui izzat ko firse paaleta hai...
