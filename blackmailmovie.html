Blackmail-2018


 6.7 / 10

Directed by Abhinay Deb


Comedy Dark


Decent Box Office


Funny in Most UnFunny Situations

Film ki कहानी


Dev ki shaadi kisi samjhautey se kam nahi hai kyonki uski biwi Reema ussey pyaar nahi karti par baaki sabhi kartavya nibhaati hai. Dev ye sab jhel-leta hai par ek din hadh hi hojaati hai jab woh Reema ko bewafai kartey huey pakadleta hai. Reema ko nahi pata ki Dev ne ussey Ranjit ke saath dekhliya hai. Dev kuch der hataash hokar ek plan banaata hai. Wo plan bigadta hi chala jaata hai par Kismat ne Dev keliye kuch aur hi teh kar rakha hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Irrfan Khan( Dev ) , Kirti Kulhari( Reena ) , Arunoday Singh( Ranjit ) , Pradhuman Singh( Anand ) , Gajraj Rao( Chawla ) , Omi Vaidya( DK ) , Divya Dutta( Dolly ) , Anuja Sathe( Prabha ) , Urmila Matondkar( Cameo )

Honest Overview



Ye film dekhne ke baad aap Irrfan ji se kahengey- "Tera Jaadoo Chal gaya"..."Star Kids ka pichwada Jal Gaya ..Toh .. TOh... Jalne De...". Agar aap Subah-Subah Nahaatey huey KHAYALI PULAO banaate hai aur Imaginary(Man-gadhat) Haalato ko Sochtey huye Hakeekat(Reality) se kahi dur chale jaatey hai - toh DEV ka kirdaar(Character) aapko apna "Brother from Another Mother" lagega. VERDICT -" MOVIE ki Intensity badhti hi jaati hai Bikul 'JOLO CHIP' ke Teekhepan(Spicyness) ki tarah". 1000% RECOMMENDED to fans of DARK Cinema !!!
