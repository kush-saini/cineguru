Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Ravi ki jo ek naujawan kaamyab singer hai jo apni maa Shanti Devi se shaadi ka taana khaate hue apne concert jaa raha hai.Concert pahuchke Ravi ki mulaqat Kajal se hoti hai.Pyaar dono ki zindagi mai dastak deta hai aur unki shaadi hojati hai.Ravi ka chachera bhai Narendra pratap Kajal ki izzat lootne ki koshish krta hai. Kajal roti hui Ravi ko yeh batati hai. Aankhon mai khoon sawar , Ravi Narendra ko maarne lagta hai.Dono mai khunkaar ladai hoti hai. Ravi aur Narendra pahadi se neeche maut ke mooh mai gir jaate hai.Vidhwa Kajal ,Ravi se bichadne ke dukh mai shehar chchod ke chali jaati hai.Kismat Kajal ko Raja se milwa deti hai.Raja apne pitah Ramakant Sahay ke saamne shaadi ki ichcha zaahir krta hai aur pitah ki naa sunne ke baad ghar chodke chala jaata hai aur Kajal se shaadi krleta hai.Raja Kajal ko apne dost se milwata hai jo ki Ravi nikalta hai.Apne bete ki maut ka badla lene keliye Dhirendra pratap Kajal aur Raja ko kidnap krleta hai .Ravi waha pahuchkar Raja aur Kajal ko chchuda leta hai aur Dhirendra ko hara deta hai.Dhirendra wapas uthkar ldne ko daudta hai. Ravi aur Dhirendra ldte hue building se gir jaate hai .Neeche girte hue bomb phat jaata hai aur dono apni jaan gawa dete hai.Raja aur Kajal ,Ravi ko shradhanjali dete hai.
