Box Office Flop


Shahrukh and Raveena's 1st movie together

Film ki कहानी


Suraj Pratap Singh aur Madanlal Malhotra achche dost hai par Sundar unki dosti mai phoot daal deta hai. Madanlal ye maanne lagta hai ki uski biwi Suraj ki wajah se iss duniya mai nahi hai. Jigri dost ab jaani dushman ban chuke hai aur sheher mai ladaiya krke hudd-dhangh macha rahe hai. Assisant Commissioner Police apne dosto Kaamdev Singh aur Shalini ki madad se Suraj ke bete Rahul aur Madanlal ki beti Shalini ko kareeb la dete hai iss umeed mai ki bachchon ki dosti ke chalte Suraj aur Madanlal apni dushmani bhula denge . Badi mushkilo ke baad Shalini aur Rahul ki shaadi hojaati hai aur anth mai sab theek hojaata hai.
