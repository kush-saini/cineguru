Film ki कहानी


Sattar Singh Punjab ke chotey se gao Taasi ka rehne wala hai aur apne betey Bahattar Singh ki shaadi keliye rishtey dekh raha hai wahi desh ke alag kone Mumbai mai UnderWorld Don Tatya Tukaram Tendulkar bhi apni behen Indu keliye aisa ladka dhoodh rahe hai jissey Indu daraa ke bhaga na sakey. Jodi maker Mansukh dono parivaro ko milwa deta hai. Dono parivar ek dusre ke saamne Police mai hone ka naatak kartey hai. Indu aur Bahattar ke mann mai pyaar ki ghantiya bhajne lagi hai. Indu aur Bahattar ki Shaadi ke din Dono parivaro ke asli kaam ka parda faash hojaata hai. DOno parivaar chain ki saans letey hai aur ab aur rishteydaari Yaari mai badal chuki hai...
