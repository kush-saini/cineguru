Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Roop Singh Rathod ki . Roop apne pitah Shambunath ki tarah ek gaayak hai aur mehfilo mai gaata hai. Roop ko apne pitah ke ilaaj keliye paiso ki zaroorat hai. Ek din Roop Ajay Narang ke hotel mai gaa raha hota hai ki tabhi Ajay ki behen Reshma pehli nazar mai usey apna dil de baith-ti hai. Reshma Roop ko kewal apne liye gawana chahti hai par Roop Patel ke liye gaana behtar samajhta hai. Ajay apni behen ki khushi keliye Patel ko tabh tak pitwaata hai jab tak vo Roop ko apne hotel se bahar nahi nikaalta. Roop paiso ki majboori ke chalte Reshma ki baat maanleta hai aur apni premika Pooja se bandhan tod deta hai aur Reshma keliye gaata hai. Shambhunath ab theek hai aur shehar chhodke jaana chahta hai. Roop bhi ab Pooja ko leke shehar chhodna chahta hai. Iss baat ki bhanak Reshma ko lagti hai aur vo khudkhushi karne ki koshish karti hai. Gussey mai Ajay Shambunath aur Pooja ko agwa( kidnap ) karke Roop ko dono mai se bas kisi ek ki jaan bachaane ka mauka deta hai. Shambunath ke gale mai faasi hai aur voh Roop ke upar khada hai. Shambunath Pooja ko bachaane keliye Roop ko laat maarke apni jaan tyaag deta hai. Roop Pooja ko bachane keliye Ajay ke yaha pahuchta hai. Roop aur Ajay mai ladai chal rahi hai , Reshma Pooja ko maarne ki dhamki deti hai ki tabhi Ajay Roop ki taraf goli chalaata hai, Pooja Roop ko dhakka maarke goli ki disha se dur kardeti hai aur vo goli Reshma ki jaan leleti hai. Ajay sudh-budh kho chuka hai aur adhmara sa ho gya hai. Roop aur Pooja mauka dekh kar waha se bhaag nikalte hai aur ab dono azad hai .

Honest Overview



Ye film dikhati hai ki kis tarah pyar ke liye insaan saari hadhe paar kardeta hai. Behetreen adakaari( acting ) by Shahrukh khan, Naseeruddin Shah and Pooja Bhatt. A must watch and great songs !!
