Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Prem aur Bhaskar ki. Prem ko Love Guru ke naam se jaana jaata hai kyonki jo log apne dil ki baat nahi bol paate unhe train karta hai. Bhaskar ek ladki ko chaahta hai par keh nahi paata kyonki wo ladki hai Raj JaiSingh ki beti Priya jo uski senior bhi hai. Prem Bhaskar ko step by step training deta hai kaise Priya ko pataye. Prem ko bhi pyar hogya hai Naina naam ki ek reporter se aur uska ek sharaarti ladka bhi hai.Naina ki dost Nikki ko Prem ke client ne dokha diya hai, isliye Naina Love Guru ka parda faash karna chahti hai jo ki usey pata nahi ki uska hi Prem hai. Prem safai deta hai ki kis tarah usne saikdo logo ko pyar jaisi khoobsurat cheez ka ehsaas dilaaya hai apne kaam ke zariye. Naina Prem ko maaf kardeti hai. Bhaskar apne pyaar ka izhaar Priya se kar hi deta hai aur Priya bhi Bhaskar se beintihaa pyaar karti hai. Dono jodo ki shaadi ho chuki hai aur dono khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Anand ) , Govinda( Bhaskar ) , Lara Dutta( Naina ) , Katrina Kaif( Priya ) , Dalip Tahil( Priya's father ) , Rajat Bedi( Neil ) , Deepshika( Pammi ) , Rajpal Yadav( Chota Don ) , Aarti Chabria( Nikki )

Honest Overview



"Jo Pyaar aapko badalne ki koshish karein uss pyaar ko hi badaldo".Achchi baat hai ki film ke gaaney achche hai. Film mai dono Govinda aur Salman ne bohot overacting ki hai. Very Loud dialogues jo kabhi-kabhi iritating lagte hai. Rajpal Yadav ki performance aapko pasand aaegi. ONE-TIME WATCH !!!
