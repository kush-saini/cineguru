Film ki कहानी


Devdas London se law karke apni maa Kaushalya ke paas laut-tah hai aur apni bachchpan ki dost Paro se milta hai. Devdas aur Paro ki dosti jald hi pyaar ka roop leleti hai . Paro ek nautanki-kaar hai , ye baat Devadas ki maa Kaushalya ko nahi bhaati aur vo iss rishtey ko thukra deti hai. Paro ki maa Savitri ne uska rishta Thakur Bhuvan se taha kar diya jo pehle se hi teen bachcho ke baap hai. Gham mai dooba Devdas nachaniya ChandraMukhi ki mehfil mai jaane lagta hai aur sharaab se gehri dosti karleta hai. Chandramukhi Devdas ko chaahne lagti hai. Devdas Paro ki shaadi mai pahuchkar usey apne saath lejaana chaahta hai aur Paro ki naa sunkar wapis lautjaata hai. Devdas aur zyaada sharaab peene lagta hai , ek din Paro Devdas se milne pahuchti hai aur Devdas ko sharaab chodkar apni zindagi ko wapis theek karne ko kehti hai. Devdas Paro se waada karta hai ki maut ko galey lagaane se pehle woh ekbaar Paro ke darwaaze pe zaroor dastak dega. Devdas desh ghumne nikalta hai aur apne dost Chunni babu se milke dosti ke naam jaam lagaata hai. Zindagi jaise Devdas ka saath chhodne hi wali hai ki tabhi Devdas Paro se kia hua apna waada poora karne ke liye girta-padta Paro ke ghar ke saamne pahuchta hai. Paro ko apni taraf aata dekhte huye Devdas humesha keliye apni aankhein bandh karleta hai. Devdas ka gham uski zindagi ke saath ab khatam ho chuka hai .

Honest Overview



Sharaab ke nashe mai dooba hua aur pyaar mai haara aashiq jo maut ke mooh mai samaane se pehle apni premika ki ek aakhri jhalak dekhna chahta hai. Sanjay Leela Bhansali ji ne jadooi direction kiya hai aur Shahrukh ne gazab ka abhinay kara hai. Iss role mai Shahrukh itne charming lag rahe hai ki Devdas se nazre hataana naamumkin sa lagta hai.
