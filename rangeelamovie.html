Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Tapori Munna ki jo filmo ki ticktey black karta hai aur apni marzi ka maalik hai. Munna ki dost Mili filmo mai kaam karne ke sapney dekhti hai aur gaano mai background dancer hai. Munna iss dosti ko pyaar mai badalne ki khwaish rakhta hai par uski himmat hi nahi hoti. Mili actor Raj Kamal ki bahut badi fan hai aur kismat aisa khel khelti hai ki Mili ko Raj ke saath ek film miljaati hai. Raj aur Mili achche dost ban-chukey hai par Raj Mili ko chaahne laga hai. Munna uss chamak-dhamak bhari duniya ka hissa kabhi nahi ban paega aur RajKamal jaisa bada aadmi bhi nahi ban-sakta, ye sochkar Munna sheher chchod-kar jaa raha hai par jaane se pehle Mili ko ek chiithi mai apne dil ki daastaa suna deta hai. Mili Raj ke saath milkar Munna ko raaste mai rok deti hai. Raj apne pyaar ki kurbaani dekar Munna aur Mili ko ek-dujhe keliye unke pyaar ka ehsaas dilaata hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Munna ) , Urmila Matondkar( Mili ) , Jackie Shroff( Raj ) , Reema Lagoo( Mili's maa ) , Achnyut Potdar( Mili's papa ) , Gulshan Grover( Steven ) , Avtar Gill

Honest Overview



Aamir ka chapri andaaz aapko lot-pot kardega. Desh ke sabse famous BHIDU ki "Herogiri" aapko pasand aaegi. Iss desh ki har Mili ke sapne aur sangharsh ko Urmila ji ne apni flawless acting ke zariye dikhaya hai. CONSTANT EXCITEMENT GUARANTEED. A 1000% MUST WATCH FOR URMILA MATONDKAR FANS !!!
