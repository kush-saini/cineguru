Film ki कहानी


Omi Shukla netaji Bhaisaab keliye gundagardi karta hai aur sab ussey Omkara ke naam se jaantey hai. Omi ke do sipahi bhi hai Langda Tyagi aur Kesu. Omi raajneeti mai jaane se pehle apni Upaadhi Kesu ko saunp deta hai. Langda iss baat se jal kar ek shadyantra rachta hai jisme Omi ki biwi Dolly ka chakkar Kesu se chalne ki baat woh Omi tak chalaaki se pahuchaa deta hai. Omi Dolly ko maarne ke baad apni jaan leleta hai aur Tyagi apne rachey shadyantra mai khud hi maara jaata hai par Kaise ? - jaan-ne keliye film dekhiye...

Film ke कलाकार

Ajay Devgn( Omi ) , Saif Ali Khan( Langda Tyagi ) , Vivek Oberoi( Kesu ) , Deepak Dobriyal( Rajju ) , Naseeruddin Shah( Bhaisaab ) , Kareena Kapoor( Dolly ) , Konkona Sen( Indu ) , Bipasha Basu( Billo )

Honest Review



Saif bilkul ek bhediye ki tarah Cunning(Chalak) lag rahe hai. " Karma woh EMI hai jiska installment bharey bina jaan nahi jaa sakti " ye film dekh-kar aap Samajh-jaaengey. Desi boli aur gaaliyo ka Unquestionable Charm aur kisi Buddhe ke andar bhi Jawaani phoonkde aise Chatpatey Item SOngs iss film mai Chaar Chaand laga-detey hai. Saif Ali Khan iss show ke asli ShowStopper hai with his Career-Defining Performance. Film ka har second Popcorn Worthy hai. MUST WATCH BEFORE YOU DIE !!!
