Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai tab ki jab Mumbai Bombay tha aur Bombay ki sadko pe paley Mashoor Don Sultan ki. Sultan ke karam kaaley hai par Usool nek hai. Police karmi ka beta Shoaib Sultan ke dal mai shaamil ho chuka hai aur Sultan ko apna bhagwaan maanta hai. Sultan ne apni gair-maujoodgi mai Dhandha sambhaalne ki zimmedaari SHoaib ko di hai. Shoaib paiso ki hawas mai Sultan ke usoolo ki bali chadha deta hai. Sultan Shoaib ko iss gustaakhi keliye bhari mehfil mai kaske tamachaa lagata hai. Badle ki dhun mai Shoaib Sultan ko khullam khulley mai maardeta hai. Kisi ko nahi pata hai ab Shoaib kaha hai...
