Impeccably Divine

Film ki कहानी


SHivam apne Malik sahab ka wafadaar hai aur unka ek Hotel bhi sambhaalta hai. Malik Shivam ko apne ghar pe reh rahi Trafficking karke laayi gayi Reema pe nazar rakhne ko kehta hai. Shivam Reema ko uske aashiq Bilal ke saath dekhleta hai aur Malik sahab ke orders ke anusaar maarne jaa hi raha hota hai ki tabhi ussey apni mrit premika ki yaad aajati hai, isliye Reema aur Bilal bach jaatey hai. Malik iss gustaakhi keliye SHivam ko apne gundo se buri tarah pitwataa hai par SHivam waha se bhaag nikalta hai aur Malik ke saath uske khaandaan ko khatam karke Reema aur Bilal ko unki manzil tak pahucha deta hai. Shivam ab apni premika ke paas jaa raha hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Shivam ) , Ashutosh Rana( Malik ) , Shaad Randhawa( Kabir ) , Shriya Saran( Alia ) , Ashish Vidhyarthi( Rajan ) , Purab Kohli( Munna ) , Salil Acharya( Ronnie ) , Mrinalini Sharma( Reema )

Honest Overview



Buraai aur Jurm ke daldal(Swamp) mai doobtey huey insaan ko agar Pyaar(Love) ka sahara mil jaaye toh Life mai Allah ki "ENTRY" aur Dil-o-Dimaag ke saare Dirt ki "EXIT" hojaati hai - ye film humhe sikhaa-kar jaati hai. SPLENDID(Shaandaar) Music Composition by Pritam, film ke gaaney Kaabil-e-Tareef hai. After Watching the film aapke upar ek Bhoot chchadh jaaega - "SPIRITUALITY ka Bhoot". TRAVEL kartey waqt film ke gaaney = "HEAVEN on Earth". 1000% RECOMMENDED to Fans of Intense Cinema !!!
