Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Lovely Singh ki jo apne maalik Rana sahab ka wafadar bodyguard hai aur unki beti Divya ko unke dushmano se protection keliye har jagah unke saath jaata hai. Divya aur uski Dost Maaya iss akdoo bodyguard par ek prank kartey hai aur Divya Chaaya naam ki ladki banke Lovely se pyaar bhari baatey karti hai. Bahar se patthar ki tarah sakht Lovely ko pyaar thoda narm bana deta hai. Lovely ko milke Divya sab sach bataana chahti hai par apne pitaah ke darr se nahi bata paati. Lovely sheher se kaafi dur gundo se Divya ko bachata hai. Rana sahab waha pahuchkar Sartaj ko apni beti bhagaane keliye maarne jaa rahe hai par Divya unhe bataati hai ki Lovely ki premika uska intezaar kar rahi hai Railway Station pe. Divya Maya ko wo premika banaake bhej deti hai taaki Rana ji ke gundey Lovely ko bakshde. Lovely kahi saalo baad Ranaji se milne Canada se aata hai apne aur Maya ke betey(Sartaj Singh) ke saath. Maya ab iss duniya mai nahi hai par uski likhi ek diary Lovely ke haath lagti hai jisme likha hai ki Asli Chaaya Divya hai. Lovely aur Divya ki shaadi hojaati hai, Divya ko apna khoya hua pyaar fir se mil chuka hai...
