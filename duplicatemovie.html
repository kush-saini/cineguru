Comedy Action


Average Box-Office


Shahrukh Khan in double role

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Bablu Chaudhary ki jo ek Chef banna chahta hai. Bablu ek hotel mai naukri ke liye interview dene jaata hai, uska banaya hua khaana sabko pasand aajata hai aur usey head chef hi naukri mil jaati hai. Hotel ki manager Sonia ki Bablu se dosti hojaati hai aur unki dosti pyaar mai badal jaati hai. Manu ek criminal hai jo jail se apne dushmano ko maarne ke liye bhaaga hai aur bikul Bablu jaisa dikhta hai. Ek din police Bablu ko Mannu samajke jail mai daal deti hai par Sonia ke bayan se vo chchut jaata hai. Mannu ko iss baat ka pata chalta hai aur vo Bablu ki maa aur Sonia ko kidnap krleta hai. Bablu waha pahuchta hai , Kaafi afra-tafri ke baad police waha pahuchti hai aur confuse hojaati hai ki asli Mannu kaun hai, tabhi Mannu ki dost Lily waha pahuchke Mannu ko jaan se maardeti hai aur apna badla leleti hai. Bablu aur Sonia fir se saath hojaate hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Bablu/Manu ) , Juhi Chawla( Sonia ) , Sonali Bendre( Lily ) , Mohnish Behl( Ravi ) , Farida Jalal( Bablu's mother ) , Tiku Talsania( Inspector ) , Sharat Saxena( Dinghra ) , Gulshan Grover( Shalaku )

Honest Review



Ye film bas Shahrukh Khan ke chaahne walo ke liye bani hai. Film Timepaas karne keliye ekbaar dekhi jaa sakti hai !!
