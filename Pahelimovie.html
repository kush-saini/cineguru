Shahrukh in double role

Film ki कहानी


Lachchi ki shaadi seth Bhanwarlal ke bete Kishan se hojaati hai. Kishan apne pitah ka chamcha hai aur bas kaam mai hi laga rehta hai. Shaadi ke agle din hi Kishan ko vyaapaar ke liye Lachchi se paanch saal dur jaana pad raha hai par phir bhi vo Lachchi ko samay nahi de raha aur kaam mai hi magn hai, Lachchi iss baat se dukhi hai. Teen din baad Kishan ka bhes lekar ek bhoot uske ghar aajata hai, Lachchi ko bhoot ki sachchai pata hai par phir bhi vo bhoot apni zindagi aur dil mai jagah deti hai, Lachchi ko bhoot se pati ka pyaar mil raha hai aur ghar mai sab bhoot ko Kishan samaj rahe hai. 4 saal beet chukey hai aur Lachchi ke pet mai bhoot ki bachchi pal rahi hai jiska naam vaha dono Looni Maa rakhne ka faisla kartey hai. Asli Kishan wapas aata hai , sabhi gharwaley achambey mai hai aur faisla nahi kar paa rahey ki asli Kishan kaun hai aur asli-nakli ka faisla karne keliye Raja ke paas jaa rahe hai. Raastey mai unhe ek baba milta hai jo bhoot ko ek botal mai kaidh kardeta hai aur asli Kishan sabke saamne aa chuka hai. Kishan ghar pahuchke Lachchi se baaton-baaton mai Looni Maa ka zikr karta hai , Lachchi samaj jaati hai ki Kishan ke sharir mai Bhoot baat kar raha hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Kishanlal / Bhoot ) , Rani Mukherji( Lachchi ) , Anupam Kher( KishanLal's father ) , Aditi Govitrikar( Kamli ) , Rajpal Yadav( Bhoja ) , Dilip Prabhavalkar( Kanwarlal ) , A.K Hangal( Jeevraj )

Honest Overview



Kahaani behad dilchasp hai . Shahrukh aur Rani ne Rajasthani boli ko kaafi achche se pesh kiya hai. Film mai khoobsurat special effects ka istemaal kiya gaya hai. Agar aapko purane kissey-kahaaniya pasand hai toh iss film ko Zaroor dekhiye !!!
