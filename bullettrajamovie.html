It's Raining Bullets !!

Film ki कहानी


Raja ko ek Shaadi mai apne Chacha pe hone wale hamle ki bhanak lag jaati hai. Raja apne mitra Rudra ke saath milke Chachaji ko bacha letey hai. Parantu Kuch dino baad Chachaji ko lallan Singh ka gang maardeta hai. Lallan se bachne keliye Raja aur Rudra jail pahuchtey hai jaha unki mulaqaat Shrivastav ji se hoti hai jinki madad se Dono jail se nikal jaatey hai. Bahar nikal kar Raja-Rudra Lallan ka khaatma kardetey hai. Ab dono Netaji Ram Babu keliye kaam karney lage hai. Raja Rudra ki maut ka badla lene keliye Ram Babu ke mitra Balraj ka "Ram Ram Satya" kardeta hai. Raja ab Netaji se bachke bhaag raha hai. CHambal ka Hero Police Inspector Arun ab Raja ke peeche lag chuka hai. Raja aur Arun ka aamna-saamna hojaata hai jisme Raja Arun ki jaan bakshdeta hai. Arun kuch din baad Encounter mai Raja ko maardeta hai par ye sab nazro ka ek dhoka tha...

Uttar Pradesh ki galiyon mai bekhauf Dabangaii aur Sansanaati Goliyon ki Awaaz se lais(loaded) ye film behad GRITTY(Desh ki Mitti se judi hui) aur AUTHENTIC lagti hai. Iss film mai Vidyut Jamwal "ICING on THE CAKE" ki tarah hai. Popcorn lekar baith jaaiye iss film ko dekhne. 1000% STRONLGY RECOMMENDED to Saif Ali Khan Die-Hard fans !!!
