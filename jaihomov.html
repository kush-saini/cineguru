Box Office Success


Very Humane Story

Film ki कहानी


Jai ek fauj se nikaala gaya fauji hai jo apni behen Geeta aur Maa ke saath rehta hai. Ek din Jai ek apaahij ladki Suman ki paper likhne mai madad karta hai, Suman traffic police ki galti ke chalte apna agla paper nahi de paati aur suicide karleti hai. Jai madad karne ke badle Teen logo ki madad karne ka tareeka nikaalta hai aur isse sheher mai faila detaa hai. Jai ek badtameez aadmi ko peet-deta hai aur wo aadmi gundey bulaake Geeta ke saath badtameezi kartey hai, Jai waha pahuchkar Gundo ki chutney bana deta hai. Wo gundey Minister Dashrath ke aadmi hotey hai. Dashrath badla lene keliye apne betey Arjun ko bhejta hai par woh Jai ke haatho maara jaata hai. Jai buri tarah ghayal hai. Dashrath Jai ko CM pe huey hamle ka kasoorvar thehraata hai par CM khud public ke saamne Dashrath ka sach bata-deta hai ki humla Dashrath ne karwaya tha. Jai ab bilkul theek hai aur public ne Dashrath ka jeena mushkil kardia hai...

Honest Overview



Film ek badhiya message sachchai aur achchai ka message dekar jaati hai. Somewhat Satisying Action scenes and Decent Acting with some really dark scenes. JUST A ONE TIME WATCH !!!
