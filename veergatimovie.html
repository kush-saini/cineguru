Action


Average Box Office


Pooja Dadwal's only movie

Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Ajay ki jisey Havaldar ne sadak se uthakar apna ghar mai jagah di aur bete ka darja diya. Ajay ka padai mai man nahi lagta aur usey jua khelne ki lat lag jaati hai. Jaise log kapde badaltey hai waise Ajay naukriya badalta hai kyonki ussey beimaani bilkul bardaasht nahi. Ajay Ekka naam ke badmaash ke yaha jaata hai aur uski bezati karke naukri karne ka prastav thukra deta hai. Iss baat se gussa Ekka , Havaldar aur uski beti Sandhya ko jaan se maardeta hai. Pratishodh ji jwala liye Ajay Ekka ko maarne pahuchta hai aur Ekka jo jalakar maar dalta hai. Aakhir mai kanoon ki goliya Ajay ki jaan leleti hai. Ajay apni maa ki godh mai dum tod-deta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Ajay ) , Divya Dutta( Sandhya ) , Atul Agnihotri( Shlok ) , Pooja Dadwal( Pooja ) , Akhilendra Mishra( Ikka ) , Saeed Jaffrey , Kulbhushan Kharbanda( Havaldar )

Honest Overview



Salman ne dumdaar dialogues ko bakhoobi bola hai. Kulbushan ji aur Akhilendra ji ne zabardast acting ki hai. Film ki direction ko dekh kar aisa lagta hai ki direction seekhte seekhte film banayi gayi hai. Bhaijaan ke fans iss movie ko zaroor dekhe. A decent timepass flick !!!
