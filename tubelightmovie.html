Film ki कहानी


Ye Laxman aur Bharat ki kahaani hai jo iss duniya mai ek-doosre ka iklautaa sahara hai. Laxman thoda mandh-buddhi hai isliye sab usko Tubelight bulaatey hai. Bharat laxman ka Maa-Baap-Bhai aur sab kuch hi hai. Bharat ko Major Rajbir Sena keliye chunletey hai Indo-China War mai ladne keliye. Laxman aur Bharat ka saath chchoot jaata hai par Laxman har din dua karta hai Bharat ke wapas laut aaney ki. Shaheed sipaahiyo mai Bharat ka naam bhi aata hai aur Laxman bhi apne bhai ke samman mai ek mombatti jalataa hai sabki tarah aur poori tarah se toot chuka hai. kuch dino ke baad khabar milti hai ki Bharat zinda hai par chotil hai aur apni yaad-daasht bhool chuka hai. Laxman Bharat ko rote huey gale laga leta hai aur Bharat ko dhundhli yaadein aane lagti hai. Bhagwan ne Tubelight ki duaey sun hee li aakhir-kaar...

Honest Overview



"Sirius" iss duniya ka sabse zyada tez chamakne wala taara hai aur Salman bhi iss film mai issi tarah chamak rahe hai apni GROUND-BREAKING ACTING performance ke zariye.
