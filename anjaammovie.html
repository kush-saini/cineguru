Anjaam-1994


 7.6 / 10

Directed by Rahul Rawail


Vengeance Thriller


Box Office Success


Best Villain Award for Shahrukh Khan

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Shivani ki . Shivani ek air hostess hai. Ek din flight mai Shivani ki mulaakaat ek ameer businessman Vijay se hoti hai jo pehli hi nazar mai ussey dil de baithta hai. Vijay apni maa ke saath Shivani ke yaha rishta leke pahuchta hai aur pata hai ki Shivani ki pehle hi Ashok se shaadi ho chuki hai. Ye dekh kar Vijay ghum mai doob jaata hai. Shivani ki shaadi ko 4 saal ho chuke hai aur vo apne pati ke saath America rehti hai .Shivani se nazdikiyan badhane ke liye Vijay Ashok se dosti badhata hai par Shivani dakke maarke Vijay ko ghar se bahar nikaal deti hai. Shivani ki badtameezi dekh-ke Ashok Shivani par haath uthata hai, Vijay yaha dekhk kr Ashok ko jaan se maar deta hai. Shivani police ko ye sab bataane jaati hai par Vijay paise khilaakar Inspector Arjun Singh ko apni jeb mai karleta hai. Vijay Shivani se pyaar ki bheekh maangta hai aur Shivani ke mana karne par ussey berehmi se maarta hai aur khud ko zakhmi karke Shivani ko salaakhon ke peeche bhijwadeta hai. Shivani ka jija uski behen Padma aur beti Pinky ko ghar se bahar nikaal deta hai. Vijay anjaane mai Padma aur Pinky par gaadi chada deta hai aur khud bhi aphaahij hojata hai. Shivani badla lene keliye pehle Mohanlal ko maut ke ghaat utaarti hai aur phir Vijay ka pata nikal-kar Tikamgarh pahuchti hai aur ek besahara aphaahij Vijay ko paati hai. Shivani Vijay ko theek krne ke baad phir badla lene ka pran leti hai. Vijay theek hojaata hai aur phir se Shivani ko apnana chahta hai , Shivani Vijay ko apne paas bulakar chalaki se uske pet mai chaaku ghop deti hai aur usey maarte hue pahadi tak lejaati hai. Vijay pahadi se latak-tey hue Shivani ko pair se neeche kheech leta hai aur ab Vijay Shivani ke pair ke zariye pahaadi pe latka hua hai. Badle ki aag mai jalti hui Shivani haath chchod deti hai aur Vijay ki jaan lete huye khudki zindagi bhi tyaag deti hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Overview



Ye film ek-tarfa pyaar aur badle ki kahaani hai. Negative role mai Shahrukh ka koi jawaab nahi . Madhuri ne badhiya abhinay kia hai. Director aur writer tareef ke asli haqdaar hai. " Chane ke khet mai " aur " Badi Mushkil hai " iconic gaane hai. Kam shabdo mai Kahe toh chatpati Masaledaar film . Must watch before you die !!
