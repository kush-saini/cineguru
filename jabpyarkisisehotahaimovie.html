Jab Pyaar Kisise Hota Hai-1998


 6.8 / 10

Directed by Deepak Sareen


Drama Romance Comedy


Box Office Hit


Soulful music

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Sooraj ki jo ki ek ameer dada ka bigda hua pota hai. Sooraj sharaabi aur ladkibaaz hai. Dadaji ke kehne par Sooraj wapis India aajata hai. Dadaji Sooraj ko Ooty ek hotel banane bhejte hai par Sooraj toh ghumne mai vyast hai. Sooraj ko Komal naam ki ek ladki se pehli nazar mai pyaar hojaata hai. Kuch dino baad Komal ko pata chalta hai Sooraj ne uska dil jeetne ke liye jhoot kaha tha isliye wo Sooraj se dur chali jaati hai. Badi mushkil se Sooraj Komal ko dhoondhta hai aur Komal se shaadi ki baat karta hai. Sooraj apni buri aadatey tyaag chuka hai. Suraj ki purani zindagi uski maujooda zindagi mai laut ke aati hai uski naajayaz aulad ke roop mai. Uss bachche ka naam hai Kabir. Sooraj Kabir se bohot pyaar karne lagta hai par kisi ko nhi bata paya hai ki Kabir uska beta hai. Shaadi se ek din pehele Sooraj Komal ke ghar pahuchkar sabke saamne Kabir aur uske rishtey ka sach batadeta hai. Sooraj ki imaandari dekh-kar Komal shaadi keliye haa kardeti hai. Sooraj ab behad khush hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Suraj ) , Twinkle Khanna( Komal ) , Namrata Shirodkar( Pooja ) , JOhnny Lever( Mahesh ) , Anupam Kher( Dadaji ) , Saeed Jaffrey( Komal's father ) , Farida Jalal( Komal's mother ) , Himani Shivpuri

Honest Overview



Pyaar jalebi jaise tede logo ko bhi seedha kaise kardeta hai iss film mai dikhaya hai .Komal ki saadgi aapko bohot pasand aaegi. Salman aur Twinkle ki onscreen-jodi superhit hai. Film ke gaaney chartbuster hai aur background music bohot pyaraa hai. Ye movie youth ko khoob pasand aaegi. Highly Recommended unke liye jinhe Music se pyaar hai !!!
