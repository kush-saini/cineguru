Film ki कहानी


Shamsher ek arms dealer hai aur apne raaste mai aane wale kisi ko bhi mitaake bas jeetne mai vishwaas rakhta hai. Shamsher ke bachche Sanjana aur Sooraj apne bhai Sikander se jalte hai kyonki uski jaeydaad ka hissa sabse bada hai. Sikander ko pata chalta hai ki uske asli maa-baap ko uske nakli baap Shamsher ne maara. Sikander badla lene pahuchta hai par Shamsher ke betey Yash pe daya karke dono baap-beto ko jail bhijwa deta hai. Aakhir mai pata chalta hai ki SIkander ne Sanjana aur Sooraj ke saath milke pehle hi ek master-plan bana rakha tha ye sab karne ka aur jo Shamsher ko fasaane ki koshish kar rahe thay( Jessica aur Yash(Shamsher's son) ) wo khud pehle hi fas chukey thay Sikander ke jaal mai. Ab Shamsher-Yash jail mai baithkar badle ki ek nayi kahaani likh rahe hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Sikander ) , Jacqueline Fernandez( Jessica ) , Anil Kapoor( Shamsher ) , Daisy Shah( Sanjana ) , Saqib Saleem( Suraj ) , Bobby Deol( Yash ) , Sharat Saxena( Sexy Uncle ) , Freddy Daruwala( Rana )

Honest Overview



Iss Film ka Glitz aur Glamour quotient behad high hai matlab khoobsurati ki baad(Flood) aa rakhi hai par Likeability(Mast film thi yaar) factor bohot kum hai, Saral shabdo mai film (Intriguing)Rochak nahi hai. Pehli do filmo ko agar Red Velvet Cake maana jaaye toh ye film ko sugar coated Dung Cake hi kaha jaa sakta hai. TERRIBLE DIALOGUES AND LACKLUSTER PERFORMANCEs. WATCH IT ONLY IF YOU LOVE SALMAN OTHERWISE PLEASE DON't Even Attempt !!!
