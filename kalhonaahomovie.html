Kal Ho Naa Ho-2003


 7.8 / 10

Directed by Nikhil Advani


Romance Drama Comedy


Box Office Hit


Shot in New York

Film ki कहानी


Naina apni maa Katherine aur daadi Lajjo ke saath NewYork sheher mai rehti hai. Naina apne pitah ko kho chuki hai aur tabse jaise ki hasna hi bhool gayi hai. Aman apni maa ke saath Naina ke pados mai rehne aata hai. Naina shuruat mai Aman ko bilkul pasand nahi karti par dheere-dheere dono mai dosti hojaati hai, Aman humesha bahut khush rehta hai aur Naina ko wapas hasna sikha deta hai. Naina Aman ko chaahne lagti hai par Aman ki shaadi ke baare mai jaanke uska dil toot-jaata hai. Rohit Naina ka dost hai par mann hi mann ussey pyaar karta hai, Aman Rohit ko Naina ka dil jeetne mai madad krta hai. Naina aur Rohit ki sagai hojaati hai. Aman ki zindagi ab zyada nahi bachi hai aur iss baat ka pata keval uski maa ko hai. Aman ki doctor aur dost Priya ke zariye Naina ko uski haalat ka pata chalta hai. Aman Rohit ko samjhaata hai ki uske paas ab zyada samay nahi hai aur voh jaatey-jaatey Naina ko Rohit ke saath hi dekhna chahta hai. Rohit aur Naina ki shaadi hojaati hai. Aman saarey pariwar ko aakhri alwida kehke gehri neend mai humesha keliye sojaata hai. Rohit aur Naina ab bhoodey ho chukey hai lekin Aman ki yaad unke dilo mai aaj bhi taaza hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Aman ) , Saif Ali Khan( Naina ) , Preity Zinta( Rohit ) , Jaya Bachchan( Jennifer ) , Sushma Seth( Lajjo ) , Reema Lagoo( Aman's Mother ) , Lilette Dubey( Jazz ) , Kajol( Special Appearance ) , Rani Mukherjee( Special Appearance ) , Sonali Bendre( Special Appearance )

Honest Overview



Film ke dialogues aur gaaney aapko kaafi pasand aaengey. Aman ka kirdaar taazi hawa ke jhokey jaisa lgta hai . Shahrukh ki aakarshak screen-presence ke saamne baaki actors pheekey lag rahe hai. Ye film aapko hasaaegi aur rulaegi bhi. Apne jigri dosto ke saath baith-kar iss film ka lutf uthaaya jaa sakta hai. " An Emotional Roller-Coaster Ride " . A must watch for every hardcore Bollywood fan !!!
