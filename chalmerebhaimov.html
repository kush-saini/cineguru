Chal Mere Bhai-2000


 4.1 / 10

Directed by David Dhawan


Comedy Drama


Average Box Office


Salman & Sanjay 1st film together

Film ki कहानी


Ye Kahaani hai Sapna ki jo apne Mama-Maami ke yaha rehne Mumbai aayi hai aur naukri ki talaash mai. Aaj bahut dino ke baad ussey ek interview mila hai aur yaha bhi usne Managing Director Vikram se saamne kharaab impression diya hai. Sapna ki maayusi dekh kar ek gentleman Ussey naukri pe rakhletey hai. Vo gentleman koi aur nahi hai par Vikram ke pitah Mr Oberoi aur company ke maalik.Sapna apne kaam mai theek-thaak hai par dil ki bohot achchi hai.Sapna ek din ghayal Vikram ko uske ghar pahucha deti hai ,Vikram ke gharwale ko sapna bohot pasand aati. Vikram ka bhai hai Prem jo Sapna ke mama se acting seekhta hai .Kuch mulaakaato ke baad Prem aur Sapna ek dusre ko chaahne lagte hai par Daadi ne Sapna ko Vikram keliye pasand kiya hai isliye Prem apne bhai ke liye apne pyaar ki kurbani dedeta hai. Vikram ko iss baat ki bhanak hai aur vo Prem-Sapna ko ek karwa hi deta hai . Sab behad khush hai..

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Sanjay Dutt( Vikram ) , Karisma Kapoor( Sapna ) , Dalip Tahil( Balraj Oberoi ) , Shakti Kapoor( Sapna's Mamaji ) , Himani Shivpuri( Sapna's maami ) , Asrani( Doctor )

Honest Overview



Ye film pheeki daal ke jaisi hai. Dialogues kahi jagah par bohot funny toh kahi jagah par ekdum feeke hai aur gaaney bhi kaam nahi karte scenes mai. Bas Karisma ji hi iss film ka sabse interesting pahalu hai unki AMAZING ON SCREEN PRESENCE aur CHARM ki wajah se. Agar aapko film ke actors behad pasand hai tabhi dekhiye iss film ko otherwise aur Kuch Dhanka karke apne 2 Ghante bachaiye !!!
