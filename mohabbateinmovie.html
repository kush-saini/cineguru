Film ki कहानी


Narayan Shankar 25 saal puraane Gurukul college ka principal hai aur sabse bohot sakhti se pesh aata hai. Gurukul mai pyaar keliye koi jagah nahi hai aur agar koi himmat krta hai toh Narayan Shankar usey college se nikaal deta hai. Raj Aryan Malhotra ek music teacher ke taur pe Gurukul mai aaya hai aur sab ladko ko bina kisi dar ke pyaar krne ki salaah deta hai. Teen ladke Sameer Vicky aur Karan teen ladkiyo Sanjana Ishika aur Kiran se pyaar krne lagte hai aur unka dil jeetne mai kaamyab hotey hai. Narayan Shankar teeno ladko ko Gurukul se humesha keliye nikaalne ka faisla krta hai. Raj Shankar Narayan ki beti Megha se pyaar karta tha aur Megha ne Raj se judaa hone ke gham mai khudkhushi karli thi. Raj Shankar ke khilaaf khada hoke teeno ladko keliye ladta hai aur Narayan Shankar ko bataata hai ki kis tarah uske niyamo ne uski beti ki jaan leli thi. Narayan Shankar saare students se maafi maangta hai aur pyaar ko Gurukul mai ek nayi zindagi dete hue principal ke audey se isteefa dedeta hai. Raj Narayan Shankar ki jagah leleta hai. Sameer Vicky Karan apni premikao ke saath bahut khush hai .
