Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Durga ki jo apne baito Karan aur Arjun ke saath ek khush-haal zindagi bita rahi hai. Thakur Sangram Singh apni antim saanse gin rahe hai aur apni bahu Durga aur potey Karan aur Arjun ko dekhna chahte hai. Thakur Karan Arjun ko apni sampatti saupne hi wale hote hai ki tabhi Durjan Singh unki jaan leleta hai. Durjan Singh apne saalo ki madad se Karan Arjun ko Durga ke saamne marwa deta hai. Durga roti hui Kaali maa ke kadmo mai girke apne beto ki zindagi wapas maangti hai. Karan aur Arjun wapas janam lete hai. Arjun Vijay hai aur Karan Ajay hai. Kismat dono bhaiyo ko phir se milwa deti hai. Dono apna beeta janam yaad karke apni maa se phir miljaate hai. Karan-Arjun ke wapas aane ka shor purey gao mai mach jaata hai. Durjan Singh Karan-Arjun ko maarne keliye nikalta hai par buri tarah haar jaata hai. Karan aur Arjun apni maa ke kehne par Durjan Singh ko maar kar apne baap ki maut ka badla le-lete hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Karan ) , Shahrukh Khan( Arjun ) , Raakhee Gulzar( Durga ) , Mamta Kulkarni , Kajol( Sonia ) , Amrish Puri( Durjan Singh ) , Ranjeet , Aasif Sheikh( Durjan's son ) , Johnny Lever

Honest Review



Film ka ek-ek frame behad entertaining hai . Rakesh Roshan ne behetreen direction kiya hai. A Must Watch and definitely recommended emotional roller-coaster ride .
