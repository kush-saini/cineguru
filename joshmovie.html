Film ki कहानी


Goa ke Vasco gao mai do rival Gang hai , Eagle gang aur Bichchoo gang . Eagle gang ka leader hai Max aur Bichchoo ka leader hai Prakash . Max aur uski behen Shirley anaath hai. Aksar dono gangs mai haatha-paai hoti rehti hai , Father Jacob ya Police Inspector hi har baar unki ladai rukwaate hai . Prakash ka bhai Rahul Mumbai mai chef hai aur abhi apne pariwar ke paas Vasco aaya hua hai. Rahul ko Vasco bhaa jaata hai. Rahul Max ki behen Shirley ko pasand karne lagta hai. Prakash paiso ki zaroorat ke chalte Alberto Vasco ki zameen hadapna chahta hai . Alberto Vasco purey Vasco ki zameen ka maalik tha. Rahul pata lagata hai ab poori zameen Mary naam ki ek lady ki hai jo Max aur Shirley ki maa bhi hai aur Alberto Vasco Max-Shirley ka baap tha .Max-Shirley iss saari baat se anjaan hai. Rahul Shirley ko ye baat bataane ke liye chitthi likhta hai. Ye chitthi Prakash ke haath lagjaati hai aur vo Max ko maarne ke iraade se nikaltaa hai, bhayanak ladai hojati aur Max khudko bachaate huey Prakash ko goli maardeta hai. Police Max ko pakad lejaati hai par Rahul court mai Prakash ka parda-faash karke Max ko chhuda leta hai. Rahul aur Shirley shaadi karlete hai aur Max bhi apni premika se shaadi karleta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Max ) , Aishwarya Rai( Shirley ) , Sharad Kapoor( Prakash ) , Chandrachur Singh( Rahul ) , Sharat Saxena( Police Inspector ) , Sushant Singh( Gotiya )
