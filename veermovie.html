Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Pindariyo ke Raja Prithvi Singh ke betey Veer ki jo Angrezo se desh ko bachaane chaahta hai. Veer apne bhai Punya ke saath sena lekar ladna aage-bad raha hai aur Madhavgarh ke Gyanendra Singh se ladaai mai baar-baar bachcha hai. Badle ki aag bhujaane keliye Veer Gyanendra Singh ke kiley pe hamla kardeta hai aur Gyanendra ki beti Yashodra ko agva karke le-aata hai aur ussey pyaar kar baith-taa hai. Gyanendra Britishers ke saath ek ghamaasan ladai mai maara jaata hai. Dono raajyo mai sulah hojaati hai. Kahi saal beet chukey hai Veer aur Yashodra ki shaadi ko aur Veer ke saamrajya ko ek naya yuvraaj mil chuka hai...
