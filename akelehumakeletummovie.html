Film ki कहानी


Rohit ek behtreen singer hai aur apne likhey huey gaane gaata hai ek Hotel mai. Ek din ROhit ki mulakaat Kiran se hoti hai, Kiran uski gayki par aur Rohit Kiran ki khoobsurati par fida hai. ROhit aur Kiran ka ek hi sapna hai Gaayak banne ka. Pyaar unki zindagi ko khushiyo se bhar deta hai aur dono shaadi karletey hai. Rohit sangharsh kar raha hai aur ab ek bachche ka baap bhi hai. Kiran Rohit ki uski gaayki ko lekar beparvahi se tangh aakar ghar chchod-deti hai aur apni dost Sunita ke saath reh rahi hai. Kismat Kiran keliye Bollywood ke darwaze khol deti hai. Kiran apni jaan-pehchaan se Rohit ko kaam dilwa-deti hai iss baat se ROhit ke Zameer ko takleef pahuchti hai aur woh kaam chchod deta hai. Kiran apne betey Sunil ko apne paas rakhne keliye ROhit ke khilaaf mukadhma(Case) ladti hai aur jeet jaati hai. Court ke andar ROhit-Kiran ko apne khoye huey pyaar ka ehsaas hojaata hai. Sunil ka apne pitah keliye pyaar Tootey huey Pariwar ko phirse jod-deta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Review



Film ke gaaney aapke chehre pe muskaan laa saktey hai aur aapko GOOSEBUMPS dene ki capability rakhtey hai. Movie Effortlessly ENgaging hai aur Har Struggler ke Jeevan ka Kissa sunaatey huye SELF-ESTEEM(Aatma-Samman) aur PYAAR keliye ladhne ka sabak deti hai. AAMIR & MANISHA ji have HIT THE LEATHER OUT OF THE PARK apni GRACEFUL performances ke zariye. BONAFIDE FAMILY ENTERTAINMENT GUARANTEED. 1000% HIGHLY RECOMMENDED unko jo apne SAPNO ke peeche purey PASSION ke saath bhaag rahey hai...
