Film ki कहानी


Gaurav Delhi mai rehta hai aur khudko Aryan Khanna ka sabse bada fan bataata hai. Gaurav abhi-abhi Aryan ki mimicry karke ek award bhi jeeta hai aur apna award Mumbai jaake Aryan ko dikhaana chahta hai. Gaurav apne maa-baap ki permission lekar Mumbai pahuchta hai aur apne jaise laakho fans ki bheed mai khud ko khoya hua paata hai. Gaurav Aryan ki burai karne wale actor Sid Kapoor ko maarta hai aur uski recording viral kardeta hai. Aryan ye sab dekh kar Sid se maafi maangta hai aur Gaurav ko jail mai bandh karwadeta hai. Aakhir-kaar Gaurav apni zindagi Aryan se milta hai par Aryan ussey apna zyada samay nahi dena chahta. Gaurav ka pyar ab badle ki aag ban chuka hai. Gaurav Aryan jaisa dikhta hai aur iss baat ka fayda uthakar Aryan ko beizzat karne ki koshish karta hai. Aryan Gaurav ka peecha-peecha karte ussey pakad hi leta hai par Gaurav pakda jaana nahi chahta aur apni jaan leleta hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Aryan Khanna/Gaurav ) , Sayani Gupta( Sunaina ) , Shreya Pilgaonkar( Neha ) , Waluscha De Sousa( Bella )

Honest Overview



" Pyaar ka Paagalpan mai badalna aur uska anjaam ". Shahrukh ne dhasoo acting ki hai khaastaur pe Gaurav ke role mai. " The Perfect Movie for Cinema lovers ". Ye movie Cinema ke ek naye daur ki shuruat hai. Director Maneesh Sharma tareef ke hakdaar hai. MUST WATCH MOVIE BEFORE YOU DIE , YES IT IS THAT GOOD !!!
