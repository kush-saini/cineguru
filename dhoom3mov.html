Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai do judwa Bhai Sahir aur Samar ki. Sahir aur Samar ke pitah Iqbal The Great Indian Circus chalaatey thay jo neelam hone ki kagar pe aagayi thi Mr. Anderson ke bank ke zariye par Iqbal ye nahi jhel paaye aur apni jaan gawa baithey. Ab Sahir aur Samar bade ho chukey hai aur har bank ko lootke tabah karna unka maqsad ban gaya hai. Sahir phirse The Great Indian Circus ko chalu kar chuka hai apne pitah ke sapne ko zinda rakhne keliye aur Samar ko duniya se chupake rakhta hai kyonki wo seedha hai. Inspector Jai aur Ali ko India se Chicago bulaya jaata hai Bank Robberies ka case solve karne keliye. Jai aur Ali apne tez dimaag ki badaulat Sahir aur Samar ko pakad hi letey hai ek behad unchche spot( Contra Dam jaise ) jaha pe se bhaagna naamumkin hai. Sahir aur Samar kood-kar maut ko bhi ek-doosre ka haath thaamkar galey lagaa letey hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Sahir/Samar ) , Jackie Shroff( Sahir/Samar's father ) , Abhishek Bachchan( Jai ) , Uday Chopra( Ali ) , Katrina Kaif( Aaliya ) , Tabrett Bicknell( Victoria ) , Siddharth Nigam( Young Samar/Sahir ) , Andrew Bicknell( Anderson )

Honest Overview



Film ka action saadharan hai aur Aamir ki performance extra-ordinary especially Samar jaisa complex kirdaar jisme bachpana hai par iraade badey hai. Film Thrill aur Excitement toh nahi par khoobsurat Cinematography aur Raw Emotion ko dikhaane mai kaamyab hui hai. UNFORTUNATELY FILM DHOOM NAHI MACHAA PAYI HAI par boring nahi hai aur IMPRESSIVE VFX bhi hai which makes it a DECENT WATCH !!!
