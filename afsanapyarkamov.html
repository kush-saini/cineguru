Film ki कहानी


Raj ke pitaah ko Duniya ka sabse bura nasha hai - Daulat ka jiske liye unhone apni biwi ko bhi chchod diya tha. Raj ko daulat hotey huey bhi uska koi moh nahi hai. College mai nayi ladki Nikita ne Raj ko apne husn se Clean Bold kardiya hai. Dheere Dheere dono mai dosti hojaati hai aur himmat karke Raj apne dil ki baat Nikita ko keh deta hai aur saamne se bhi hari jhandi mil chuki hai. Raj ki khushi ka koi thikaana nahi hai. Raj aur Nikita ke gharwale iss rishtey ko nahi maante. Raj aur Nikita ghar se bhaag jaatey hai. College ke badmaash Vikram Gundey lekar waha pahuch jaata hai par Raj un sab ki haddiyon ka MDH masala banaa-deta hai. Raj aur Nikita ke gharwale apne bachcho ki zid ke aage haar maan letey hai. Raj aur Nikita ke chehro pe pyaar ki ronak phir laut aayi hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Aamir Khan( Raj ) , Neelam Kothari( Nikita ) , Deepak Tijori( Deepak ) , Saeed Jaffrey( Anand ) , Kiran Kumar( Raj's papa ) , Beena Banerjee( Raj's maa ) , Rakesh Bedi( Rakesh ) , Viju Khote( Professor ) , Jeet Upendra( Vikram )

Jawaani ke Utsah aur "Beparvaah Attitude" ko College ke frame ke zariye dikhaya gaya hai. FIlm ke Scenes zyada filmy naa lagtey huey niji zindagi se judey huey lagtey hai.
