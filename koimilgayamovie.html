Koi... Mil Gaya-2003


 8.3 / 10

Directed by Rakesh Roshan


Action Science Fiction


Volcanic Box Office


One Man Show

Film ki कहानी


Rohit Apni umr ke bachcho se thoda alag hai aur bhola bhi. Rohit ki maa aur Dost ussey bahut pyaar kartey hai. Rohit ke sheher mai UFO dhikne ki khabrein udd rahi hai. Ek din paas hi kay Jungle mai jaake Rohit ki mulaakat ek Alien se hoti hai. Dono mai dosti hojaati hai aur Rohit uska naam Jaadu rakhta hai. Jaadu ki jaadui shaktiyo ke Rohit Shaktishaali aur Tezz ban gaya hai. Space Research wale Jaadu ko uthaa lejaatey hai. Rohit Jaadu ko chchudaake Uske Planet wapas bhejne mai kaamyab hojaata hai. Rohit Jaadu ko kabhi nahi bhula paaega...

Film ke कलाकार

Hrithik Roshan( Rohit ) , Preity Zinta( Nisha ) , Rajat Bedi( Raj ) , Rekha( Sonia ) , Prem Chopra( Harbhans ) , Mukesh Rishi( Khurshid ) , Johnny Lever( Sukhwani ) , Akash Khurana( Principal )

Honest Overview



Kabhi Kareley si Kadhvahat(BITTERNESS) toh Kabhi Rooh-Afza jaisi Mithaas(SWEETNESS) - Issi Fark mai chhupa mai Zindagi ka asli ZAAYKA(Flavour). FIlm mai Bachpan ki Khushboo hai aur "Apni khaamiyo ke baavjudh bhi Duniya ke saamne Siir Uthaake chalne" ki VALUABLE learning hai. Ye film SINGLEs keliye ek "Ray of Hope"(Ummeed ki Kiran) saabit hoti hai - film dekh-kar aapko ye samajh aaega. VERDICT - " NUTELLA ki tarah FIlm mai bhi koi kamiyan nahi hai". A MUSt-Watch for those jinka Birthday 2010 ke baad aata hai. 1000% RECOMMENDED to Every Enthusiastic Cinema Premi !!!
