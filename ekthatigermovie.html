Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Avinash Rathore urf "Tiger" ki jo ek RAW agent hai aur abhi ek khatarnaak mission se wapas India lauta hai. Tiger ke Senior Shenoy sir ussey naye mission pe Ireland bhej rahe hai Prof. Anwar pe nazar rakhne aur zaroori intel collect karne keliye. Ireland pahuchkar Tiger Prof.Anwar ki housekeeper Zoya se dosti karke Professor ke dosti karne mai kaamyab ho chuka hai. Zoya Tiger ko Maneesh ke naam se jaanti hai aur infact Manish ko chaahne bhi lagi hai aur humaare James Bond ke dil mai bhi ghantiya bhaj rahi hai. Tiger Zoya ko Prof ke ghar se intel churaatey huye pakadta hai. Zoya ek ISI agent hai par Tiger ussey zinda chchodkar intel leaata hai India. Ek naye mission pe Zoya aur Tiger ittefaq se miltey hai aur milkar apne pyaar ki duniya basaane bhaag jaatey hai. Tiger ko jaan se maarne ka order nikalta hai par Tiger Zoya ko lekar bhaagne mai kaamyab hojaata hai. Tiger aur Zoya bahut saavdhaani se jee rahe hai sabki nazro se bachke par dono saath khush hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Tiger ) , Katrina Kaif( Zoya ) , Ranvir Shorey( Gopi ) , Gavie Chahal( Abrar ) , Girish Karnad( Shenoy ) , Roshan Seth( Prof Anwar Kidwai )
