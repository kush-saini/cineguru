Film ki कहानी


Avinash abhi USA se padai karke lauta hai aur uske pitah Balraj ne ussey apni company sambhaalne ki zimmedaari saunpi hai. EK din Golf Course pe Avinash ko Malvika naam ki ek ladki pasand aati hai. Avinash Malvika se nazdeekiya badaaney keliye ussey apni haisiyat ki sachchai chupaata hai. Malvika Avinash ke jhoot bolne se behad naraaz hai, Balraj Avinash ki taraf se Malvika se maafi maangta hai. Avi aur Malvika ki shaadi hojaati hai. Malvika ko ek ladka hota hai. Ek din Avinash ek accident mai maara jaata hai jissey ab Malvika bikul akeli hogayi hai. Malvika ka dost Rajat ussey chaahta hai aur Balraj se Malvika ka haath maangta hai. Balraj Mavika ka asli baap naa hotey huey bhi uski godh-bharai karta hai. Malvika aur Rajat ek nayi khush-haal zindagi ki shuruat kar rahe hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Avinash ) , Amitabh Bachchan( Balraj ) , Hema Malini( Shobhna ) , Rani Mukherji( Malvika ) , JOhn Abraham( Rajat ) , Om Puri( Balwant ) , Rajpal Yadav( House-help ) , Sharat Saxena( Malvika's papa )

Honest Overview



Balraj ka kirdaar iss samaaj keliye ek adarsh hai. Ye film "COOL" banne ki koshish mai bohot tez daudti hai aur zameen pe muh ke bal girti hai, Film ke serious portions aapko zyada pasand aaengey. Suprisingly great Acting by John Abraham. NOT THE BEST but MERELY STILL WATCHABLE !!!
