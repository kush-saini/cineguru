Film ki कहानी


Javed Abbas aur Arun Verma Special Task Force officers hai achche dost bhi hai. Javed akela baap hai jo apne chaar bachcho ko akela sambhaalta hai vahi doosri taraf akela rehta hai. Arun Aur Abbas drug market ke baadshah KKV ko pakadne mai kaamyab hojaate hai par KKV apne dosto ka istemaal karke chchoot jaata hai. Ek anjaan aadmi Abbas ko maardeta hai aur Javed ke chaar bachcho ki zimmedaari ab Arun ke kandho par aa chuki hai. Dheere-dheere Arun bachcho se ghul-mil jaata hai jisme Javed ki dost Geeta uski madad karti hai. Geeta asli mai ek undercover agent aur court mai gawahi deke Arun ko drug dealer saabit krwadeti hai , Arun ki naukri chali jaati hai. Arun apne dum pe Javed ke kaatil ka pata lagane nikalta hai aur pata lagaleta hai ki KKV ne nahi balki Special Task force ke hi ek officer Sawant ne Javed ko maara hai. Arun Sawant ko jaan se maardeta hai aur apne dost ki maut ka badla leleta hai. Arun aur Geeta ab bachcho ko saath mai paal rahe hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Arun ) , Jackie Shroff( Javed ) , Juhi Chawla( Geeta ) , Nirmal Pandey( KKV ) , Raj Zutshi( Sawant )
