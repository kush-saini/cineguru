Based on Re-incarnation

Film ki कहानी


JB apne bete Vicky ki shaadi apne dost Archaelogist DD ki beti Sonia se karwadeta hai. DD ek baba se Sangramgarh ki kahaani sunta hai. Sangramgarh Rani Suryalekha ki aatma se shrapit hai aur Suryalekha ki aatma ko tab tak mukti nahi milegi jab tak uski mulakaat Suryavanshi Vikram Singh se nahi hogi. Vicky ko iss baat ka ehsaas hojaata hai ki voh hi Vikram Singh ka avtaar hai. Vicky Sonia ke saath Suryalekha ki gufa mai pahuchta hai aur Suryavanshi ka roop lekar Suryalekha ko aazad kardeta hai. Vicky aur Sonia gufa se bahar nikaltey hai aur apni zindagi saath mai khushi-khushi bitaatey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Vicky/Rajkumar ) , Amrita Singh( Suryalekha ) , Sheeba( Sonia ) , Saeed Jaffrey( Vicky's father ) , Ajit Vachani , Sushma Seth( Rajmata ) , Shakti Kapoor , Puneet Issar , Kader Khan
