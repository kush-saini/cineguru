 4.5 / 10

Flop at Box Office


A Bit Dissapointing

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai RAW ke Behetreen Agent Vinod ki. Agent Vinod ka saathi Rajan ek khufiya Mission mai maara jaata hai. Rajan marne se pehle Vinod ko 242 ka peecha karne ko bolta hai. Apne tezz dimaag aur daring ki madad se Agent Vinod pata laga leta hai ki 242 Nuclear Bomb ka Detonator hai. Vinod ISI Agent Ruby ki madad se apni jaan pe khelkar Nuclear Bomb ko phatne se rok deta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Vinod ) , Kareena Kapoor( Iram/Ruby ) , Adil Hussain( Colonel ) , Prem Chopra( Kazaan ) , Ram Kapoor( Abu Sayed ) , Gulshan Grover( Tehmur ) , Rajat Kapoor( ISI Chief ) , Shahbaz Khan( Huzefa )

Honest Overview



The film dosen't Live upto Expectations Bina Aloo ke Pani-Puri ki tarah. Packed with Gorgeous Faces & Playlist Worthy Songs par Film zyada Entertain nahi karti hai. Film mai MOST EXCITING cheez hai - THE Yellow CAMARO with Stripes. OVERALL - AN Action Film jisme dher saari bak-bak hai aur VERY LITTLE ACTIOn hai. Iss film ko dekhne se better CID ka koi episode jaake Dekhein. NOT A SINGLE "WOW" moment in this SAIF Ali Khan film !!!
