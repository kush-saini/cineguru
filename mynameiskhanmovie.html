Film ki कहानी


Rizwan Khan ek honhaar ladka hai aur autism ki wajah se apni feelings nahi jata paata . Rizvan apni maa aur bhai Zakir ke saath rehta hai. Kahi saal beet chuke hai aur Maa ke guzarne ke baad Rizvan Zakir ke paas San Francisco chala jaata hai. Rizvan salesman ka kaam karta hai aur ek din kaam ke waqt uski mulaqaat Mandira se hoti hai, Dono mai dosti hojaati . Dosti Mulaqaato mai aur mulakaatein Pyaar mai badal jaati hai. Rizvan Mandira se shaadi karleta hai. Kuch dino baad America pe 9/11 ka attack hojaata hai. Muslimo ki taraf Americans ke dilo mai nafrat paida ho jaati hai. Mandira ka beta Sam isi nafrat ka shikar hoke maara jaata hai. Mandira Sam ki maut ka zimmedaar Rizwan ko thehrati hai aur usey chale jaane ko kehti hai. Rizwan waha se chala jaata hai aur ab uski zindagi ka maqsad hai USA ke president se milna aur batana ki har Muslim terrorist nahi hota. Rizwan sab mushkilo se ladta hua aage bad raha hai aur President ke paas pahuch jaata hai par kisi galatfaimi ke kaaran useey jail mai daal dia jaata hai. Kuch Indian reporters ki madad se Rizwan jail se nikaal dia jaata hai. Aatankwaadi Rizwan ko zakhmi kardete hai, theek hone ke baad Rizwan President se milkar apni baat kehne mai kaamyab hojaata hai. Mandira aur Rizwan fir se saath hai aur Sam ke hatyaaro ko andar kardia jaata hai.
