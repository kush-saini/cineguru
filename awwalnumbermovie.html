Unique Storyline

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai etablished Cricketer Ronnie ki jo apne ghamand mai choor hoke sports ke discipline ko bhool chuka hai. Ronnie ke badey bhai Vicky ek raubdaar Deputy Inspector General hai aur Cricketer Selection Committee ka Head bhi. Vicky Ronnie ki gair-zimmedaari dekh-kar ussey team se nikaalke ek naye ladke Sunny ko khelne ka mauka deta hai. Ronnie Sunny ke pehle match mai Stadium mai bomb lagakar ussey jaan se maarke apni team se nikaale jaane ki beizzati ka badla lena chahta hai. Vicky ko iss baat ki bhanak lag chuki hai aur voh Stadium pahcuchkar deshwasiyo ko bachaane keliye apne bhai ka balidaan khushi khushi dedeta hai. Sunny aakhri ball pe chakka maarke apna pehla shatak banatey huey India ko match jitaa-deta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Dev Anand( Vicky ) , Aditya Pancholi( Ronny ) , Aamir Khan( Sunny ) , Ekta Sohini( Aarti ) , Kulbhushan Kharbanda( Kundi ) , Anjan Shrivastav( Cameo ) , MacMohan( Mani ) , Ajit Vachani( Inspector )

Honest Overview



Film ka entertainment level kisi Cricket Match ki tarah upar neeche hota rehta hai matlab kabhi toh Sixer aur kabhi Clean Bold. DevAnand apne Magnetic style ki dialogue-baazi se aapko screen se chipka ke rakhengey. Film ka gaana " Pucho naa " aap kuch dino tak gungunaatey rahengey. YOU WON'T REGRET WATCHING THIS MAZEDAAR MOVIE !!!
