Don 2 - 2011


 8.1 / 10

Directed by Farhan Akhtar


Crime Action Thriller


Box Office Hit


Shot in Berlin

Film ki कहानी


Don Malasia mai khudko police he hawale kardeta hai aur jail mai usko apna purana dushman Vardhan milta hai aur Vardhan ki madad se Don jail se nikalne mai kaamyab hojaata hai. Don paise chaapne wali plates keliye JK Diwan ko blackmail karta hai par Diwan Don ko Jabbar ke haatho marwane ki koshish karta hai, Don Jabbar ki jaan bakshke ussey apne saath kaam karne deta hai. Don Jabbar Vardhan aur Sameer ke saath milke plates churaane mai kaamyab hojaata hai par Jabbar aur Vardhan Don ko dhoka dekar plates lelete hai. Bhayanak ladai mai Don Jabbar ko maar-deta hai aur Vardhan ko ghayal kardeta hai. Sameer police ko bulake Don ko pakadwa deta hai, Don Sameer ko bahar nikalke maarne ki dhamki deta hai. Europe ke drug market ki information dene ke badle Don chchut jaata hai. Diwan plates ko surakshit jagah par rakhne gaadi se jaa raha hai tabhi neeche bomb phat-jaata hai. Blast mai plates barbaad ho chuki hai. Sameer asli plates lekar Don se milne Bridge par pahuchta hai kyonki jo plates blast mai barbaad hui thi wo Don ne badal di thi aur asli plates Sameer ko mail kardi thi.

Film ke कलाकार

Honest Overview



Film mai dher saara action aur dher saara style hai. Don ka kirdaar ek pal mai dil-fek aashiq hai aur dusre hi pal mai ek beraham khooni hai. " Iconic dialogues, next-level action aur Don ka tez-dimaag ". Action films pasand karne walo ke liye hi ye film bani hai. Suspense and thrill guaranteed !!!
