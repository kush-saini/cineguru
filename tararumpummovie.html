Ta Ra Rum Pum-2007


 6.6 / 10

Directed by Siddharth Anand


Slice-of-Life Drama Sports


Box Office Hit


Adorable Love Story

Film ki कहानी


Rajveer Singh New York mai Taxi chalaata hai. Rajveer Singh ke racing talent ko samajhkar Racing Team ka Manager Harry Rajveer ko Professional Racer banne ka mauka deta hai. Kismat aur lady luck Rajveer ko the famous racer RV banadeti hai. Rajveer ki lady Love Radhika se uski shaadi hojaati hai. RV ki fizool kharche ki aadat se Radhika pareshan hai. RV ek race mai buri tarah injure hokar apni saari daulat shaurat kho baith-taa hai aur ab gareebi mai zindagi bita raha hai. Rv apne betey Champ keliye firse Racer banke Race jeetkar Rusty se apni puraani haar ka badla leleta hai. RV apni khoyi hui Reputation aur Status fir se paa leta hai aur ab dekh-bhaal ke kharch karne laga hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Saif Ali Khan( Rajveer ) , Rani Mukherji( Radhika ) , Shruthi Seth( Sasha ) , Javed Jaffrey( Harry ) , Victor Banerjee( Radhika's father ) , Ali Haji( Champ )

Honest Overview



Ye film Aaj kal ki Tezz-Taraar generation ko thoda thehraav aur kam salary mai Latest Iphone khareedhne walo ko Financial Management ka ek zaroori sabak sikhaati hai. Rani Mukherji aur Saif Ali Khan ji ki On-Screen jodi Convincingly Real jodi lag rahi hai. Youth-Oriented Songs aur ye film Har Love Marriage Couple ki dukhti rag pe haath rakhti hai. "Pariwar keliye insaan ka Pyaar ussey Zimmedaari aur Zindagi se samjhauta karna dono sikha deta hai" ye film aapse kehnaa chaahti hai. TEARS and GIGGLES Guaranteed. 1000% RECOMMENDED to Youngsters looking for a Heart-Warming and Sweet story !!!
