Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Sultan Ali Khan ji jo apne dosto ke saath matargasti karta phirta hai. Sultan ki dosti hojaati hai Aarfa se jo pehlwani mai State Champion hai par Sultan iss dosti ko pyaar samajh baitha hai. Aarfa Sultan ko uski aukaat yaad dilaake apni zindagi se nikaal faikti hai. Sultan bhi ab thaan chuka hai Champion Pehelwan banne ki. Ek mahine ki kadhi mehnat ke baad Sultan Aarfa ke pitah ke akhaade ke pehelwano ko haraa deta hai. Dekhte hi Dekhte Sultan Olympic mai Gold Medal jeet jaata hai. Aarfa ke aankhon mai Sultan keliye izzat aur pyaar dono hai. Shaadi hojaati hai dono ki. Poora Haryana Sultan ko izzat se nawaz raha hai. Ghamand Sultan ke sar pe chad jaata hai. Aarfa pait se hai aur Sultan Turkey gaya hai Championship keliye. Sultan ko apne bachche ki maut ka pata chalta hai jiske liye Aarfa Sultan ko kasoorwaar thehraati hai. Sultan mann se haarke pehelwani chchod chuka hai. Akash Oberoi badi mushkil se Sultan ko Free-Style Wrestling ke competition keliye raazi karwake train karaata hai. Sultan Competiton jeet jaata hai aur Aarfa ussey maaf kar chuki hai aur dono saath hai...

Honest Review



Sultan ke fitoor aur jazbe ko Salaam. Ye film humhe sikhaati hai "Agar Nischaiy atoot ho toh rastaa bhi milega aur manzil bhi". Guroor aur Ghamand mai farak ki baareek rekha ko film mai bakhoobi pesh kiya gaya hai. Ye film Female athletes ko Cinema ka tribute hai.
