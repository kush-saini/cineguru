Film ki कहानी


Yeh kahaani hai Shekhar Prem aur Kishen ki. Kishen ek seedha-saadha newspaper editor hai aur Shekhar uske newspaper keliye photos kheechta hai aur dono bhai jaise hai. Prem Kishen ka awaara ladki-baaz dost hai jo Pooja jaisi pyari biwi ke hotey huey bhi bahar moo maarta phirta hai. Kishen ki biwi Kaajal saas-bahu serial dekhna pasand karti hai aur har Kishen ki har choti choti baat pe shak karti hai jisse Kishen tungh aa chuka hai. Prem apni dost Kishen se apni dost Bobby ka chakkar chalaana chaahta hai. Bobby aur Kishen ko saath dekha jaata hai aur ye baat chupaaney keliye Kishen apne dosto Prem aur Shekhar ke saath jhooth ka jaal boonta hai jisme wo teeno khud hi phas jaatey hai. Pooja Prem ko uski saari harkatey jaan-ne ke baad bhi maaf kardeti hai. Saara confusion ab clear ho chuka hai Kaajal ko ab koi shak nahi hai. Par ye teeno ek nayi ladki ko dekh-kar fir se fisal jaatey hai.

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Prem ) , Anil Kapoor( Kishen ) , Fardeen Khan( Shekhar ) , Bipasha Basu( Bobby ) , Lara Dutta( Kaajal ) , Esha Deol( Pooja ) , Celina Jaitly( Sanjana ) , Boman Irani( Minister ) , Dinesh Hingoo( Doctor ) , Anjan Shrivastav( Judge )

Honest Overview



Yeh film humhe sikhaati hai ki agar Imaarat ki buniyaad mazboot ho toh koi bhi aapda jheli jaa sakti hai bikul ek shaadi ki tarah jisme Bharose aur Pyaar ki buniyaad hoti hai.Iss film ka dher saara masti bhara pagalpan dekh-kar aapko mazaa aa jaega. Iss film ko dosto ke saath baithkar enjoy karein. 1000% recommended !!!
