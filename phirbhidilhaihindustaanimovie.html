Film ki कहानी


Ajay aur Ria virodhi news channels mai reporter hai. Ajay apne channel ki rating badane keliye Don Choti ki madad se mantri Ramakant pe ek nakli hamla karwana ka plan karta hai. Iss nakli hamle mai Ramakant ka saala Mohan ke haatho mara jaata hai, Ramakant logo ki sympathy se vote batortne lagta hai. Mohan ko police arrest karleti hai. Ajay aur Ria ab saath mai kaam kar rahe hai, Mohan jail se bhag jaata hai aur Ria Ajay ko unki gaadi mai chupa hua milta hai ,Mohan batata hai ki usne apni biwi ke rape aur maut ka badla lene keliye Ramakant ke saale ko maara. Ajay aur Ria Mohan ki sachchi gawai ek tape mai record karlete hai aur apne-apne channel ke boss Kaka aur Chinoy ki madad se Mohan ko insaaf dilana chahte hai. Kaka aur Chinoy mantriyo se mile hote hai, Mohan ko maut ki sazaa sunayi jaati hai. Ajay aur Ria tape wapas haasil karlete hai aur TV par broadcast kardete hai. Mohan ko aazaad krdia jaata hai. Ajay Ria ko propose karta hai aur Ria haa kehedeti hai.
