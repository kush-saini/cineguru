Dil Toh Pagal Hai-1997


 7.9 / 10

Directed by Subhash Ghai


Romance Dance Music


BlockBuster


National Film Award for Karisma Kapoor

Film ki कहानी


Rahul aur Nisha ek dance group ke member hai aur achche dost bhi. Rahul ek Maya naam ka naatak direct krna chahta hai aur Nisha ke saath naatak ki rehearsal shuru krdeta hai. Nisha ka pair mai chot lag jaati hai aur doctor ke kehne par vo naatak mai bhaag nahi leti. Rahul ab phirse ek ladki ki talaash mai hai jo achcha naach sake , tabhi ek din woh Pooja ko naachtey hue dekhta hai aur ussey apne naatak mai leleta hai. Rahul aur Pooja achche dost banjaate hai. Pooja apne bachpan ke dost Ajay ke saath Germany chalijaati hai , Ajay London jaate huye Pooja se apne pyaar ka izhaar krta hai aur Pooja haa kardeti hai. Nisha theek hoke wapas laut kar ye paati hai ki naatak mai uski jagah Pooja ne leli hai aur apne pyaar Rahul ko Pooja ke saath dekh-kar Nisha ka dil toot jaata hai . Rahul aur Pooja rehearsal mai kaafi samay saath mai bitaate hai aur isi beech mai ek dusre se pyaar karne lagte hai. Naatak se kuch din pehle Ajay waha pahuchkar apne aur Pooja ke rishtey ka elaan sabke saamne krdeta hai, Rahul ye jaankar dukhi hai. Nisha Rahul ko bataati hai ki apne pyaar ko dur hota dekh usey bhi itn hi dukh hua tha jitna aaj Rahul ko ho raha hai. Ajay Pooja aur Rahul ke dil ki baat jaanta hai aur nataak ke ant mai Pooja keliye Rahul ki recording play kardeta hai, janta ki taaliyo ke saath naatak khatam hota hai. Pooja aur rahul ek dusre ko laga-kar apne pyaar ko sabke saamne apnaatey hai. Ajay aur Nisha apni zindagiyo mai pyaar ko fir se ek mauka dete hai .

Film ke कलाकार

Shahrukh Khan( Rahul ) , Madhuri Dixit( Pooja ) , Karisma Kapoor( Nisha ) , Akshay Kumar( Ajay ) , Farida Jalal( Ajay's mother ) , Deven Verma( Ajay's father ) , Aruna Irani( Anamika )

Honest Overview



Pyaar aur pyaar ki takraar ko iss film mai bakhoobi dikhaya gaya hai. Film ke gaano ko behad khoosurati se peish kiya gaya hai. Shahrukh-Madhuri ki jodi khoob jum rahi hai. Romance se bharpoor ye movie aapko zaroor pasand aaegi. 1000% recommended !!!
