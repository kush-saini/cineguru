Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Sameer ki, bachpan se hi Sameer ke gussey ki wajah se sab pareshan hai. Sameer ki premika ussey chchodke kisi aur se shaadi krleti hai uske gussey se darke. Toota hua dil lekar Sameer Goa aaya hai apne gussey ko shaant karne.Kuch dino mai uski mulaakaat Colonel ki beti Rani se hoti hai aur nazarein miltey hi Sameer ko pyar hojaata hai. Sameer aur Rani dheere-dheere kareeb aa hi rahe hotey ki tabhi Sunny aajata hai aur sabka dil jeet kar Sameer aur Rani ko dur karne ki koshish karta hai. Iss sab se pareshan hokar Sameer Goa chchodne ka faisla karta hai aur waha ussey vida karne Sunny aata hai, Dono ladtey ladtey Stadium pahuchtey hai jaha Sameer puri duniya ke saamne Rani se pyar ka izhaar karta hai aur Shaadi keliye propose karta hai. Rani haa kehdeti hai aur Sunny waha pe aakar Sameer ko batata hai ki wo uske bachpan ka dost Arun hai jo Goa keval Sameer ke gussa ka ilaaj krne aaya tha.Sunny aur Sameer galey lag jaatey hai. Sameer aur Rani ki shaadi hogyi aur gussa bhi chchoo ho gya...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Sameer ) , Akshay Kumar( Sunny ) , Priyanka Chopra( Rani ) , Amrish Puri( Colonel ) , Kader Khan( Duggal Saab ) , Satish Shah( Maamaji ) , Rajpal Yadav( Pandit ) , Amrita Arora( Roma ) , Vindu( Eagle Gang member )

Honest Review



Sunny ka kirdaar aapko bohot hasaega aur zindagi ki zaroori seekh bhi dekar jaega. Akshay Kumar ji comedy jagat ko ek bahut bada tohfa hai aapko yeh film dekh kar samajh aayega. Behetreen Screenplay aur Direction, Director ko 21 topo ki salaami di jaani chaahiye. Yeh film ko ab ek "CULT STATUS " mil chuka hai. Google karke dekho Cult ka matlab agar nahi pata toh. 1000% recommended for Genuine Comedy Lovers!!!
