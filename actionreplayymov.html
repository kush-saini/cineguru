Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Bunty ki jo apne maa-baap ke jhagdo se pareshan aa chuka hai. Bunty ke pitah Kishan aur Maa Mala 35 saal ek saath rehtey huey bhi ek dusre ki zindagi se anjaan hai. Bunty apne maa-baap ki shaadi bachaaney keliye Professor Anthony ki banayi hui Time Machine se 1975 mai chala jaata hai aur apne sharmiley Baap ko Nidarr banadeta hai. Bunty Kishen ke dil mai Mala keliye pyaar jaga deta hai aur Ghamandi Mala ko 70's ki Bholi Heroine mai transform kardeta hai. Mala aur Kishen Apne parivaro ke khilaaf jaakar shaadi karletey hai. Bunty sabko alvida kehkar wapas aajata hai Vartman(Present) mai. Kishen aur Mala ke jhagdey pyar bhari shararato mai badal chukey hai. Bunty apne mission mai kaamyab ho chuka hai...

Honest Overview



COLORS aur CONTRAST fILM KO IMMERSIVE banatey hai. Film ke dialogues BACHKAANEy hai par AKSHAY ka BUGS-BUNNY avtaar is A JOY TO WATCH. Iss film ko apne Langotiya Yaaro ke saath zaroor dekhein.
