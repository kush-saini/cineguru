Dark Action Thriller


Box-Office


Spine-Chilling Story

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Rohan Bhatnagar ki jo ek Andhaa Voice Artist hai aur uski Mulaakat uske hi jaisi Andhi Supriya se hojaati hai. Mulakaat Dosti mai badal gayi aur Dosti Pyaar mai aur dono Shaadi karletey hai. Supriya apne saath huey kukaram se tootkar Aatma-Hatya karleti hai. Supriya ke doshi Amit aur Wasim ko Rohan behad chalaaki se maar deta hai. Amit ke bhai MadhavRao ko Bhagwan ke paas pahuchake Rohan apna badla poora karta hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Hrithik Roshan( Rohan ) , Yami Gautam( Supriya ) , Rohit Roy( Amit ) , Ronit Roy( Madhav Rao ) , Narendra Jha( Inspector Amol ) , Urvashi Rautela( Cameo ) , Girish Kulkarni( Praveen ) , Akhilendra Mishra( Wasim's abba )

Honest Overview



Ye film humhe "Feeling ko Driving Force banake kaise Asambhav Karya" kiya jaata hai ye sikhaati hai. Film aapke Dil aur Mann ko Jhanjhor degi kuch dino keliye. Movie Incredibly Dark hai aur mann ko bhaari kardeti hai. Ye film Planning aur Execution ka mahatva Samjhati hai. Agar aap Entrepreneur banna chaahtey hai toh zaroor dekhein. VERDICT - "UN Believable Screenplay and Story and BEBASI ko bakhoobhi pesh kiya gaya hai". A MUST Watch Before YOu Die !!!
