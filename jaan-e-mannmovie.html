Box Office Flop


A Modern Fairytale

Film ki कहानी


Suhaan aur Piya ka talaak ho raha hai. Piya talaak mai Suhaan se 50 laakh rupay maang rahi hai hai. Suhaan ki haalat than-than gopal hai , VO apne Lawyer Mama ke paas jaata hai salah leney. Suhaan ki dimaag ki batti jalti hai aur aur wo Piya ke liye ek aashiq dhoondhne ka plan banaata hai. Suhaan Agastya ko India bulaata hai, Agastya ko Piya ke divorce ki khabar jaan ke bura lagta hai kyonki wo college mai Piya ka dost tha aur secret lover bhi, Kismat ne ussey dusra mauka diya hai. Suhaan Agastya ko Piya se milwane New York lekar jaata hai aur unka chakkar chalane ki koshish karta hai par chakkar chalaatey-chalaatey wo apni hone wali ex-wife se firse pyaar kar baith-taa hai. Agastya unke saath hone par khush hai aur waha se chala jaata hai. Kahi saal beet chuke hai Agastya space se Suhaan ko call karke apni biwi Preity Zintakova se milwata hai jo ki Piya ki humshakal hai. Sab khushi se apni zindagi bitaatey hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Suhaan ) , Akshay Kumar( Agastya ) , Preity Zinta( Piya/Preity ) , Anupam Kher( Mama ) , Aman Verma( Zubin ) , Nawab Shah( Vishal ) , Soni Razdan( Mrs Goyal ) , Javed Sheikh( Samrat )

Honest Overview



Iss movie mai ek ajeeb sa Puraani disney movies ka old-school charm hai. Agar aapko sapne dekhna pasand hai toh ye film aapko unhi sapno mai wapas lejaaegi. Akshay Kumar ne apni subtle acting se film mai comedy ka tadka lagaya hai. Movie thodi alag hai normal filmo se. Dekhiyega zaroor aapko movie pasand aane ke chances kaafi zyada hai !!!
