King Uncle - 1993

Directed by Rakesh Roshan


Comedy Drama


Box Office Success


Popular family movie

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Ashok Bansal ki jo ek raees businessman hai. Ashok bahut nakchad hai aur gareebi se nafrat krtaa hai kyonki Ashok ki maa ussey aur uske bhai(Anil) aur behen(Sunita) ko ek aadmi ke liye chchod kar chali gayi thi. Ashok business ke alawa kisike liye samay nahi nikaal pata. Sunita Ashok ke manager se pyaar karti hai par Ashok sunita ki shaadi kisi aur se kra deta hai. Anil Kavita naam ki ek garib ldki se pyaar krne lgta hai par Ashok iss rishte ko nahi apnata . Anil jhagda krke ghar chchod ke chala jaata hai aur kavita se shaadi krleta hai. Munna naam ki bachchi anaathalya se bhaagke se Ashok ke yaha pahuch jaati hai. Munna ashok ke ghar mai khoob dhama-chaukdi machaati hai aur Ashok ko phir se ek narm-dil insaan bana deti hai aur Ashok bhi Munna ko apni beti ki tarah chahne lgta hai. Munna ke anaathalya ki warden Shanti aur Pratap ashok ke paise thagne ke liye chalaaki se Munna ko kidnap krlete hai. Ashok aur Anil waha pahuchke gundo ki khoob dhulayi krte hai aur Pratap ko maut ke ghaat utaar dete hai. Munna Ashok ko ke gale laga leti hai. Ashok apne purey pariwar ke saath ek nayi khush-haal zindagi ki shuruat karta hai.

Honest Overview



Kaafi emotional movie hai. Iss film ko purey pariwaar ke saath baithke dekha jaana chaahiye. A must watch movie for everyone!!
