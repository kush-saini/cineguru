Dabangg-2010


 7.3 / 10

Directed by Abhinav Singh Kashyap


Action Drama Comedy


BlockBuster


Sonakshi Sinha's debut film

Film ki कहानी


Bachpan mai hi Chulbul ne apne pitaah ko kho diya tha. Chulbul ki maa ne shaadi karli Prajapati Pandey se. Sautele baap aur kamakal sauteley bhai Makkhi ne kabhi Chulbul ko apna nahi samjha. Par ab CHulbul bada hokar policewala ban gya hai. Chulbul Pandey apni dabangayi keliye mashoor hai. Makkhi Nirmala se pyaar karta hai par Bauji uski shaadi kisi ameer ladki se karana chaahte hai apni factory ko bachaane keliye. EK din CHulbul ghar pahuchke apni maa ko mara hua paata hai. Ab uska koi nahi hai sivaey sautelay baap-bhai ke. CHulbul Rajjo naam ki ladki se shaadi karleta hai Makkhi-Nirmala ki shaadi ke mandap mai unse pehle. Nirmala ke bauji shaadi rok detey hai. CHulbul apne neta dost Dayal Babu ki madad se Gundey Chedi Singh ka bhaari nuksaan kardeta hai. Badla lene keliye Chedi Prajapti Pandey ki factory ko raakh kardeta hai. CHulbul aur Makkhi ko pata chalta hai ki Chedi ne hi unki maa ko maara hai. Dono bhai apni maa ki maut ka badla lene pahuch jaatey aur Chedi ka dum ghot kar usey beraham maut maartey hai. Saara pariwar ab kareeb aa gya hai aur Makkhi-Nirmala ki shaadi bhi ho gayi hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Salman Khan( Chulbul ) , Sonakshi Sinha( Rajjo ) , Arbaaz Khan( Makkhi ) , Vinod Khanna( Bauji ) , Dimple Kapadia( Maa ) , Sonu Sood( Chedi ) , Mahesh Manjrekar( Haria ) , Mahi Gill( Nirmala ) , Tinnu Anand( Nirmala's father ) , Anupam Kher( Dayal Babu ) , Om Puri( Kasturi )

Honest Overview



Salman ne apne swag se Chulbul Pandey naam ke kirdaar mai jaan foonk di hai. Vazandaar dialogues , Dhamaakedaar Action aur Chatpatey gaaney iss film mai koot-koot ke bharey hai. Complete Entertainment ka capsule hai ye film, Zyada socho mat bas nigal jao. PANACHE + ACTING ka deadly combo. 1000% Very Strongly RECOMMENDED !!!
