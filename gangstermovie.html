Film ki कहानी


Gangster Daya se pyaar karke Simran ne bahut takleefe sahi hai aur ab Daya se badhti duriyo ke kaaran ussey nashey ki lat lag chuki hai. Simran ki mulakaat ek club Singer Akash se hoti hai. Akash aur Simran ek dusrey ko chaahney lagey hai. Daya Simran ko Rangey haatho pakadhne ke baavjudh bhi Maaf kardeta hai. SImran firse Daya ke saath ussi puraani Police se bhaagne wali zindagi mai laut chuki hai. Daya ko police pakad leti hai aur faansi ki sazah deti hai. Simran ko pata chalta hai ki ye sab Akash ki chaal thi Daya ko pakadhne keliye. Simran apna badla leke Firse Daya ke paas pahuch gayi hai issbaar ek nayi duniya mai...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Akash ) , Kangana Ranaut( Simran ) , Shiney Ahuja( Daya ) , Gulshan Grover( Khan )
