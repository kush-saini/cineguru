Crime Education

Interesting Take on Education System

Film ki कहानी


Ye kahaani hai Rakesh ki jo Jugaad se Kamzor bachcho ko Achche Colleges mai daakhila dilwa-kar Karodo rupay kamaa raha hai. Rakesh apne iss kaam ko samaaj-seva samjahta hai. Rakesh ki dost Nupur ussey pakadwaa deti hai kyonki Rakesh keliye Kaam karne wala Nupur ka bhai Sattu depression mai apni jaan leleta hai. Rakesh court mai mukadhma haarke Kuch Mahino baad bahar nikal chuka hai aur ab naye Karnaamo ki tayaari mai hai...

Film ke कलाकार

Emraan Haashmi( Rakesh ) , Rajesh Jais( Vakeel ) , Shreya Dhanwantry( Nupur ) , Shibani Bedi( Rakesh's Biwi )

Honest Overview



Ye film aapki Umeedon(HOPES) ki Imaarat(Building) ko todne wali JCB Machine ke jaisi hai jisko dekhne keliye koi Bheed(Audience) ka jamaa ho paana Thoda DIFFICULT lagta hai. Ye film "MAA-BAAP ke armaano ke Bojh apne Naazuk Kandho pe Uthaaye huye" har uss Bachche ki kahaani hai jo ab sirf ek STUDENT banke reh haya hai. A One TIme Watch for 10-12th Std ke Bachchey Log. VERDICT - " It's Like Cheeni Wali Roti - You May or May not Like it" !!!
